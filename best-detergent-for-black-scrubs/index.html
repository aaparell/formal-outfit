The Best Detergent For Black Scrubs Keep Them Looking New

Written by Robby May 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you wear black scrubs for work or just love to wear them out and about? Keeping them looking new can be a challenge, especially if you don't use the right detergent. Black scrubs can easily fade, get stained, or lose their shape if not properly maintained. This can be frustrating and costly, but there's a solution: finding the best detergent for black scrubs.

The target of this article is to help you find the best detergent for black scrubs to keep them looking new and prevent fading, staining, and loss of shape.

In summary, this article will cover:

  • The importance of using the right detergent for black scrubs
  • The best detergent for black scrubs
  • How to best care for black scrubs to prevent fading, staining, and loss of shape
  • Tips and tricks to keep black scrubs looking new

What is the Best Detergent for Black Scrubs?

After trying several detergents, we have found that the best detergent for black scrubs is Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent. This detergent is specially formulated to clean and protect dark colors like black, keeping them looking new for longer. It's also effective at removing stains and odors without damaging the fabric, which is essential for scrubs that are worn in medical or food service settings.

Personally, I have been using Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent for my black scrubs for over a year, and I have noticed a significant improvement in how they look and feel. The color has stayed rich and deep, and there are no stains or odors even after many washes.

How to Use Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent for Black Scrubs

When using Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent for black scrubs, follow these steps:

  • Sort your black scrubs from other clothes to prevent color bleeding
  • Use the recommended amount of detergent according to the size of your load
  • Wash your black scrubs in cold water to prevent fading
  • Dry your black scrubs on low heat or air dry to prevent shrinking

Why is Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent the Best for Black Scrubs?

Tide Plus Ultra Stain Release HE Turbo Clean Laundry Detergent is the best for black scrubs because it's specially formulated to clean and protect dark colors without damaging the fabric. It contains a unique formula that removes stains and odors while preserving the color and texture of the fabric. It's also HE (High Efficiency) detergent, which means it's more concentrated and uses less water, making it more efficient and cost-effective in the long run.

How to Care for Black Scrubs to Keep Them Looking New

Aside from using the right detergent, there are other things you can do to care for your black scrubs and keep them looking new:

  • Wash them inside out to prevent fading
  • Avoid using fabric softeners, which can leave residue and damage the fabric
  • Remove stains as soon as possible to prevent setting
  • Hang them to dry instead of using a dryer to prevent shrinking and damage
