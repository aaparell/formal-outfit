Most Valuable Wwe Magazines A Guide To Building Your Collection

Written by Daniel Aug 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a die-hard fan of the WWE, looking to expand your collection with some of the most valuable WWE magazines out there? Look no further! In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the most valuable WWE magazines, from what they are to where to find them.

What Are Most Valuable WWE Magazines?

Most valuable WWE magazines are issues of wrestling publications that have become highly sought after by collectors due to their rarity, historical significance, or special features. These magazines may feature iconic matches, interviews with legendary wrestlers, or even limited edition covers or posters.

Whether you're a collector looking to add to your collection, a fan looking for a piece of wrestling history, or simply curious about what makes certain magazines so valuable, read on for our guide to the most valuable WWE magazines out there.

The Official WWE Magazine

The Official WWE Magazine is one of the most popular wrestling publications in the world, and for good reason. With exclusive interviews, behind-the-scenes access, and coverage of all the latest WWE events, it's a must-read for any fan. But did you know that some issues of The Official WWE Magazine are also highly valuable?

For example, the January 1999 issue featuring Stone Cold Steve Austin on the cover can fetch over $100 on auction sites like eBay. Similarly, the June 2002 issue with The Rock on the cover can sell for up to $75.

WWF Magazine

Before The Official WWE Magazine, there was WWF Magazine. Published by the World Wrestling Federation (now WWE) from 1984 to 2001, this iconic publication featured interviews, match previews, and exclusive photos of all your favorite wrestlers.

Some of the most valuable issues of WWF Magazine include the January 1988 issue with Andre the Giant on the cover (worth around $100), the May 1990 issue featuring The Ultimate Warrior ($75-$100), and the December 1991 issue with Hulk Hogan on the cover (up to $150).

The Bottom Line

Whether you're a collector, a fan, or simply curious about the world of professional wrestling, most valuable WWE magazines offer a unique glimpse into the history and culture of this iconic sport. From rare interviews to limited edition covers, these magazines are a must-have for any true fan.

Where to Find Most Valuable WWE Magazines

If you're looking to add some of the most valuable WWE magazines to your collection, there are a few places you can start your search:

  • Auction sites like eBay or Heritage Auctions
  • Specialty wrestling memorabilia stores
  • Online forums and collector groups

Remember to do your research before making a purchase, and be sure to check the condition and authenticity of any magazine you're considering buying.

Why Are Most Valuable WWE Magazines So Valuable?

There are a few reasons why some issues of wrestling magazines are so highly valued by collectors:

  • Rarity: Some magazines were printed in limited quantities, making them hard to find today.
  • Historical significance: Magazines that feature iconic matches or interviews with legendary wrestlers are highly sought after by fans.
  • Special features: Limited edition covers, posters, or other special features can make a magazine more valuable.

Ultimately, the value of a wrestling magazine comes down to supply and demand. If a particular issue is highly sought after by collectors but is difficult to find, its value will likely be quite high.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Are most valuable WWE magazines only from the 80s and 90s?

A: While many of the most valuable WWE magazines are from the 80s and 90s, there are also some more recent issues that are highly sought after by collectors. For example, the August 2014 issue of The Official WWE Magazine featuring Bray Wyatt on the cover can sell for up to $50.

Q: Are all issues of The Official WWE Magazine valuable?

A: No, not all issues of The Official WWE Magazine are valuable. The value of a particular issue depends on a number of factors, including its rarity, historical significance, and special features.

Q: Can I make money by selling most valuable WWE magazines?

A: While some issues of wrestling magazines can be quite valuable, it's important to remember that the market for these items can be volatile. If you're looking to make money by selling most valuable WWE magazines, it's important to do your research and be patient.

Q: Can I still enjoy most valuable WWE magazines even if I'm not a collector?

A: Absolutely! While most valuable WWE magazines are highly sought after by collectors, they can also be enjoyed by fans who simply want to learn more about the history and culture of professional wrestling.

Conclusion of Most Valuable WWE Magazines

From The Official WWE Magazine to classic issues of WWF Magazine, most valuable WWE magazines offer a unique window into the world of professional wrestling. Whether you're a collector, a fan, or simply curious about the history of this iconic sport, these magazines are a must-have for anyone looking to expand their collection or learn more about the world of wrestling.
