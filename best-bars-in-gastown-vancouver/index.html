Discover The Best Bars In Gastown Vancouver For A Night Out

Written by Oliver Jun 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in search of the perfect place to grab a drink or two with your friends or colleagues? Gastown Vancouver has some of the coolest bars in town. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to decide which ones to visit. This post will guide you through the best bars in Gastown Vancouver that are worth checking out.

When it comes to finding the best bars in Gastown Vancouver, it's essential to consider your preferences. Do you prefer a bar with a great atmosphere or one with a wide selection of drinks? Do you want a bar that serves food or one with a live music performance? Whatever your preferences, Gastown Vancouver has something for everyone.

In this post, we will explore the best bars in Gastown Vancouver, including their unique features and why they stand out from the rest. We will also provide tips on how to make the most out of your night out in Gastown Vancouver.

Alibi Room

Alibi Room is one of the best bars in Gastown Vancouver, known for its extensive selection of craft beers and delicious food. The bar has a cozy atmosphere, perfect for a night out with friends or a date. The menu changes frequently, so you can expect to find new and exciting drinks and dishes every time you visit. The staff is friendly and knowledgeable, making it a great place to try new beers or get recommendations.

The Diamond

The Diamond is a sophisticated cocktail bar with a speakeasy vibe. The bar has an intimate setting, with dim lighting and plush seating, perfect for a romantic night out or a small group gathering. The bartenders are skilled in creating unique and creative cocktails, so you can expect to find something new and exciting every time you visit.

The Irish Heather

The Irish Heather is a traditional Irish pub with a modern twist. The bar has a warm and inviting atmosphere, with wooden furnishings and a cozy fireplace. The pub serves a wide selection of beers and whiskeys, perfect for those who enjoy a good drink. The pub also has a restaurant that serves delicious Irish dishes, making it a great place to grab a bite to eat before or after your night out.

Clough Club

Clough Club is a trendy cocktail bar with a vintage vibe. The bar has a wide selection of cocktails, including their signature drinks, which are served in unique glassware. The bar also has a small food menu, perfect for a light snack to accompany your drinks. The bar has a DJ on weekends, making it a great place to dance the night away.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the dress code for the bars in Gastown Vancouver?

A: The dress code for the bars in Gastown Vancouver is usually casual. However, it's always best to check the specific bar's website or call ahead to confirm any dress code requirements.

Q: Are the bars in Gastown Vancouver expensive?

A: The prices of drinks in Gastown Vancouver bars vary depending on the location and the type of drink you order. However, most bars have reasonable prices, and you can always find happy hour deals to save some money.

Q: Can I reserve a table at the bars in Gastown Vancouver?

A: Most bars in Gastown Vancouver do not take reservations, especially on weekends. However, some bars may allow you to reserve a table for larger groups, so it's best to call ahead and check.

Q: What time do the bars in Gastown Vancouver close?

A: The closing time of bars in Gastown Vancouver varies depending on the location and the day of the week. However, most bars close around 2 am on weekends and 1 am on weekdays.

Conclusion of Best Bars in Gastown Vancouver

Gastown Vancouver is a hub of unique bars, each with its own charm and vibe. Whether you're looking for a cozy pub or a trendy cocktail bar, you're sure to find something that suits your taste. The bars we've highlighted in this post are just a few of the best bars in Gastown Vancouver worth visiting. So, grab your friends or colleagues and head out for a night of fun and drinks!
