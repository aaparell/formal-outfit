Discovering The Traditional Dress Of Maharashtra

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you curious about the cultural attire of Maharashtra? Do you want to know more about the traditional dress of Maharashtra? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will dive deep into the topic of what is traditional dress of Maharashtra and its significance in the state's culture.

The traditional dress of Maharashtra is an essential part of the state's rich culture and heritage. The attire is not only beautiful but also reflects the state's history, customs, and traditions. However, with the changing times, people tend to forget their roots and opt for modern attire, which has led to the fading of traditional dress.

The traditional dress of Maharashtra for men is the Dhoti and Pheta, while women wear sarees like Nauvari and Paithani. These attires are made from cotton or silk, and the colors used in the dresses are bright and vibrant. The fabrics and designs used in the attire are unique to Maharashtra and have been passed down from generations.

In summary, the traditional dress of Maharashtra has a deep-rooted significance in the state's culture and heritage. It reflects the history, customs, and traditions of Maharashtra, and it is an essential aspect of the state's identity.

What is the significance of Nauvari saree and Pheta?

As a Maharashtrian, I have a personal experience of wearing Nauvari saree and Pheta. The Nauvari saree is a nine-yard saree worn by women, which is draped differently than a regular saree. The saree is worn by women during festivals, weddings, and other important occasions. The Pheta is a traditional headgear worn by men during weddings, festivals, and other traditional events. It is made from cotton or silk and is available in various colors.

How is the traditional dress of Maharashtra preserved?

The traditional dress of Maharashtra is being preserved through various initiatives by the state government and non-profit organizations. These initiatives include organizing traditional fashion shows, promoting the use of traditional attire in schools and colleges, and organizing workshops to teach people how to make traditional attire. These initiatives have helped in preserving the traditional dress of Maharashtra and passing it down to the next generations.

What is the significance of Paithani saree?

Paithani saree is a type of silk saree, which is handwoven in the Paithan town of Maharashtra. The saree is known for its unique design and patterns, which are created by weaving different colored threads together. It takes around six months to one year to weave a Paithani saree. The saree is worn by women during weddings, festivals, and other important occasions.

How to wear a Nauvari saree?

Wearing a Nauvari saree is a bit different from wearing a regular saree. The saree is draped in a way that it covers both legs and resembles a dhoti. First, the saree is tucked at the waist and then draped over the left shoulder. The remaining length of the saree is then draped around the legs and tucked in at the back. The pallu is then draped over the right shoulder.

The beauty of traditional dress

As a Maharashtrian, I feel proud of my culture and heritage. Wearing traditional attire not only makes us feel connected to our roots but also makes us stand out in a crowd. Whenever I wear a Nauvari saree or Pheta, I feel a sense of pride and belongingness to my state's culture.
