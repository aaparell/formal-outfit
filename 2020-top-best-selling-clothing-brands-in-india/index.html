2020 Top Best Selling Clothing Brands in India: A Complete Guide

When it comes to fashion and clothing, India has always been a trendsetter. With a diverse culture and a rich heritage, Indian fashion has always been a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. As we step into the new decade, let's take a look at the top best selling clothing brands in India in 2020.

With an increasing demand for affordable yet stylish clothing, the Indian fashion industry has witnessed a significant surge in recent years. From traditional wear to western wear, there is a brand for every taste and budget. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right brand that suits your style and preferences.

In this article, we aim to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the top best selling clothing brands in India in 2020. We will cover everything from ethnic wear to western wear, casual wear to formal wear, and everything in between.

Top Ethnic Wear Brands in India

When it comes to ethnic wear, India has a rich heritage of textiles, fabrics, and designs. Here are some of the top ethnic wear brands in India:

1. Fabindia: Fabindia is a brand that celebrates the rich heritage of Indian textiles and handicrafts. Their collection includes a wide range of ethnic wear for men, women, and kids, along with home decor and accessories.

2. Biba: Biba is a popular ethnic wear brand that offers a range of kurtas, kurtis, salwar suits, and lehengas for women. They are known for their vibrant colors, intricate designs, and affordable prices.

3. W for Woman: W for Woman is a brand that caters to the modern Indian woman who wants to look stylish yet traditional. Their collection includes a range of kurtas, kurtis, salwar suits, and fusion wear.

Top Western Wear Brands in India

With the rise of globalization and western influence, western wear has become a popular choice among Indian millennials. Here are some of the top western wear brands in India:

1. H&M: H&M is a Swedish multinational brand that offers a range of affordable yet stylish clothing for men, women, and kids. They are known for their trendy designs and sustainable fashion practices.

2. Zara: Zara is a Spanish fashion brand that has become a household name in India. Their collection includes a range of clothing, footwear, and accessories for men, women, and kids.

3. Forever 21: Forever 21 is an American fast-fashion brand that offers a range of chic and trendy clothing for women. They are known for their affordable prices and wide range of sizes.

Conclusion

With so many options available, it can be challenging to choose the right clothing brand that suits your style and budget. However, by keeping in mind your preferences and needs, you can easily find the perfect brand that complements your personality. Whether you prefer ethnic wear or western wear, there is a brand for everyone in India.

How to Choose the Right Clothing Brand in India?

Here are some tips to help you choose the right clothing brand in India:

1. Identify your style: Before choosing a clothing brand, it is essential to identify your style and preferences. Do you prefer ethnic wear or western wear? Do you like bold colors or subtle designs? These questions will help you narrow down your options and choose the right brand.

2. Research: Once you have identified your style, research different clothing brands in India that cater to your preferences. Look for reviews, ratings, and customer feedback to get an idea of the brand's quality and service.

3. Budget: Clothing brands in India offer a wide range of prices, from affordable to luxury. Determine your budget and choose a brand that fits within your price range.
