What Men s Shoes Are In Style 2021

Written by Bardi Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to update your shoe collection in 2021 but not sure what's in style? Look no further as we have compiled a list of the latest trends in men's shoes that are sure to elevate your fashion game.

Having the right shoes can make or break an outfit, and it's essential to have a few versatile pairs that can be worn for any occasion. Whether you're heading to work, a night out with friends, or a casual weekend, we've got you covered with the latest trends in men's shoes.

What Men's Shoes Are in Style 2021?

The top trend in men's shoes for 2021 is the chunky sole. We're seeing this style on all types of shoes, from sneakers to dress shoes. The chunky sole adds a modern edge to any outfit and is perfect for those who want to make a statement.

Another popular trend is the return of the classic penny loafer. This timeless shoe can be dressed up or down and is perfect for any occasion. We're also seeing more men's boots with a rugged, outdoorsy look that are perfect for the winter months.

For sneakers, the trend is all about bold colors and unique designs. Think bright neon, animal prints, and retro styles. High-top sneakers are also making a comeback, and they're perfect for adding a touch of street style to any outfit.

To sum it up, chunky soles, penny loafers, rugged boots, and bold sneakers are all in style for men's shoes in 2021.

My Personal Experience with Men's Shoes in 2021

As someone who loves fashion, I've been keeping up with the latest trends in men's shoes. I recently purchased a pair of chunky sole sneakers in a bright neon green color, and they have quickly become my go-to shoe for any casual occasion.

I've also been wearing my classic penny loafers more often, as they're perfect for dressing up any outfit. And for those colder days, I've been wearing my rugged boots with a wool lining, which keeps my feet warm and stylish.

How to Style Men's Shoes in 2021

The key to styling men's shoes in 2021 is all about balance. If you're wearing a bold shoe, keep the rest of your outfit simple and understated. For example, if you're wearing bright neon sneakers, pair them with a neutral outfit like black jeans and a white t-shirt.

If you're wearing a classic shoe like the penny loafer, you can dress them up with a suit or dress them down with jeans and a sweater. The key is to find a shoe that works with your personal style and can be worn for any occasion.

Do's and Don'ts of Men's Shoes in 2021

Do invest in versatile shoes that can be worn for any occasion.

Don't be afraid to try new shoe trends, but make sure they work with your personal style.

Do keep your shoes clean and in good condition to make them last longer.

Don't wear shoes that are uncomfortable or don't fit properly. Your shoes should be both stylish and comfortable.

Where to Buy Men's Shoes in 2021

The best places to buy men's shoes in 2021 are online retailers like Amazon, Zappos, and Nordstrom. You can also find trendy shoes at your local department stores like Macy's and Bloomingdale's.

Conclusion of What Men's Shoes Are in Style 2021

In conclusion, men's shoes in 2021 are all about chunky soles, classic penny loafers, rugged boots, and bold sneakers. Remember to find a shoe that works with your personal style and can be worn for any occasion. And don't be afraid to try new shoe trends, as they can add a modern edge to any outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What are the top shoe colors for men in 2021?

A: Bold colors like neon, animal prints, and retro styles are all on-trend for men's shoes in 2021.

Q: Can I wear chunky sole shoes to work?

A: Yes, you can wear chunky sole shoes to work, but make sure to pair them with a more formal outfit like a suit or dress pants.

Q: Are high-top sneakers still in style?

A: Yes, high-top sneakers are making a comeback in 2021 and are perfect for adding a touch of street style to any outfit.

Q: What's the best way to keep my shoes clean?

A: The best way to keep your shoes clean is to wipe them down with a damp cloth and store them in a cool, dry place. You can also use a shoe cleaner or protector to help keep them in good condition.
