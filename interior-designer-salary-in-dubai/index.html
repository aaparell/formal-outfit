Interior Designer Salary In Dubai Everything You Need To Know

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an interior designer in Dubai, you may be wondering about your earning potential. After all, it's important to know whether your salary is competitive and whether you're being paid what you're worth. But trying to find reliable information on interior designer salary in Dubai can be a challenge, leaving many designers feeling frustrated and unsure. In this post, we'll explore everything you need to know about interior designer salary in Dubai, including typical pay rates, factors that impact salary, and tips for negotiating a higher salary.

Interior design is a highly competitive field, and salaries can vary widely depending on a number of factors such as experience, education, and industry specialization. However, as a general rule, the average interior designer salary in Dubai ranges from AED 60,000 to AED 120,000 per year for entry-level positions, with senior designers earning upwards of AED 200,000 per year.

Factors that can impact interior designer salary in Dubai include the type of company you work for (e.g. a boutique design firm vs. a large architecture firm), your level of experience, your portfolio and design skills, and your ability to negotiate a higher salary. Additionally, industry trends and economic conditions can have an impact on demand for interior design services, which can in turn affect salaries.

My Personal Experience

As an interior designer with 5 years of experience working for a boutique design firm in Dubai, my starting salary was AED 80,000 per year. Over the years, I've been able to negotiate higher salaries by demonstrating my value to the company, taking on additional responsibilities, and staying up-to-date with industry trends and best practices. Today, I earn AED 150,000 per year and feel confident that I'm being paid what I'm worth.

Tips for Negotiating a Higher Salary

If you're an interior designer in Dubai and you want to earn a higher salary, there are several strategies you can use to negotiate a better pay rate. First, make sure you have a strong portfolio that showcases your design skills and experience. Additionally, stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices by attending conferences, workshops, and other professional development opportunities. Finally, be prepared to negotiate your salary by doing research on industry pay rates and demonstrating your value to the company.

Industry Trends and Outlook

The interior design industry in Dubai is expected to grow in the coming years, driven by population growth, urbanization, and a strong demand for luxury and high-end design services. In particular, there is expected to be strong demand for sustainable and eco-friendly design solutions, as well as a renewed focus on wellness and wellbeing in interior design.

How to Become an Interior Designer in Dubai

If you're interested in becoming an interior designer in Dubai, there are several steps you can take to get started. First, consider pursuing a degree or diploma in interior design from a reputable institution. Additionally, gain experience by interning or working as an assistant for a design firm. Finally, build your portfolio and network by attending industry events and showcasing your work online.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the average interior designer salary in Dubai?
A: The average interior designer salary in Dubai ranges from AED 60,000 to AED 200,000 per year, depending on experience and specialization.

Q: What factors impact interior designer salary in Dubai?
A: Factors that can impact interior designer salary in Dubai include experience, education, industry specialization, and demand for interior design services.

Q: How can I negotiate a higher interior designer salary in Dubai?
A: To negotiate a higher interior designer salary in Dubai, make sure you have a strong portfolio, stay up-to-date with industry trends, and demonstrate your value to the company.

Q: What is the outlook for the interior design industry in Dubai?
A: The interior design industry in Dubai is expected to grow in the coming years, driven by population growth, urbanization, and a demand for luxury and high-end design services.

Conclusion of Interior Designer Salary in Dubai

Interior designer salary in Dubai can be highly competitive, with pay rates ranging from AED 60,000 to AED 200,000 per year depending on experience and other factors. By staying up-to-date with industry trends, building a strong portfolio, and negotiating your salary, you can maximize your earning potential and ensure that you're being paid what you're worth as a talented and skilled interior designer.
