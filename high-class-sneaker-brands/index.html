High Class Sneaker Brands For The Fashion Forward

Written by William Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old sneakers every day? Do you want to step up your fashion game without sacrificing comfort? Look no further than high class sneaker brands.

Sneakers have become a staple in fashion, but not all sneakers are created equal. While some may prioritize style over comfort, high class sneaker brands offer the perfect balance of both.

If you're looking for sneakers that are a cut above the rest, high class sneaker brands are the way to go. These brands offer premium materials, unique designs, and exceptional comfort. They cater to the fashion-forward who want to stand out from the crowd.

In this article, we'll explore some of the top high class sneaker brands and what makes them so special. We'll also delve into the history of sneakers and how they've evolved to become a fashion statement.

1. Common Projects

When it comes to high class sneaker brands, Common Projects is a name that stands out. Founded in 2004, this New York-based company offers minimalistic designs made from premium Italian leather.

I personally own a pair of white Common Projects Achilles Low and they have quickly become my go-to sneakers. The leather is incredibly soft and the design is sleek and versatile. They're perfect for dressing up or down.

2. Golden Goose

Golden Goose is an Italian brand known for its distressed and vintage-inspired designs. Each pair is handcrafted in Venice, making them unique and one-of-a-kind.

I purchased a pair of Golden Goose Superstars and was blown away by the level of detail and craftsmanship. The leather is buttery soft and the distressing is done in a way that adds character without looking too worn out.

The Evolution of Sneakers

Sneakers have come a long way since their humble beginnings in the late 1800s. Originally designed for athletic purposes, they were made from simple materials like canvas and rubber.

It wasn't until the 1970s that sneakers began to make their mark in fashion. This was largely due to the rise of hip-hop culture, which embraced sneakers as a symbol of rebellion and individuality.

In the years that followed, sneakers continued to evolve and become more fashion-forward. Today, high class sneaker brands offer designs that are both stylish and functional, making them a must-have for any fashion enthusiast.

How to Style High Class Sneakers

When it comes to styling high class sneakers, the possibilities are endless. They can be dressed up with a suit or dressed down with jeans and a t-shirt.

One of my favorite ways to style them is with a pair of cropped trousers and a button-up shirt. This creates a modern and sophisticated look that's perfect for the office or a night out.

3. Balenciaga

Balenciaga is a luxury fashion house known for its bold and avant-garde designs. Their sneakers are no exception, featuring chunky soles and oversized logos.

I recently purchased a pair of Balenciaga Triple S sneakers and they're definitely a statement piece. They're not for the faint of heart, but if you're looking to make a statement, these are the sneakers for you.

4. Yeezy

Yeezy is a collaboration between Adidas and Kanye West. Their sneakers are known for their unique designs and limited releases.

I was lucky enough to snag a pair of Yeezy Boost 350 V2s and they're easily one of the most comfortable sneakers I own. The Primeknit upper conforms to your foot and the Boost sole provides unparalleled comfort.

Question and Answer

Q: What sets high class sneaker brands apart from other sneakers?

A: High class sneaker brands offer premium materials, unique designs, and exceptional comfort. They prioritize both style and function.

Q: How do I style high class sneakers?

A: High class sneakers can be dressed up or down. They look great with a suit or with jeans and a t-shirt.

Q: Are high class sneakers worth the investment?

A: Yes, high class sneakers are worth the investment. They offer unparalleled comfort and style that can elevate any outfit.

Q: Where can I purchase high class sneakers?

A: High class sneakers can be purchased from the brand's website or from luxury retailers like Nordstrom or Saks Fifth Avenue.

Conclusion of High Class Sneaker Brands

If you're looking to up your sneaker game, high class sneaker brands are the way to go. They offer premium materials, unique designs, and exceptional comfort. Whether you're a sneaker enthusiast or a fashion-forward individual, high class sneakers are a must-have in your wardrobe.
