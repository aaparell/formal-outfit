What Is An Oxford Dress Shoe

Written by Eveline Oct 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing sneakers or flip-flops all the time? Do you want to look sharp and elegant for a special occasion or a fancy night out? If so, you might want to consider wearing an Oxford dress shoe, which is a classic and timeless footwear option that suits many styles and occasions.

However, if you're not familiar with the term "Oxford dress shoe," you might feel confused or overwhelmed by the variety of shoes available. You might wonder what makes them different from other types of shoes, or what to look for when shopping for them. Don't worry; we've got you covered.

What is an Oxford Dress Shoe?

An Oxford dress shoe is a type of lace-up shoe that features "closed lacing," which means that the eyelets are stitched under the vamp (the front part of the shoe) instead of on top of it. This gives the shoe a sleek and minimalist look, as there are no flaps or overlays on the top of the shoe. Oxfords are typically made of leather, although some models may have suede or fabric uppers.

Oxford dress shoes come in various colors, such as black, brown, burgundy, or tan, and finishes, such as glossy, matte, or textured. They may also have different toe shapes, such as round, square, or pointed, and sole constructions, such as leather, rubber, or combination. However, regardless of these details, all Oxfords share the same basic structure and style.

Why Wear an Oxford Dress Shoe?

There are several reasons why you might want to wear an Oxford dress shoe:

  • They are versatile and can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion and the outfit. You can wear them with a suit, a blazer, a dress shirt, or even jeans or chinos.
  • They are comfortable and supportive, as they provide a snug fit and a stable base for your feet. They also have a low heel, which reduces the strain on your back and legs.
  • They are stylish and sophisticated, as they convey a sense of class, elegance, and attention to detail. They can enhance your overall image and make you stand out in a positive way.

How to Choose an Oxford Dress Shoe?

When choosing an Oxford dress shoe, there are several factors to consider:

  • Material: Choose a leather or suede upper that is soft, durable, and breathable. Avoid synthetic or cheap materials that may crack or peel over time.
  • Color: Choose a color that matches or complements your outfit and your personal style. Black is the most formal and versatile option, while brown, burgundy, or tan can add warmth and depth to your look.
  • Fit: Choose a size that fits snugly but not too tight, with enough room for your toes to wiggle and your feet to breathe. Make sure the shoe doesn't slip, pinch, or rub against your skin.
  • Quality: Choose a shoe that is well-made, with sturdy stitching, smooth edges, and a solid sole. Check the details, such as the eyelets, the laces, the lining, and the insole, to ensure they are of good quality and comfort.

Where to Buy an Oxford Dress Shoe?

You can buy an Oxford dress shoe from various sources, such as:

  • Retail stores: Visit a local shoe store or a department store that sells men's shoes. Try on different models and brands to find the one that suits you best. Ask the salesperson for advice or recommendations if needed.
  • Online stores: Browse an online shoe store or a marketplace that offers a wide range of Oxfords. Read the reviews and ratings from other customers to get an idea of the sizing, quality, and style. Check the return policy and the shipping options before placing your order.
  • Boutiques: Visit a boutique that specializes in men's shoes and accessories. They may offer customized or bespoke options that cater to your unique preferences and needs.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between an Oxford and a Derby shoe?

A: The main difference between an Oxford and a Derby shoe is the lacing system. Oxfords have closed lacing, while Derbies have open lacing, which means that the eyelets are stitched on top of the vamp. This gives Derbies a more casual and relaxed look, as they can accommodate wider or higher feet.

Q: Can I wear an Oxford dress shoe with a suit or a tuxedo?

A: Yes, in fact, Oxfords are the most appropriate and elegant shoe choice for formal occasions, such as weddings, galas, or business meetings. However, make sure to choose a black or dark brown pair that matches the color and style of your suit or tuxedo.

Q: How do I clean and maintain my Oxford dress shoe?

A: To clean your Oxford dress shoe, wipe it gently with a damp cloth or a soft brush to remove dirt and dust. Avoid using harsh chemicals or water, as they may damage the leather or the finish. To maintain the shine and the suppleness of the leather, apply a leather conditioner or a wax polish regularly, following the instructions on the product label.

Q: Can I wear an Oxford dress shoe with casual outfits?

A: Yes, Oxfords can add a touch of sophistication and refinement to your casual or smart-casual outfits, such as jeans, chinos, or blazers. However, make sure to choose a less formal or textured pair that matches the style and color of your outfit.

Conclusion of What is an Oxford Dress Shoe

In summary, an Oxford dress shoe is a versatile, comfortable, and stylish shoe that can elevate your look and your confidence. Whether you need a shoe for a formal event, a business meeting, or a casual outing, an Oxford can fit the bill. Just remember to choose a high-quality, well-fitting, and appropriate pair that suits your taste and your needs.
