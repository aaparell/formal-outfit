How to Choose Between Classic Fit and Custom Fit Ralph Lauren Shirts

Buying a Ralph Lauren shirt can be a daunting task, especially when you have to choose between classic and custom fit. The wrong fit can leave you feeling uncomfortable and self-conscious. This article will help you understand the difference between classic fit and custom fit Ralph Lauren shirts, so you can make an informed choice.

If you've ever bought a shirt that was too tight or too loose, you know how uncomfortable it can be. A poorly fitted shirt can make you feel self-conscious and even affect your posture. This is why it's important to choose the right fit when buying a Ralph Lauren shirt. The two most common fits are classic and custom.

Classic fit Ralph Lauren shirts are designed to be comfortable and relaxed. They are cut with a bit more room in the chest and waist, making them perfect for men who prefer a more traditional look. Custom fit Ralph Lauren shirts, on the other hand, are tailored to fit your body perfectly. They are slimmer in the chest and waist, giving you a more modern and streamlined look.

In summary, classic fit Ralph Lauren shirts are relaxed and comfortable, while custom fit shirts are tailored to fit your body perfectly. The choice between the two depends on your personal style and comfort level.

Classic Fit Ralph Lauren Shirts

When I first started buying Ralph Lauren shirts, I always went for the classic fit. I liked the way they felt and they gave me a traditional look that I was comfortable with. The classic fit is perfect for men who prefer a more relaxed style or have a bigger build. The shirts are cut with more room in the chest and waist, making them more comfortable to wear. They also have longer sleeves and a longer tail, which is perfect for taller men.

The great thing about classic fit Ralph Lauren shirts is that they can be dressed up or down. You can wear them with jeans for a casual look or pair them with dress pants for a more formal occasion. They come in a variety of colors and patterns, so you can find one that suits your personal style.

Custom Fit Ralph Lauren Shirts

After a while, I started to notice that my classic fit Ralph Lauren shirts didn't fit me as well as they used to. I decided to try on a custom fit shirt and I was amazed at the difference. Which One Should You Choose?

The choice between classic and custom fit Ralph Lauren shirts ultimately comes down to your personal style and comfort level. If you prefer a more relaxed style or have a bigger build, then the classic fit is perfect for you. If you prefer a more modern and streamlined look, then the custom fit is the way to go. Try on both styles and see which one feels more comfortable and looks better on you.

How to Care for Your Ralph Lauren Shirt

Once you've found the perfect Ralph Lauren shirt, it's important to take care of it properly. Always follow the care instructions on the label to ensure that your shirt lasts as long as possible. Here are some general tips for caring for your Ralph Lauren shirt:

  1. Wash your shirt in cold water to prevent shrinking.
  2. Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach.
  3. Hang your shirt to dry to prevent wrinkles.
  4. If you must use a dryer, use a low heat setting.
  5. Iron your shirt on a low heat setting, if necessary.
