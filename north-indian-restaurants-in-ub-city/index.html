Discover The Best North Indian Restaurants In Ub City

Written by Oliver Jun 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you love the flavors of North Indian cuisine and are looking to explore some of the best restaurants in UB City? With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to dine. From traditional tandoori dishes to rich, creamy curries, North Indian cuisine is known for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. However, finding the perfect restaurant that serves authentic North Indian cuisine can be a challenge.

If you're a foodie looking for the best North Indian restaurants in UB City, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the top restaurants that offer the best North Indian cuisine in UB City.

When it comes to North Indian cuisine, UB City is the perfect destination to satisfy your cravings. With its range of restaurants and cafes, UB City offers the perfect blend of luxury and authenticity. From fine dining restaurants to casual eateries, you'll find something for everyone.

So, let's dive into the top North Indian restaurants in UB City and discover the best dishes they have to offer.

1. Rasovara

If you're looking for an authentic North Indian dining experience, Rasovara is the perfect place to be. Located at The Collection, UB City, Rasovara offers a range of dishes that are inspired by the royal kitchens of Rajasthan and Gujarat. The ambience of the restaurant is elegant and regal, which adds to the overall dining experience. One of the must-try dishes at Rasovara is the Rajasthani thali, which is a platter that consists of a variety of dishes including dal, kadhi, vegetables, and sweets.

2. Dum Pukht Jolly Nabobs

If you're in the mood for some fine dining, Dum Pukht Jolly Nabobs is the perfect restaurant for you. Located at The Ritz-Carlton, UB City, Dum Pukht Jolly Nabobs offers a range of North Indian dishes that are prepared using traditional cooking techniques. The restaurant's signature dish is the Dum Pukht biryani, which is a slow-cooked biryani that is infused with aromatic spices.

3. Made in Punjab

If you're looking for a casual dining experience, Made in Punjab is the perfect restaurant for you. Located at Level 2, UB City, Made in Punjab offers a range of North Indian dishes that are inspired by the streets of Punjab. The restaurant's ambience is vibrant and lively, which adds to the overall dining experience. One of the must-try dishes at Made in Punjab is the butter chicken, which is a creamy and flavorful dish that is served with naan or rice.

Why Choose North Indian Restaurants in UB City?

Now that we've explored the top North Indian restaurants in UB City, you might be wondering why you should choose these restaurants over others. North Indian cuisine is known for its bold flavors and aromatic spices, and these restaurants offer an authentic dining experience that can't be found anywhere else. Whether you're looking for fine dining or a casual experience, UB City offers a range of restaurants that cater to all tastes and preferences.

How to Choose the Best North Indian Restaurant in UB City?

Choosing the best North Indian restaurant in UB City can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to the city. However, there are a few things you can keep in mind to help you make the right choice. Firstly, look for restaurants that offer authentic North Indian cuisine that is prepared using traditional cooking techniques. Secondly, read reviews and ratings of the restaurants to get an idea of the quality of food and service. Lastly, consider the ambience and overall dining experience offered by the restaurant.

4. Saffron

If you're looking for a fine dining experience that offers a range of North Indian dishes, Saffron is the perfect restaurant for you. Located at The Park, UB City, Saffron offers a range of dishes that are prepared by expert chefs using traditional cooking techniques. The restaurant's signature dish is the dum ki nalli, which is a slow-cooked lamb shank that is infused with aromatic spices.

5. MTR 1924

If you're looking for a taste of traditional South Indian and North Indian cuisine, MTR 1924 is the perfect restaurant for you. Located at Level 2, UB City, MTR 1924 offers a range of dishes that are inspired by the flavors of Karnataka and North India. One of the must-try dishes at MTR 1924 is the masala dosa, which is a crispy dosa that is filled with a spicy potato filling.
