5 Ethical Menswear Brands You Should Know About

Written by William Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever think about the clothes you wear and where they come from? Are you concerned about the ethical practices of the fashion industry? If so, you're not alone. Many people are becoming more aware of the impact that their clothing choices have on the environment and on the people who make them. Finding ethical menswear brands can be a challenge, but there are some great options out there if you know where to look.

The target of ethical menswear brands is to provide clothing that is made in a way that is environmentally and socially responsible. This means that the fabrics are sourced sustainably, the people who make the clothes are paid fair wages and work in safe conditions, and the companies have a commitment to reducing their environmental impact. These brands are often more expensive than fast fashion options, but the quality and durability of the clothing is typically higher.

In this article, we will introduce you to five ethical menswear brands that are worth checking out. We'll cover the target of each brand, their sustainability practices, and some of their standout pieces. Whether you're looking for casual wear or something more formal, there's an ethical menswear brand out there for you.

1. Patagonia

Patagonia is a well-known brand that has been committed to environmental and social responsibility since its inception in 1973. They are known for their high-quality outdoor clothing, but they also offer a range of casual and formal wear. Patagonia is committed to using sustainable materials, such as organic cotton and recycled polyester, and they have a program in place to ensure fair labor practices throughout their supply chain. Their standout pieces include their Better Sweater and their 100% Organic Cotton T-Shirts.

2. Outerknown

Outerknown is a newer brand that was founded by surfer Kelly Slater in 2015. They are committed to sustainability and transparency in their supply chain, and they use a range of sustainable materials, such as organic cotton, hemp, and recycled polyester. Outerknown is known for their casual wear, such as their S.E.A. Jeans and their Blanket Shirt.

3. Nudie Jeans

Nudie Jeans is a Swedish denim brand that is committed to sustainability and ethical manufacturing. They use organic cotton and recycled materials in their clothing, and they have a repair program in place to extend the life of their products. Nudie Jeans is known for their high-quality denim, such as their Grim Tim and Lean Dean styles.

4. Everlane

Everlane is a brand that is committed to radical transparency in their supply chain. They provide information on the factories where their clothing is made, the cost of production, and their markups. Everlane uses sustainable materials, such as organic cotton and recycled polyester, and they are known for their high-quality basics, such as their T-Shirts and their Cashmere Crew.

5. Veja

Veja is a French sneaker brand that is committed to sustainability and fair labor practices. They use sustainable materials, such as organic cotton and wild rubber, and they have a commitment to transparency in their supply chain. Veja is known for their stylish sneakers, such as their V-10 and their Esplar styles.

Question and Answer

Q: Why are ethical menswear brands important?

A: Ethical menswear brands are important because they provide clothing that is made in a way that is environmentally and socially responsible. This means that the fabrics are sourced sustainably, the people who make the clothes are paid fair wages and work in safe conditions, and the companies have a commitment to reducing their environmental impact.

Q: Are ethical menswear brands more expensive than fast fashion options?

A: Yes, ethical menswear brands are typically more expensive than fast fashion options. However, the quality and durability of the clothing is typically higher, which means that the clothing will last longer and need to be replaced less frequently.

Q: What are some sustainable materials that ethical menswear brands use?

A: Some sustainable materials that ethical menswear brands use include organic cotton, hemp, recycled polyester, and wild rubber.

Q: How can I find ethical menswear brands?

A: You can find ethical menswear brands by doing research online, checking out sustainable fashion blogs and websites, and asking for recommendations from friends who are interested in ethical fashion.

Conclusion of Ethical Menswear Brands

If you're looking for clothing that is made in a way that is environmentally and socially responsible, then ethical menswear brands are a great option. Patagonia, Outerknown, Nudie Jeans, Everlane, and Veja are just a few of the many ethical menswear brands that are out there. By supporting these brands, you can feel good about the impact that your clothing choices are having on the world.
