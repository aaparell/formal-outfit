Discovering Local Montreal Clothing Brands A Guide

Written by Frank Oct 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of purchasing generic clothing from big retail stores? Do you want to support local businesses and wear unique pieces that reflect Montreal's culture and style? Look no further! In this guide, we will introduce you to local Montreal clothing brands that offer quality, creativity, and affordability.

Montreal is known for its vibrant fashion scene and diverse communities. However, finding local clothing brands can be challenging, especially if you are not familiar with the city's neighborhoods and events. Moreover, many people hesitate to shop from small businesses due to the misconception that they are expensive or inconvenient.

Our target is to showcase the variety of local Montreal clothing brands and their products, while debunking myths and encouraging readers to explore new options. We believe that supporting local businesses not only benefits the economy but also contributes to preserving Montreal's identity and spirit.

Overall, this guide will provide you with a comprehensive overview of local Montreal clothing brands, including their specialties, locations, and values. We will also share our personal experiences and tips for finding the right brand for your taste and budget.

1. Frank And Oak

Frank And Oak is a Montreal-based brand that offers sustainable and stylish clothing for men and women. Their collections feature classic and modern designs, made from eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton and recycled polyester. Frank And Oak also provides a monthly subscription service, where members can receive personalized recommendations and discounts.

As a Frank And Oak customer, I appreciate the brand's commitment to reducing waste and promoting ethical practices in the fashion industry. Their clothes are comfortable, durable, and versatile, suitable for various occasions and seasons. I recommend checking out their online store or visiting their physical locations in Montreal, including the flagship store on St-Catherine Street.

2. Harricana

Harricana is a luxury brand that specializes in creating high-end clothing and accessories from recycled fur and leather. Founded by Marie-Eve and Mélissa Proulx, Harricana emphasizes sustainability and craftsmanship in every piece, while embracing Quebec's heritage and environment. Their collections range from jackets and hats to jewelry and home decor, all made with the highest attention to detail and quality.

As a Harricana admirer, I admire the brand's ability to transform old materials into new and exquisite creations, without compromising on style or ethics. Their products are unique, elegant, and timeless, perfect for those who appreciate luxury and sustainability. I recommend visiting their boutique on St-Laurent Boulevard or browsing their website for inspiration and admiration.

3. Atelier New Regime

Atelier New Regime is a streetwear brand that celebrates Montreal's diverse culture and youth. Founded by Koku Awuye and Gildas Awuye, Atelier New Regime offers bold and colorful clothing that reflects the city's street art, music, and sports scenes. Their collections include hoodies, t-shirts, jackets, and accessories, all designed with a rebellious and creative spirit.

As an Atelier New Regime follower, I appreciate the brand's ability to capture Montreal's energy and diversity in their clothing. Their products are comfortable, expressive, and affordable, suitable for those who want to stand out and make a statement. I recommend visiting their boutique on Saint-Hubert Street or checking out their online store for new releases and collaborations.

4. M0851

M0851 is a leather goods brand that combines functionality and elegance in their products. Founded by Frédéric Mamarbachi, M0851 offers a range of bags, jackets, wallets, and accessories, all made from premium leather and designed with a minimalist and modern approach. Their products are timeless, durable, and suitable for various lifestyles and preferences.

As a M0851 customer, I appreciate the brand's attention to detail and quality in their products, as well as their commitment to environmental and social responsibility. Their products are versatile, practical, and stylish, suitable for those who value simplicity and sophistication. I recommend visiting their flagship store on St-Laurent Boulevard or exploring their online store for new arrivals and customization options.

Question and Answer

Q1. Are local Montreal clothing brands more expensive than big retail brands?

A1. Not necessarily. While some local brands may have higher prices due to their production methods or materials, many others offer competitive and affordable prices. Moreover, shopping from local brands can save you money in the long run, as their products are usually of higher quality and last longer than mass-produced items.

Q2. Where can I find local Montreal clothing brands?

A2. You can find local Montreal clothing brands in various locations, such as independent stores, markets, festivals, and online platforms. Some popular neighborhoods for local fashion include the Plateau, Mile End, and Griffintown. You can also check out events such as Puces Pop, Souk @ SAT, and Marché des Possibles.

Q3. What makes local Montreal clothing brands unique?

A3. Local Montreal clothing brands are unique because they reflect the city's culture, creativity, and diversity. They often incorporate elements of Quebecois or Canadian heritage, as well as international influences, into their designs. Moreover, local brands prioritize sustainability, ethics, and innovation in their production methods, creating products that are both fashionable and conscious.

Q4. How can I support local Montreal clothing brands?

A4. You can support local Montreal clothing brands by purchasing their products, sharing their story on social media, attending their events or shows, and recommending them to your friends and family. You can also join local fashion communities or organizations, such as Montreal Fashion Week or Fashion Takes Action, to learn more about the industry and connect with like-minded individuals.

Conclusion of Local Montreal Clothing Brands

Local Montreal clothing brands offer a wide range of options for those who want to express themselves, support their community, and contribute to a sustainable and ethical fashion industry. From sustainable and stylish to luxury and heritage, local brands showcase Montreal's identity and creativity in every piece. We hope that this guide has inspired you to explore new brands and styles, and to appreciate the beauty and value of local fashion.
