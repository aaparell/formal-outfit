Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear dress shoes with jeans?

A: It depends on the style of dress shoes and the occasion. Generally, dress shoes are better suited for formal occasions and should be worn with dress pants. However, if you choose a more casual style of dress shoe, such as a loafer, they can be worn with jeans for a more casual look.

Q: Should I go for a classic or modern style of dress shoe?

A: It depends on your personal style and the occasion. Classic styles, such as oxfords, are more formal and are better suited for dressier occasions. Modern styles, such as loafers, can be dressed up or down and are better suited for more casual occasions.

Q: How do I care for my dress shoes?

A: To care for your dress shoes, use a quality leather cleaner and conditioner. Avoid getting your shoes wet and let them dry naturally if they do get wet. Use a shoehorn to help preserve the shape of the shoe when putting them on.

Q: How often should I replace my dress shoes?

A: It depends on how often you wear them and how well you care for them. Generally, a quality pair of dress shoes can last for several years with proper care.
