Question and Answer

Q: How much does it cost to get custom t-shirts made?

A: The cost of custom t-shirts can vary depending on the printing method, the number of shirts ordered, and the complexity of the design. Local print shops and screen printing companies may charge anywhere from $10-$30 per shirt, while online t-shirt printing services typically charge between $8-$20 per shirt.

Q: How long does it take to get custom t-shirts made?

A: The turnaround time for custom t-shirts can vary depending on the printing method and the company you choose. Local print shops and screen printing companies may take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks to complete an order, while online t-shirt printing services typically have a faster turnaround time of 1-2 weeks.

Q: What is the best material for custom t-shirts?

A: The best material for custom t-shirts depends on your needs and preferences. Cotton is a popular choice for its softness and durability, while polyester is a good option for moisture-wicking and athletic wear. Some t-shirts may also include blends of cotton and polyester or other materials.

Q: Can I get custom t-shirts made for a small order?

A: Yes, many t-shirt printing services offer small order options for as little as one shirt. However, keep in mind that the price per shirt may be higher for smaller orders.
