Cool Clothing Stores In Calgary A Guide To The Best Fashion Finds

Written by Luffy Jul 28, 2023 · 5 min read

Ah, Calgary. Known for its outdoor adventures and beautiful scenery, it's also home to some of the coolest clothing stores in Canada. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. If you're looking to revamp your wardrobe or add some unique pieces to your collection, read on for our guide to the best cool clothing stores in Calgary.

If you're tired of the same old styles found at big box retailers or overwhelmed by the fast fashion industry, you're not alone. Many people are seeking unique, ethically-made clothing options that reflect their personal style. That's where cool clothing stores in Calgary come in. These shops offer a curated selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories that are often locally-made or sourced from sustainable and ethical brands.

So, what's the target of cool clothing stores in Calgary? It's for people who want to stand out from the crowd and express their individuality through fashion. It's for those who want to support small businesses and make a positive impact on the environment and society through their shopping choices.

In summary, if you're looking for cool clothing stores in Calgary, you're in luck. These shops offer unique, ethically-made clothing options that reflect your personal style while also making a positive impact on the environment and society.

The Livery Shop

Located in the historic Inglewood neighbourhood, The Livery Shop is a must-visit for anyone looking for cool clothing stores in Calgary. The shop offers a well-curated selection of clothing, accessories, and home decor items that are often designed and made in Canada. The Livery Shop is also known for its collaborations with local artists and designers, which means you can find one-of-a-kind pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

My personal experience at The Livery Shop was amazing. As soon as I walked in, I was greeted by friendly staff who were happy to help me find the perfect outfit for an upcoming event. I ended up leaving with a beautiful handmade dress that I received so many compliments on. I also loved that The Livery Shop puts a strong emphasis on supporting local designers and artists.

Gravity Pope

If you're a shoe lover, you need to check out Gravity Pope. This cool clothing store in Calgary offers a wide selection of shoes from some of the world's best designers. From casual sneakers to dressy heels, you'll find something for every occasion at Gravity Pope. The shop also offers a selection of clothing and accessories that complement their footwear collection.

My personal experience at Gravity Pope was a dream come true. As someone who loves shoes, I was in heaven browsing their selection of unique and stylish footwear. I ended up leaving with a pair of funky sneakers that I've been wearing non-stop since I bought them.

Plant

Plant is a cool clothing store in Calgary that focuses on sustainable and ethical fashion. The shop offers a selection of clothing, accessories, and beauty products that are made from eco-friendly materials and are often locally-made. Plant also puts a strong emphasis on transparency and education, so you can feel good knowing that the products you're buying are making a positive impact on the environment and society.

If you're looking to make more conscious shopping choices, I highly recommend checking out Plant. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and they'll help you find the perfect sustainable and ethical pieces to add to your wardrobe.

Understudy

Understudy is a cool clothing store in Calgary that offers a selection of men's and women's clothing, accessories, and lifestyle products. The shop focuses on quality over quantity, offering unique pieces that are designed to last. Understudy also puts a strong emphasis on community, hosting events and collaborations with local artists and designers.

My personal experience at Understudy was great. The shop had a welcoming atmosphere, and the staff was friendly and helpful. I loved browsing their selection of unique clothing and accessories, and I ended up leaving with a beautiful leather bag that I've been using every day since I bought it.

Conclusion of Cool Clothing Stores in Calgary

Whether you're looking for sustainable and ethical clothing options, unique footwear, or stylish accessories, there are plenty of cool clothing stores in Calgary to choose from. These shops offer a curated selection of high-quality pieces that are designed to last, making them a great choice for anyone who wants to express their individuality through fashion while also making a positive impact on the environment and society.
