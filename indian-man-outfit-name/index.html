Sherwani

As one of the most popular and elegant outfits for Indian men, the sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is typically worn for formal occasions such as weddings and other special events. The sherwani is usually made from silk or other fine fabrics and is often embellished with intricate embroidery or beading. I had a personal experience wearing a sherwani at my friend's wedding, and it was undoubtedly one of the most comfortable and stylish outfits I have ever worn.

Kurta

The kurta is a popular and versatile Indian man outfit that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions. It is a long tunic-style shirt that is typically made from cotton or silk and can be worn with either pajama pants or jeans. I have worn a kurta to several events, and it is undoubtedly one of the most comfortable and stylish outfits in my wardrobe.

Dhoti

The dhoti is a traditional Indian garment that is typically worn by men for formal occasions such as weddings and religious ceremonies. It is a long piece of fabric that is wrapped around the waist and legs in a specific way to create a loose, flowing garment. The dhoti is usually made from cotton or silk and can be quite comfortable to wear, although it does take some practice to master the art of wrapping it correctly.

How to Wear a Sherwani

Wearing a sherwani is relatively simple, but there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that you look your best. Firstly, make sure that you choose a sherwani that fits well and is comfortable to wear. It is also important to choose the right accessories, such as a turban or a pair of traditional Indian shoes. Finally, make sure that you know how to tie a dupatta (a long scarf that is often worn with a sherwani) correctly.

Bandhgala

The bandhgala is a traditional Indian jacket that is typically worn for formal occasions such as weddings and other special events. It is a short, fitted jacket that is often made from silk or other fine fabrics and is embellished with intricate embroidery or beading. The bandhgala can be worn with either pajama pants or jeans, depending on the occasion.
