The Best Suit Brands in the UK: Finding the Perfect Fit

Choosing the perfect suit can be a daunting task, especially when there are so many options to choose from. From the fit to the style, there are many factors to consider when selecting a suit that not only looks good but feels good too. Additionally, with so many different brands to choose from, it can be challenging to know where to start. In this post, we will explore some of the best suit brands in the UK and provide tips on how to find the perfect fit for you.

When it comes to selecting a suit, it's essential to keep your body type in mind. Different brands have different fits, and what may work for one person may not work for another. Additionally, consider the occasion you are purchasing the suit for. Is it for a wedding or a job interview? The type of event will dictate the style of the suit you choose.

Overall, the goal of selecting a suit is to find one that fits well and makes you feel confident. The best suit brands in the UK offer a range of styles and fits to cater to different individuals' needs. Below we will explore some of the top brands and what they have to offer.

1. Moss Bros

Moss Bros is a well-known brand in the UK and has been around since 1851. They offer a wide range of suits, from traditional to contemporary styles. One of the unique features of Moss Bros is their "Tailor Me" service, which allows customers to customize their suits to fit their specific body type.

Personally, I had a great experience with Moss Bros when selecting a suit for my brother's wedding. The staff was incredibly helpful and provided a lot of guidance on selecting the right fit for my body type. The suit was also reasonably priced, which was a bonus.

2. Ted Baker

Ted Baker is a luxury brand that offers a range of suits that cater to different body types. Their suits are known for their attention to detail, and the brand offers a range of styles from classic to contemporary. Additionally, Ted Baker offers a range of accessories, including ties and pocket squares, that can complement your suit.

I recently purchased a Ted Baker suit for a job interview, and I was impressed with the quality and fit. The suit was a bit pricier than some of the other brands I looked at, but it was worth it for the quality.

3. Reiss

Reiss is a brand that offers high-quality suits with a modern, contemporary twist. Their suits are known for their slim fit and attention to detail. The brand offers a range of styles and colors to cater to different individuals' needs.

I had a great experience with Reiss when selecting a suit for my friend's wedding. The staff was incredibly friendly and provided a lot of guidance on selecting the right fit for my body type. The suit was a bit pricier than some of the other brands I looked at, but it was worth it for the quality and fit.

The Importance of Finding the Right Fit

Finding the right fit is crucial when selecting a suit. A suit that fits well will make you feel confident and comfortable. Additionally, a well-fitted suit can make you look slimmer and taller. When selecting a suit, consider the following:

  • The shoulder seam should sit on the shoulder bone
  • The sleeve should end at the wrist bone
  • The jacket should be fitted but not too tight
  • The trousers should sit comfortably on the waist and not be too long or too short

How to Care for Your Suit

Once you have found the perfect suit, it's essential to take care of it to ensure it lasts for years to come. Here are some tips on how to care for your suit:

  • Hang your suit on a wooden hanger to maintain its shape
  • Avoid wearing your suit more than once a week to prevent wear and tear
  • Ensure your suit is dry cleaned only
  • Store your suit in a breathable garment bag to protect it from dust and dirt

Conclusion of Best Suit Brands UK

Overall, selecting the perfect suit can be a challenging task, but by keeping your body type and the occasion in mind, you can narrow down your options. The best suit brands in the UK offer a range of styles and fits to cater to different individuals' needs. When selecting a suit, remember to focus on finding the right fit, and take care of your suit to ensure it lasts for years to come.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best way to find the right fit?

A: The best way to find the right fit is to try on different suits and see which one feels the most comfortable. Additionally, consider the occasion you are purchasing the suit for and what style would be appropriate.

Q: What is the benefit of purchasing a tailored suit?

A: The benefit of purchasing a tailored suit is that it is customized to your body type, ensuring the perfect fit. Additionally, you can customize the suit to your liking, adding unique features such as a monogram or specific buttons.

Q: Can you wear a suit to a casual event?

A: It depends on the occasion. If the event is very casual, it may not be appropriate to wear a suit. However, if the event is semi-casual, a suit can be worn with a more casual shirt or shoes.

Q: How often should you dry clean your suit?

A: It is recommended to dry clean your suit only when necessary, as the chemicals used in the process can break down the fibers of the suit. If your suit has a stain or odor, it may be time to dry clean it. Otherwise, spot cleaning may be sufficient.
