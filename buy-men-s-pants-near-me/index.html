Knowing Your Measurements

Before you start shopping for pants, it's essential to know your measurements. This will help you find the right size and fit for your body type. To determine your measurements, you will need a measuring tape and a friend to assist you.

First, measure your waist by wrapping the measuring tape around the narrowest part of your waist. Next, measure your inseam by measuring from the top of your inner thigh to the bottom of your ankle. Finally, measure your hips by wrapping the measuring tape around the widest part of your hips.
