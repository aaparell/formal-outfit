What To Wear With Black And Red Shoes A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Black and red shoes are a statement piece that can add a pop of color and personality to any outfit. However, figuring out what to wear with them can be a challenge, especially if you're not used to wearing bold colors. In this guide, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to style black and red shoes.

Are you struggling to find the right outfit to wear with your black and red shoes? Maybe you're worried about looking too flashy or not knowing what colors to pair with them. These are common pain points that many people experience when it comes to styling these types of shoes.

The good news is that black and red shoes can be paired with a variety of outfits and colors. The key is to find balance and make sure the shoes don't overpower the rest of your outfit. Keep reading for our top tips on what to wear with black and red shoes.

In summary, black and red shoes are a versatile statement piece that can elevate any outfit. To style them, balance is key- make sure the shoes don't overpower the rest of your outfit. Now, let's dive deeper into our top tips on how to wear black and red shoes.

Pair with Neutral Colors

One of the easiest ways to style black and red shoes is to pair them with neutral colors such as black, white, grey, or beige. This allows the shoes to stand out without being too overwhelming. For example, you could wear black and red sneakers with black jeans and a white t-shirt for a casual yet put-together look.

Personally, I love wearing my black and red pumps with a black dress and a beige trench coat. The neutral colors provide a sleek and sophisticated backdrop for the bold shoes.

Go for a Monochromatic Look

If you're feeling bold, you can also try a monochromatic look by pairing your black and red shoes with similar shades of red or black. For example, you could wear red pants with black and red sneakers or a black dress with red pumps.

When trying a monochromatic look, make sure to vary the textures and shades to add dimension to your outfit. You don't want to look like you're wearing a costume!

Add a Pop of Color

If you want to add some color to your outfit but don't want to go overboard, you can pair your black and red shoes with a single statement piece in a complementary color. For example, you could wear a red scarf with a black sweater and black jeans.

Another option is to wear black and red sneakers with a denim jacket and a yellow t-shirt. The yellow provides a fun contrast to the black and red shoes without being too overwhelming.

Accessorize with Black and Red

If you're not ready to commit to wearing black and red clothing, you can still accessorize with these colors. For example, you could wear black pants, a white blouse, and a black and red scarf. This allows you to add a pop of color without being too flashy.

Personally, I love wearing my black and red shoes with a black crossbody bag and a red lip. It's a subtle yet effective way to tie the outfit together.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black and red shoes with other bright colors?

A: Yes, you can, but make sure to balance the outfit and choose complementary colors. For example, you could wear black and red sneakers with a green or blue dress.

Q: Can I wear black and red shoes to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can, but make sure to choose a dress or suit that complements the shoes. For example, you could wear black and red pumps with a black dress and silver jewelry.

Q: Are black and red shoes only for women?

A: No, black and red shoes can be worn by anyone. Men can pair black and red sneakers with black or grey jeans and a white t-shirt for a casual look.

Q: Can I wear black and red shoes to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace allows for casual attire, you can wear black and red sneakers with black pants and a blouse. If your workplace is more formal, you can wear black and red pumps with a black suit or dress.

Conclusion of What to Wear with Black and Red Shoes

In conclusion, black and red shoes are a versatile and stylish statement piece that can be paired with a variety of outfits and colors. The key is to find balance and make sure the shoes don't overpower the rest of your outfit. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, black and red shoes can add a pop of color and personality to your outfit. So go ahead and rock those bold shoes!
