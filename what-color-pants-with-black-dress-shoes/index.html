What Color Pants To Wear With Black Dress Shoes

Written by Daniel Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Black dress shoes are a staple in every man's wardrobe. They're versatile, stylish, and can be worn with just about anything. But when it comes to pairing them with pants, many men struggle with what colors will work best. In this tutorial blog post, we will answer your question about what color pants to wear with black dress shoes.

It's frustrating when we can't decide on what color pants to wear with black dress shoes. Some of us might even give up and end up wearing the same old pants every time. But, it doesn't have to be that way! We understand that it's a common pain point for many men.

The answer to what color pants to wear with black dress shoes is pretty simple. You can wear black dress shoes with black, navy blue, charcoal grey, light grey, and even burgundy pants. These colors will look great with your black dress shoes and make you look stylish and put together.

In summary, the best colors to wear with black dress shoes are black, navy blue, charcoal grey, light grey, and burgundy. These colors will ensure that you look stylish and put together.

Black Pants with Black Dress Shoes

When it comes to pairing black pants with black dress shoes, it's a no-brainer. This combination is classic, stylish, and perfect for any occasion. You can wear a black suit with black dress shoes for a formal event or black jeans with black dress shoes for a casual day out. I personally love wearing black dress shoes with black pants because it's effortless and always looks good.

Navy Blue Pants with Black Dress Shoes

Navy blue pants are a great option to pair with black dress shoes. The combination of navy blue and black is classic and timeless. You can wear navy blue pants with a white dress shirt and a black blazer for a formal event or navy blue jeans with a black t-shirt for a casual day out. I love this combination because it's simple yet stylish.

Charcoal Grey Pants with Black Dress Shoes

Charcoal grey pants are another great option to pair with black dress shoes. This combination is perfect for a business casual or semi-formal event. You can wear charcoal grey pants with a white dress shirt and a black blazer for a more formal look or with a black t-shirt for a more casual look. I love this combination because it's versatile and can be dressed up or down.

Light Grey Pants with Black Dress Shoes

Light grey pants are a great option for a more casual look. You can wear light grey pants with a black t-shirt and black dress shoes for a day out with friends or with a white dress shirt and black blazer for a semi-formal event. This combination is perfect for the summer months and looks great with a tan.

Burgundy Pants with Black Dress Shoes

Burgundy pants are a bold and stylish option to pair with black dress shoes. This combination is perfect for a night out or a special event. You can wear burgundy pants with a black dress shirt and black dress shoes for a bold and stylish look. I personally love this combination because it's unique and stands out from the crowd.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear brown pants with black dress shoes?

A: No, it's not recommended to wear brown pants with black dress shoes. Brown and black are contrasting colors that don't pair well together.

Q: Can I wear white pants with black dress shoes?

A: Yes, you can wear white pants with black dress shoes. This combination is perfect for the summer months and looks great with a black t-shirt or a white dress shirt.

Q: Can I wear green pants with black dress shoes?

A: Yes, you can wear green pants with black dress shoes. Green is a versatile color that pairs well with black dress shoes. You can wear olive green pants with a black t-shirt for a casual look or a darker shade of green with a black blazer for a more formal event.

Q: Can I wear black dress shoes with jeans?

A: Yes, you can wear black dress shoes with jeans. This combination is perfect for a casual day out or a night out with friends. You can wear black jeans with a white t-shirt and black dress shoes for a classic and stylish look.

Conclusion of What Color Pants with Black Dress Shoes

Pairing pants with black dress shoes doesn't have to be a difficult task. Stick to the classic and timeless colors like black, navy blue, charcoal grey, light grey, and burgundy, and you'll always look stylish and put together. Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and combinations, and always remember to wear what makes you feel confident and comfortable.
