What is the World Global 100?

The World Global 100 is an annual ranking of the most sustainable companies in the world, compiled by the Canadian media and investment research company Corporate Knights. The list is based on a range of ESG factors, including carbon emissions, waste reduction, gender diversity, and executive compensation.

To be eligible for the World Global 100, companies must have a market capitalization of at least $2 billion and be publicly traded. The list includes businesses from a range of industries and countries, including both developed and emerging economies.

Why Is Sustainability Important?

Sustainability is important for a number of reasons. From an environmental perspective, it is essential for protecting our planet and ensuring that future generations have access to the resources they need to thrive. It can also have significant economic benefits for businesses that prioritize sustainability, including increased efficiency, reduced costs, and improved brand reputation.

Additionally, many consumers are becoming more interested in supporting sustainable businesses, and may be more likely to purchase products or services from companies that prioritize environmental and social responsibility. This can create a competitive advantage for businesses that are committed to sustainability.

How Are Companies Selected for the World Global 100?

The World Global 100 uses a range of ESG factors to determine which companies are the most sustainable. These factors include:

  • Energy productivity
  • Carbon productivity
  • Water productivity
  • Waste productivity
  • CEO-to-worker pay ratio
  • Leadership diversity
  • Employee turnover
  • Lost-time injury rate

Each company is given a score based on these factors, and the top 100 businesses are included in the final ranking. The World Global 100 is updated annually to reflect changes in company performance and ESG factors.
