Pain Points of Expensive Boxing

For many boxing fans, the cost of attending a match can be a significant pain point. Tickets for some of the more expensive boxing matches can cost thousands of dollars, putting them out of reach for most casual fans. Additionally, the cost of travel, accommodations, and other expenses can quickly add up, making it challenging for fans to attend these matches.

The Mayweather vs. Pacquiao fight was so expensive for a few different reasons.
