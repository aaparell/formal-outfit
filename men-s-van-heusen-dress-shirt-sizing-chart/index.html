Understanding the Van Heusen Dress Shirt Sizing Chart

The Van Heusen dress shirt sizing chart is designed to be easy to use and understand. It includes detailed measurements for the collar, chest, waist, and sleeves, as well as guidance for choosing the right fit. To use the chart, you'll need to take your own measurements and compare them to the chart.

When taking your measurements, it's important to use a soft tape measure and to measure over bare skin or thin clothing. For the collar measurement, measure around the base of your neck where the collar would sit. For the chest measurement, measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure parallel to the ground. For the waist measurement, measure around your natural waistline, which is usually around your belly button. For the sleeve measurement, measure from the center of your back to your wrist, with your arm extended and your elbow slightly bent.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Fit

When using the Van Heusen dress shirt sizing chart, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that you find your perfect fit. First, consider the style of shirt you're looking for. If you're shopping for a classic dress shirt, you may want a more fitted look, while a more casual style may be more forgiving in terms of fit.

Next, consider your body type. If you have a larger chest or broader shoulders, you may need to size up to ensure that the shirt fits comfortably. Conversely, if you have a slimmer build, you may need to size down for a more fitted look.

Finally, take into account the fabric of the shirt. Some fabrics, such as cotton, may shrink slightly after washing, so you may want to size up to accommodate this. Other fabrics, such as synthetic blends, may not shrink as much, so you may want to size down for a more fitted look.

Common Questions About the Van Heusen Dress Shirt Sizing Chart

Q: How do I know if my shirt fits properly?

A: A properly fitting shirt should fit snugly but not be too tight. The collar should fit comfortably around your neck, without feeling constricting. The sleeves should hit at your wrist bone and allow for comfortable movement. The shirt should also be long enough to tuck in, but not so long that it bunches up.

Q: Can I return a shirt if it doesn't fit?

A: Yes, most retailers allow for returns or exchanges if the shirt doesn't fit properly. Be sure to check the retailer's return policy before making your purchase.

Q: Do Van Heusen dress shirts come in different fits?

A: Yes, Van Heusen dress shirts come in a variety of fits, including classic fit, slim fit, and extra slim fit. Be sure to check the size chart and fit guide to find the best fit for your body type and style preference.

Q: Should I dry clean or wash my Van Heusen dress shirt?

A: It depends on the fabric of the shirt. Some fabrics, such as silk or wool, may require dry cleaning to maintain their shape and texture. Other fabrics, such as cotton or polyester, can be washed at home. Be sure to check the care instructions on your shirt before washing or dry cleaning.

Conclusion

The Van Heusen dress shirt sizing chart is a valuable resource for men who are looking for the perfect fit. By taking accurate measurements and using the size chart, you can find a shirt that fits comfortably and looks great.
