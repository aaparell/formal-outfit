The answer to which gum brand lasts the longest is not straightforward as different people have different preferences. However, some brands are known for their longevity and flavor retention. These include brands such as Extra, Trident, and Eclipse.

The longevity of gum depends on several factors such as the type of sweetener used, the flavorings, and the texture.
