Who Is The Best Welterweight Boxer Today

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Boxing has been one of the most exciting sports to watch for decades. The welterweight division, in particular, has seen some of the greatest boxers in history. However, with so many talented fighters in the division, it can be challenging to determine who is the best welterweight boxer today.

After evaluating the current rankings, statistics, and performances, it's safe to say that the best welterweight boxer today is Terence Crawford.

Crawford, also known as "Bud," is an American professional boxer who has held multiple world championships in three different weight classes. He currently holds the WBO welterweight title, with an impressive record of 37 wins and zero losses.

In addition to his unbeaten record, Crawford has an impressive knockout ratio of 78.4%, making him one of the most dangerous boxers in the division. He has defeated some of the top fighters in the welterweight division, including Amir Khan, Jeff Horn, and Kell Brook.

Why is Terence Crawford the Best Welterweight Boxer Today?

As previously mentioned, Crawford has a perfect record and a high knockout ratio, which is enough to make him the best welterweight boxer today. However, there are several other reasons why he stands out from the rest of the division.

Firstly, Crawford is incredibly versatile in the ring. He can switch between orthodox and southpaw stances seamlessly, making it challenging for his opponents to predict his movements. He also has an excellent jab and footwork, allowing him to control the pace of the fight and set up his power shots.

Secondly, Crawford has a high boxing IQ. He can read his opponent's movements and adjust his game plan accordingly. This ability allows him to exploit his opponent's weaknesses and dominate the fight.

What About Other Top Welterweight Boxers?

While Crawford is undoubtedly the best welterweight boxer today, there are several other talented fighters in the division.

Errol Spence Jr. is another top welterweight boxer with an impressive record of 27 wins and zero losses. He currently holds the IBF and WBC titles and has defeated some of the top fighters in the division, including Shawn Porter and Danny Garcia.

Manny Pacquiao is another legendary fighter who has held multiple world championships in eight different weight classes. Although he is now 42 years old, he still holds the WBA welterweight title and has defeated some of the top fighters in the division, including Keith Thurman and Adrien Broner.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Terence Crawford is the best welterweight boxer today. His unbeaten record, high knockout ratio, versatility, and high boxing IQ make him a formidable opponent in the ring. However, the welterweight division is filled with talented fighters, and it will be exciting to see who will rise to the top in the future.
