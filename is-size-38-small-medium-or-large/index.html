Is Size 38 Small Medium Or Large

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to buying clothing, one of the most frustrating things can be trying to figure out what size to get. With so many different size charts and variations between brands and styles, it can be difficult to know what size will fit you best. One common question that many people have is whether size 38 is considered small, medium, or large.

Trying to determine what size 38 means can be a real pain point for many shoppers. It can be especially frustrating when shopping online and not being able to try clothes on first. Many people have had the experience of ordering a size that they thought would fit, only to have it be too big or too small.

The answer to the question of whether size 38 is small, medium, or large depends on a few different factors. The most important factor is where you are located in the world. Size charts can vary widely between countries and regions, so it's important to know which size chart you are using.

In general, if you are in the United States, size 38 would be considered a medium. In Europe, size 38 is typically a small. However, these are just general guidelines, and it's always best to refer to the specific size chart for the brand and style you are interested in.

Understanding Size 38 in More Detail

When I was shopping for a dress recently, I came across a size chart that listed size 38 as a medium. I was a little confused by this, as I had always thought of size 38 as being on the smaller side. After doing some research, I learned that size 38 is indeed considered a medium in the US, but a small in Europe.

One important thing to note is that size charts can vary widely between brands and even between different styles from the same brand. It's always best to refer to the specific size chart for the item you are interested in, rather than assuming that you know what size to get based on past experience.

Tips for Finding the Right Size

When it comes to finding the right size, there are a few tips that can be helpful. First, take accurate measurements of your body and compare them to the size chart for the brand you are interested in. This can help you get a better idea of what size to order.

Second, pay attention to the fit of the garment. When trying on clothes, look for any areas where the fabric is pulling or bunching, as this can indicate that the size is not quite right. If possible, try on multiple sizes to see which one fits best.

Common Questions About Size 38

1. Is size 38 considered small or medium?

In the United States, size 38 is typically considered a medium. In Europe, it is considered a small.

2. What is the best way to determine what size to get?

The best way to determine what size to get is to take accurate measurements of your body and compare them to the size chart for the brand you are interested in.

3. Can size 38 vary between brands?

Yes, size 38 can vary between different brands and even between different styles from the same brand. It's always best to refer to the specific size chart for the item you are interested in.

4. Should I always order the same size, regardless of the brand or style?

No, it's important to refer to the specific size chart for the brand and style you are interested in, rather than assuming that you know what size to get based on past experience.

Conclusion of Is Size 38 Small Medium or Large

When it comes to determining whether size 38 is small, medium, or large, the answer depends on a few different factors. In general, size 38 is considered a medium in the United States and a small in Europe, but it's always best to refer to the specific size chart for the brand and style you are interested in. By taking accurate measurements and paying attention to the fit of the garment, you can increase your chances of finding the right size.
