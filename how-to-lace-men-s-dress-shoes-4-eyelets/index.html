How To Lace Men s Dress Shoes 4 Eyelets A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to dressing up for formal occasions, men's dress shoes are a must-have. However, lacing them up can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're not used to it. In this post, we'll walk you through how to lace men's dress shoes with 4 eyelets in a way that's easy to understand.

Have you ever struggled with getting your dress shoes properly laced up? Perhaps you've found that the laces come undone easily, or maybe you've had trouble finding the right lacing pattern. Whatever your pain points may be, we're here to help.

To start, make sure that your dress shoes are clean and dry. This will prevent any dirt or debris from getting caught in the laces and making them difficult to tighten. Once your shoes are ready, you can begin lacing them up.

Before we get started with the step-by-step guide, here's a quick summary of what we'll cover:

  • The importance of clean, dry shoes
  • How to lace men's dress shoes with 4 eyelets
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Tips for making your laces stay put

Step 1: Start at the Bottom

The first step is to thread your laces through the bottom two eyelets, starting from the inside of the shoe. This will create a solid foundation for the rest of your lacing pattern.

Make sure that both ends of the lace are even before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Criss-Cross the Laces

Next, take the right end of the lace and thread it through the second eyelet on the left side of the shoe, so that it comes out on the top. Then, take the left end of the lace and thread it through the second eyelet on the right side of the shoe, so that it also comes out on the top.

Make sure that you pull the laces tight after each step to prevent any slack.

Step 3: Repeat the Pattern

Continue the criss-cross pattern, threading the laces through the next set of eyelets on each side of the shoe until you reach the top. Make sure that you pull the laces tight after each step to keep them snug against your foot.

Once you reach the top, tie your laces in a knot and tuck the ends into the sides of your shoes.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

When lacing up your dress shoes, there are a few common mistakes that you should avoid. These include:

  • Not pulling the laces tight enough
  • Skipping eyelets
  • Using the wrong lacing pattern

By following the steps above and paying attention to these common mistakes, you can ensure that your dress shoes are properly laced up and ready to go.

Tips for Making Your Laces Stay Put

If you've ever had your laces come untied during an important event, you know how frustrating it can be. Here are a few tips for making sure that your laces stay put:

  • Double-knot your laces
  • Use a small amount of hairspray or clear nail polish to stiffen the ends of the laces
  • Invest in high-quality laces that are less likely to come undone

Double-Knotting Your Laces

To double-knot your laces, tie them as you normally would and then tie another knot on top of the first one. This will create a more secure knot that is less likely to come undone.

Using Hairspray or Clear Nail Polish to Stiffen the Ends of Your Laces

If you find that your laces come undone easily, you can use a small amount of hairspray or clear nail polish to stiffen the ends of the laces. This will make them less likely to fray and unravel.

Conclusion

Knowing how to lace men's dress shoes with 4 eyelets is an important skill that every man should have. By following the steps outlined above and avoiding common mistakes, you can ensure that your dress shoes are properly laced up and ready to go for your next formal event.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the best lacing pattern for men's dress shoes with 4 eyelets?

A. The criss-cross lacing pattern is the best option for men's dress shoes with 4 eyelets.

Q. How can I make my laces stay put?

A. You can double-knot your laces, use a small amount of hairspray or clear nail polish to stiffen the ends of the laces, or invest in high-quality laces that are less likely to come undone.

Q. Should I skip any eyelets when lacing up my dress shoes?

A. No, you should not skip any eyelets when lacing up your dress shoes. This will create an uneven lacing pattern and may cause your shoes to fit improperly.

Q. How do I tie my laces properly?

A. To tie your laces properly, start by making a loop with one end of the lace. Then, make a similar loop with the other end of the lace. Cross the loops over each other and tuck one loop behind the other. Pull the loops tight to create a knot.
