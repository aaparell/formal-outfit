Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Wedding guest dress ideas 2020 have been a hot topic among fashion enthusiasts and wedding attendees. Finding the perfect outfit to wear to a wedding can be a daunting task, especially with the changing trends and styles in the fashion industry. However, fret not! We've got you covered with the latest wedding guest dress ideas 2020 that will make you stand out in any wedding.

Wedding guests often face the dilemma of choosing an appropriate outfit that follows the dress code and matches the wedding theme. Moreover, it should be comfortable enough to dance the night away and make you look stunning in pictures. The pressure to find the perfect wedding guest dress can be overwhelming, but we are here to help!

Our ultimate guide to wedding guest dress ideas 2020 will provide you with all the necessary information and tips to choose the perfect outfit for any wedding. We have curated the latest trends and styles that will make you look chic and stylish, without overshadowing the bride.

In summary, our guide will cover everything from dress codes, color schemes, styles, and accessories to create the perfect wedding guest outfit. Let's dive in!

Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020: Floral Dresses

Floral dresses are one of the most popular wedding guest dress ideas 2020. They are feminine, classy, and suitable for any wedding theme. You can choose from a variety of floral prints, including small or large, bright or muted, and bold or pastel. Floral dresses come in various styles, from maxi to midi to mini, and you can pair them with heels or flats, depending on the dress code.

Personally, I attended my best friend's garden wedding last summer and wore a pastel floral midi dress with nude heels. The dress was flowy and comfortable, perfect for dancing, and I received many compliments on it. I highly recommend floral dresses for any wedding guest looking for a timeless and versatile outfit.

Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020: Jumpsuits

Jumpsuits are a trendy and chic alternative to dresses and have become one of the popular wedding guest dress ideas 2020. Jumpsuits come in various styles, from wide-leg to tapered, and can be paired with statement earrings or a necklace to add some sparkle. They are comfortable, easy to wear, and suitable for any wedding dress code, from casual to black-tie.

Last fall, I attended a friend's beach wedding and wore a black wide-leg jumpsuit with a gold choker necklace. The outfit was effortless yet elegant, and I received many compliments on it. I highly recommend jumpsuits for any wedding guest looking for a modern and sophisticated outfit.

Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020: Color Schemes

Choosing the right color scheme for a wedding guest outfit can be tricky. However, there are some basic guidelines to follow. Avoid wearing white, ivory, or cream, as those colors are reserved for the bride. Similarly, avoid wearing black, as it can be seen as mourning or inappropriate for a wedding celebration.

Instead, opt for bold and bright colors, such as red, pink, purple, or blue. Pastel colors, such as blush, mint, or lavender, are also suitable for spring or summer weddings. If you prefer neutral colors, choose shades of gray, beige, or navy blue, and add some color with accessories.

Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020: Accessories

Accessories can make or break a wedding guest outfit. It's essential to choose the right accessories that match the dress code, theme, and color scheme. For instance, if you're wearing a bold print or color, opt for minimal accessories, such as stud earrings or a simple bracelet. If you're wearing a simple dress, you can add some sparkle with statement earrings or a necklace.

Shoes are also crucial to complete the wedding guest outfit. Choose comfortable yet stylish shoes, such as block heels, wedges, or sandals, depending on the dress code and venue. Avoid wearing stilettos or platforms, as they can be uncomfortable and challenging to walk in.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a white dress to a wedding if it has some color accents?

A: No, it's best to avoid wearing white dresses, even if they have some color accents. White is traditionally reserved for the bride, and wearing a white dress can be seen as disrespectful or attention-seeking.

Q: Can I wear a black dress to a wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing black dresses to a wedding, as black can be seen as mourning or inappropriate for a wedding celebration. However, if the dress code is black-tie, you can wear a black dress, but make sure to add some color with accessories.

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to a formal wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a jumpsuit to a formal wedding, but make sure to choose a dressy and elegant jumpsuit, such as a wide-leg or fitted style. Pair it with statement jewelry and heels to elevate the outfit.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a casual wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and venue of the wedding. If the dress code is casual and the wedding is outdoors or on a farm, you can wear jeans, but make sure to dress them up with a blouse, blazer, and heels or flats.

Conclusion of Wedding Guest Dress Ideas 2020

In conclusion, choosing the perfect outfit for a wedding can be stressful, but with our wedding guest dress ideas 2020 guide, you can look stylish and comfortable without upstaging the bride. Remember to follow the dress code, choose the right color scheme and accessories, and most importantly, have fun!
