Dress Price In Dubai A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Dubai is known for its luxurious lifestyle, high-end shopping malls, and extravagant fashion. But with all the glitz and glamour, comes the constant struggle of finding the perfect dress without breaking the bank. Dress shopping can be overwhelming, especially when you’re on a budget. But fret not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about dress price in Dubai.

When it comes to dress shopping, the price can be a major pain point for many shoppers. Dubai is known for its high-end fashion, and it’s no secret that designer dresses can cost a fortune. However, there are ways to find affordable options without compromising on quality.

If you’re looking for designer dresses, then be prepared to shell out a considerable amount of money. Designer dresses can range anywhere from AED 1,000 to AED 10,000, depending on the brand and the style. However, if you’re willing to compromise on the brand, then you can find dresses for as low as AED 100.

High-End Dress Shopping

My personal experience with high-end dress shopping in Dubai has been both exciting and overwhelming. The high-end shopping malls in Dubai, such as Dubai Mall and Mall of Emirates, offer a wide range of designer dresses from renowned brands such as Chanel, Dior, and Gucci. However, the prices can be quite steep. The average price of a designer dress in these malls is around AED 3,000. But if you’re lucky, you might find a good deal during the sale season.

Budget Dress Shopping

When it comes to budget dress shopping, there are several options available in Dubai. One of the most popular options is the Dubai Outlet Mall, where you can find designer dresses at discounted prices. Another option is the Dragon Mart, which offers a wide range of affordable dresses from various brands.

Tips for Dress Shopping in Dubai

If you’re planning to go dress shopping in Dubai, then here are some tips that might come in handy:

- Do your research beforehand and make a list of the stores you want to visit.

- Always check for ongoing sales and discounts.

- Don’t be afraid to negotiate the price, especially in local markets.

- Keep an eye out for the seasonal sales, such as the Dubai Shopping Festival and Dubai Summer Surprises.

How to Find Affordable Dresses in Dubai?

If you’re on a tight budget, then here are some tips on how to find affordable dresses in Dubai:

- Look for local markets such as the Karama Market and the Satwa Market.

- Check out the second-hand stores such as The Closet and Garderobe.

- Look for online shopping websites such as Namshi and Shein that offer affordable dresses.

- Visit the outlet malls such as Dubai Outlet Mall and Ibn Battuta Mall.

Conclusion of Dress Price in Dubai

Shopping for dresses in Dubai can be both exciting and overwhelming. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can find affordable options without compromising on quality. Whether you’re looking for designer dresses or budget options, Dubai has something for everyone. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average price of a designer dress in Dubai?

A: The average price of a designer dress in Dubai is around AED 3,000.

Q: Where can I find affordable dresses in Dubai?

A: You can find affordable dresses in local markets such as the Karama Market and the Satwa Market, second-hand stores such as The Closet and Garderobe, online shopping websites such as Namshi and Shein, and outlet malls such as Dubai Outlet Mall and Ibn Battuta Mall.

Q: Can I negotiate the price of a dress in Dubai?

A: Yes, you can negotiate the price of a dress in local markets and some small stores.

Q: When is the best time to go dress shopping in Dubai?

A: The best time to go dress shopping in Dubai is during the seasonal sales such as the Dubai Shopping Festival and Dubai Summer Surprises.
