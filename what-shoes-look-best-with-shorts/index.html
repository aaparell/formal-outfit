What Shoes Look Best With Shorts A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Shorts are a summer wardrobe staple, but figuring out what shoes to wear with them can be a challenge. You want to look stylish and put-together, but also be comfortable and practical. In this tutorial, we will explore the best shoes to wear with shorts and provide tips on how to style them for any occasion.

When it comes to what shoes look best with shorts, there are a few pain points that many people experience. Some struggle with finding shoes that match the style of their shorts, while others have trouble choosing shoes that are comfortable enough for all-day wear. Additionally, there is the challenge of finding shoes that are appropriate for different occasions, from casual outings to more formal events.

The answer to what shoes look best with shorts depends on several factors, including the style of the shorts, the occasion, and personal preferences. However, there are some general guidelines to follow that can help you choose the perfect pair of shoes.

First and foremost, consider the length and fit of your shorts. Shorter shorts tend to look better with shoes that have a low profile, such as sneakers or sandals. Longer shorts can be paired with a wider range of shoes, including loafers, boat shoes, and even ankle boots.

The Classic Sneaker

For a casual and comfortable look, sneakers are a great choice to wear with shorts. White sneakers are particularly versatile and can be paired with almost any color or style of shorts. If you're feeling bold, try a pair of colorful or patterned sneakers to add some personality to your outfit.

The Stylish Sandal

Sandals are perfect for hot summer days and are a great option for pairing with shorts. For a more casual look, opt for flip-flops or slides. For a more polished look, choose a pair of leather sandals with straps and buckles.

The Versatile Loafer

Loafers are a great option for those who want to dress up their shorts a bit. They are perfect for more formal occasions, such as weddings or business meetings. Choose a pair of loafers in a neutral color, such as brown or black, and pair them with tailored shorts and a button-up shirt for a sophisticated look.

The Trendy Espadrille

For a chic and trendy look, consider wearing espadrilles with your shorts. These shoes are perfect for a day out with friends or a brunch date. Choose a pair in a bright color or pattern to add some flair to your outfit.

My Personal Experience

As someone who loves wearing shorts throughout the summer, I have found that sneakers and sandals are my go-to shoes for pairing with them. I love wearing my white Converse Chuck Taylors with denim shorts and a graphic t-shirt for a casual look. For a more dressed-up occasion, I choose a pair of leather sandals with my tailored shorts and a button-up shirt.

Tips for Choosing the Right Shoes

When choosing what shoes to wear with your shorts, always consider the occasion and your personal style. If you're unsure, it's better to err on the side of caution and choose a more classic shoe style. Additionally, make sure to choose shoes that are comfortable and appropriate for the weather. You don't want to be stuck in hot, uncomfortable shoes on a sunny day!

What Shoes Look Best with Athletic Shorts?

For athletic shorts, sneakers are the best choice. Choose a pair of running shoes or cross-trainers that offer support and comfort for your feet. Additionally, socks are important when wearing athletic shoes, so make sure to choose a pair that is breathable and moisture-wicking.

How to Style Ankle Boots with Shorts?

Ankle boots are a great option for pairing with longer shorts or even denim cutoffs. Choose a pair of boots with a low heel and a neutral color, such as black or brown. Pair them with a simple t-shirt and a denim jacket for a casual yet put-together look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear high heels with shorts?

A: While it's technically possible to wear high heels with shorts, it's not recommended. The combination can look awkward and unbalanced, and it's not very practical for walking around in the summer heat.

Q: Can I wear boots with shorts?

A: Yes, boots can be worn with shorts, but it's important to choose the right style. Ankle boots are a great option, while knee-high boots can look too heavy and bulky with shorts.

Q: Are there any shoes that don't look good with shorts?

A: While everyone has their own personal style, there are some shoe styles that generally don't pair well with shorts, such as high heels or snow boots.

Q: Can I wear dress shoes with shorts?

A: Dress shoes, such as Oxfords or wingtips, can be worn with shorts for a dressier look. However, this combination is best reserved for more formal occasions.

Conclusion of What Shoes Look Best with Shorts

Choosing the right shoes to wear with shorts can seem daunting, but by following these tips and guidelines, you can create a stylish and comfortable outfit for any occasion. Whether you prefer sneakers, sandals, or loafers, there is a perfect shoe out there for you. So go forth, rock your shorts, and step out in style!
