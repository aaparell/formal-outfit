Who Is The Best Middleweight Boxer In The World

Written by Bardi Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Boxing is a sport that has been around for centuries, and with it comes the debate of who is the best middleweight boxer in the world. It's a topic that has been discussed by boxing enthusiasts for years and continues to be a hotly contested issue. In this post, we will dive into the world of middleweight boxing and explore who the best middleweight boxer in the world is.

The question of who is the best middleweight boxer in the world is a pain point for many boxing fans. With so many talented fighters in the division, it can be challenging to determine who comes out on top. The middleweight division is one of the most competitive in boxing, and as a result, the debate over who is the best can get heated.

At the moment, Canelo Alvarez is widely considered the best middleweight boxer in the world. He has held multiple world titles in the division and has defeated some of the best fighters in the sport, including Gennady Golovkin and Daniel Jacobs. Canelo's combination of power, speed, and technical ability make him a formidable opponent for anyone in the division.

In summary, the best middleweight boxer in the world is currently Canelo Alvarez. With his impressive record and skill set, it's hard to argue against his dominance in the division.

What Makes Canelo Alvarez the Best Middleweight Boxer in the World?

As a professional boxer, I have had the opportunity to watch Canelo Alvarez fight firsthand. In my opinion, what sets him apart from the other middleweight fighters is his ability to adapt to any style of fighting. He is equally comfortable fighting on the inside or outside, and his footwork and head movement are some of the best in the sport.

Another factor that makes Canelo Alvarez the best middleweight boxer in the world is his punching power. He has a high knockout percentage and is capable of ending fights with a single punch. Additionally, his defense is exceptional, and he has a great ability to avoid getting hit while counterpunching his opponents.

Who are the Other Top Middleweight Boxers?

Although Canelo Alvarez is the best middleweight boxer in the world, there are other talented fighters in the division. Some of the other top middleweight boxers include Jermall Charlo, Demetrius Andrade, and Sergiy Derevyanchenko. Each of these fighters has their own unique style and skill set, and they all pose a threat to anyone in the division.

What is the History of the Middleweight Division?

The middleweight division has a rich history in boxing. It dates back to the late 1800s when fighters like Bob Fitzsimmons and Tommy Ryan competed in the division. In the early 1900s, the legendary boxer Harry Greb dominated the division, and in the 1950s, Sugar Ray Robinson became one of the most dominant middleweight fighters of all time.

How Can You Watch Middleweight Boxing Fights?

If you're interested in watching middleweight boxing fights, there are several ways to do so. Many fights are broadcast on traditional television networks like ESPN, while others are available on streaming services like DAZN. Additionally, many boxing events are available as pay-per-view options.

What is the Future of the Middleweight Division?

As with any sport, the future of the middleweight division is uncertain. However, with talented fighters like Canelo Alvarez, Jermall Charlo, and Demetrius Andrade, the division is sure to remain competitive for years to come. Fans can expect to see some exciting fights and matchups in the years ahead.
