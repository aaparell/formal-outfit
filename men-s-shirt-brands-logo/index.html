Types of Men's Shirt Brands Logo

There are several different types of men's shirt brands logo, each with its own unique style and message. Some of the most common types include:

Wordmark Logo

A wordmark logo is a logo that consists of the brand name in a specific font or typography. This type of logo is simple and straightforward, and it is often used by brands that want to convey a sense of classic simplicity. Examples include Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger, and Lacoste.

How to Choose a Wordmark Logo

When choosing a wordmark logo, it is important to consider the font and typography. Look for a font that is simple, clean, and easy to read. Avoid fonts that are too fancy or elaborate, as they can be difficult to read and may not convey the right message.

Symbolic Logo

A symbolic logo is a logo that uses an image or symbol to represent the brand. This type of logo is often used by brands that want to convey a sense of uniqueness or creativity. Examples include Lacoste's crocodile, Ralph Lauren's polo player, and Abercrombie & Fitch's moose.

How to Choose a Symbolic Logo

When choosing a symbolic logo, it is important to consider the image or symbol. Look for an image that is unique, memorable, and easily recognizable. Avoid images that are too complex or abstract, as they can be difficult to interpret and may not convey the right message.

Combination Logo
