How to Wear a Saree in Old Style: A Step-by-Step Guide

Are you tired of wearing the same old saree style? Do you want to flaunt your traditional saree in a new way? If so, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of wearing a saree in old style, step-by-step.

Wearing a saree in an old style can be a bit tricky, but with a little practice and patience, you can master it. The key is to follow the steps carefully and take your time. So, let's get started.

To wear a saree in old style, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the right saree

The first step in wearing a saree in old style is to choose the right saree. Opt for a saree with a traditional print or embroidery. It should have a length of around 5.5 meters and a width of 1.2 meters.

Step 2: Drape the saree

Start by draping the saree around your waist, with the plain end of the saree on your left side. Tuck it in at the waist, and make one complete round.

Step 3: Make pleats

Next, make pleats of around 5-7 inches in width. Ensure that the pleats are facing towards the left side. Tuck the pleats into your waistband, making sure they are neat and straight.

Step 4: Drape the pallu

Now, take the pallu and drape it over your left shoulder. Make sure the pallu is long enough to reach your knees. Pin the pallu to your blouse on your left shoulder.

Step 5: Accessorize

Accessorize your saree with traditional jewelry, such as bangles, earrings, and a necklace. Opt for a bindi on your forehead to complete the look.

By following these steps, you can wear a saree in old style and look stunning. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to try it a few times before wearing it for a special occasion.
