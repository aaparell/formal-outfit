Discovering The Most Popular Jean Brands Of 2020

Written by Oliver Jul 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Jeans have always been a timeless fashion staple, and in 2020, there are countless brands to choose from. With so many options, it can be overwhelming to determine which brand is right for you. Whether you're on the hunt for the perfect skinny jeans or a comfortable pair of boyfriend jeans, this article will help you navigate the most popular jean brands of 2020.

When it comes to choosing the right jean brand, it's important to consider factors such as the fit, material, and style. Additionally, you'll want to take into account the brand's reputation, as well as any ethical or sustainable practices they may have. With these factors in mind, let's dive into the most popular jean brands of 2020.

Levi's

Levi's is a classic jean brand that has been around since 1853. They are known for their high-quality denim, classic styles, and wide range of sizes. Levi's offers everything from skinny jeans to boyfriend jeans, and they have a reputation for being durable and long-lasting.

Personally, I love Levi's because they have a wide range of fits and styles that cater to different body types. Their high-waisted skinny jeans are my go-to, and I appreciate that they offer sustainable options such as their Wellthread collection.

Wrangler

Wrangler is another classic jean brand that has been around for over 70 years. They are known for their rugged and durable jeans, which are perfect for those who work outdoors or need a pair of jeans that can withstand wear and tear.

I recently purchased a pair of Wrangler's high-waisted straight leg jeans, and I was pleasantly surprised by how comfortable and flattering they were. I appreciate that Wrangler offers a range of sizes and fits, and they also have a sustainable denim collection.

Lucky Brand

Lucky Brand is a popular jean brand that offers a range of styles, from classic bootcut jeans to trendy distressed jeans. They are known for their comfortable and soft denim, which is perfect for everyday wear.

Personally, I love Lucky Brand because their jeans are incredibly comfortable and flattering. Their Lolita skinny jeans are one of my favorites, and I appreciate that they offer a variety of inseam lengths for those who are shorter or taller.

Agolde

Agolde is a newer jean brand that has quickly gained popularity due to their trendy styles and sustainable practices. They offer a range of styles, from high-waisted straight leg jeans to cropped flare jeans.

I recently purchased a pair of Agolde's Riley high-rise straight leg jeans, and I love how comfortable and stylish they are. I appreciate that Agolde uses sustainable materials and ethical labor practices, making them a great choice for those who prioritize sustainability.

Question and Answer

Q: What are the most popular jean styles in 2020?

A: Some of the most popular jean styles in 2020 include high-waisted skinny jeans, wide-leg jeans, and distressed jeans.

Q: Are there any sustainable jean brands?

A: Yes, many jean brands are now offering sustainable options, such as Levi's Wellthread collection and Agolde's use of sustainable materials and ethical labor practices.

Q: What should I consider when choosing a jean brand?

A: When choosing a jean brand, consider factors such as the fit, material, style, and brand reputation. Additionally, you may want to take into account any ethical or sustainable practices the brand may have.

Q: Are there any jean brands that offer a wide range of sizes?

A: Yes, many jean brands now offer a wide range of sizes, including Levi's and Lucky Brand.

Conclusion of Most Popular Jean Brands 2020

When it comes to choosing the right jean brand, there are countless options to choose from. Whether you prefer classic styles or trendy options, it's important to consider factors such as the fit, material, and brand reputation. Additionally, you may want to prioritize sustainable and ethical practices when selecting a jean brand. By keeping these factors in mind and exploring the most popular jean brands of 2020, you're sure to find the perfect pair of jeans for your wardrobe.
