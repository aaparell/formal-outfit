Most Expensive Disney Cars Diecast: Lightning McQueen (Gold)

One of the most expensive Disney Cars diecast models is Lightning McQueen (Gold). This model was launched in 2013 at the Tokyo Toy Show and was limited to only 10 pieces worldwide. The gold-plated Lightning McQueen diecast model comes in a special display case with a certificate of authenticity. The price of this model is around $10,000.

I had the opportunity to witness this rare and exclusive diecast model at a Disney Cars exhibition. The model was displayed in a glass case, and its shiny gold finish caught everyone's attention. The attention to detail in the design and the exclusivity of the model make it worth its high price tag.

Francesco Bernoulli (Platinum) is another popular Disney Cars diecast model that is expensive and exclusive. This model was released in 2013 at the Tokyo Toy Show and was limited to only 10 pieces worldwide. The exclusivity and rarity of these Disney Cars diecast models are the main reasons why they are so expensive. Limited to only 10 pieces worldwide, these models are considered a rare find and are highly sought after by diecast collectors. Adding these expensive and exclusive Disney Cars diecast models to your collection can be a challenge. The limited number of pieces and high demand make them difficult to find. However, you can keep an eye out for auctions, online marketplaces, and conventions to find these models. It is also essential to be prepared to pay a high price for these models if you find them.

Most Expensive Disney Cars Diecast: Cars 2 London Chase Set

The Cars 2 London Chase Set is another expensive Disney Cars diecast model that was released in 2011. This set comes with five diecast models, including Lightning McQueen, Finn McMissile, Holley Shiftwell, and two Lemons. The set was limited to only 1000 pieces worldwide and comes in a special collector's case. The price of this set is around $1,200.

As a Disney Cars collector, I was thrilled to add this set to my collection. The set's unique design and the inclusion of five diecast models make it a valuable addition to any collection.

Cars 2 Acer with Torch and Screen is another expensive Disney Cars diecast model that was released in 2011. This model comes with a torch and screen that projects images of Acer on any surface.
