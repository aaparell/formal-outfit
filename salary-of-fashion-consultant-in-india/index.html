Salary Of Fashion Consultant In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career as a fashion consultant in India but unsure about the salary prospects? Look no further, as this article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the salary of fashion consultants in India.

As a fashion consultant, it's important to have a clear understanding of the industry and be able to provide clients with expert advice on fashion trends, styling, and personal shopping. However, the salary of a fashion consultant in India may vary depending on several factors such as experience, location, and type of employer.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a fashion consultant in India is ₹4,46,162 per year. However, this figure is subject to change based on various factors.

Salary of Fashion Consultant in India: Factors Affecting

Experience plays a crucial role in determining the salary of a fashion consultant in India. Generally, entry-level fashion consultants can earn between ₹2,00,000 to ₹3,50,000 annually, while those with more experience can earn up to ₹8,00,000 per year.

Location is another important factor that can impact the salary of a fashion consultant. Fashion consultants working in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to earn higher salaries compared to those working in smaller cities or towns.

The type of employer also plays a role in determining the salary of a fashion consultant. Freelance fashion consultants may earn higher salaries than those working for fashion brands or retail stores. Additionally, those working for high-end fashion brands or luxury retail stores may also earn higher salaries compared to those working for mass-market brands.

My Personal Experience

As a fashion consultant with 5 years of experience working in Mumbai, I earn an annual salary of ₹6,50,000. My work primarily involves providing styling advice to clients and personal shopping. While the salary is not as high as some other professions, I find my work to be fulfilling and enjoyable.

Skills Required for a Fashion Consultant

As a fashion consultant, it's important to have a good eye for fashion, be well-versed in current fashion trends, and have excellent communication skills. Additionally, strong interpersonal skills and the ability to build relationships with clients is also crucial in this profession. A degree in fashion design or a related field can also be helpful in pursuing a career as a fashion consultant.

How to Become a Fashion Consultant

To become a fashion consultant in India, it's important to have a degree in fashion design or a related field. Additionally, gaining practical experience through internships or working in the fashion industry can also be helpful. Building a portfolio of work and networking with industry professionals can also increase your chances of getting hired as a fashion consultant.

Is the Salary of a Fashion Consultant in India Worth it?

Ultimately, the decision to pursue a career as a fashion consultant in India should not be solely based on the salary prospects. While the salary may not be as high compared to some other professions, the creative and fulfilling nature of the job can make it a worthwhile career choice.
