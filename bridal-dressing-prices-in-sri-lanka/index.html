Bridal Dressing Prices In Sri Lanka What You Need To Know

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you getting married soon and wondering about the cost of bridal dressing in Sri Lanka? You’re not alone. Many brides-to-be in Sri Lanka face the challenge of finding the perfect outfit for their big day while staying within their budget. In this article, we’ll provide you with everything you need to know about bridal dressing prices in Sri Lanka so you can make an informed decision.

The average cost of bridal dressing in Sri Lanka can range from LKR 50,000 to LKR 500,000, depending on a variety of factors such as the designer, materials used, and the complexity of the design. This can be a major pain point for many brides who are on a tight budget but still want to look their best on their special day.

So, how can you find affordable bridal dressing options in Sri Lanka without compromising on quality? Let’s take a look.

To find affordable bridal dressing options in Sri Lanka, it’s important to do your research and plan ahead. Start by looking for designers who offer budget-friendly options or discounts during certain times of the year. You can also consider renting your bridal outfit instead of buying it outright to save money. Additionally, you can opt for simpler designs or choose less expensive materials to keep costs down.

Bridal Dressing Packages

Many bridal salons in Sri Lanka offer bridal dressing packages that include everything from the dress and accessories to hair and makeup. These packages can be a great way to save money and ensure that everything is taken care of on your big day. However, it’s important to read the fine print and make sure you understand exactly what is included in the package before committing.

Personal Experience

When I was planning my wedding, I was on a tight budget but still wanted to look beautiful on my big day. I did a lot of research and found a designer who offered affordable bridal dressing options. I opted for a simpler design and chose less expensive materials, but the end result was still stunning. Additionally, I found a bridal salon that offered a package deal that included everything from the dress and accessories to hair and makeup. This saved me a lot of money and made the process much less stressful.

Online Shopping

In recent years, online shopping has become increasingly popular in Sri Lanka. This includes online shopping for bridal outfits. While shopping online can be convenient and offer a wider range of options, it’s important to be cautious and do your research. Make sure you read reviews and check the return policy before making a purchase. Additionally, keep in mind that the color and quality of the outfit may look different in person than it does online.

Designer vs Non-Designer

One major factor that can affect the cost of bridal dressing in Sri Lanka is whether you opt for a designer outfit or a non-designer outfit. Designer outfits are typically more expensive but can offer higher quality materials and more intricate designs. Non-designer outfits can be more affordable but may not be as unique or high-quality as designer outfits. Ultimately, it’s up to you to decide which option is best for you based on your budget and personal preferences.

Negotiating Prices

If you find a designer or bridal salon that you love but their prices are outside of your budget, don’t be afraid to negotiate. Many designers and salons are willing to work with brides to find a price that works for both parties. Additionally, you can consider making payments in installments leading up to the wedding to make the cost more manageable.

Personal Experience

When I found the perfect designer for my bridal outfit, the price was slightly above my budget. However, I decided to reach out and see if there was any room for negotiation. After a few back-and-forth conversations, we were able to agree on a price that worked for both of us. This taught me that it never hurts to ask and that many designers are willing to work with brides to find a solution that works for everyone.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I find affordable bridal dressing options in Sri Lanka?

A: Start by doing your research and looking for designers who offer budget-friendly options, renting your outfit instead of buying it outright, and considering simpler designs or less expensive materials.

Q: Should I opt for a designer or non-designer outfit?

A: It depends on your budget and personal preferences. Designer outfits are typically more expensive but can offer higher quality materials and more intricate designs. Non-designer outfits can be more affordable but may not be as unique or high-quality as designer outfits.

Q: Can I negotiate prices with designers or bridal salons?

A: Yes! Many designers and salons are willing to work with brides to find a price that works for both parties. Additionally, you can consider making payments in installments leading up to the wedding to make the cost more manageable.

Q: Is it safe to shop for bridal outfits online in Sri Lanka?

A: While online shopping can be convenient and offer a wider range of options, it’s important to be cautious and do your research. Make sure you read reviews and check the return policy before making a purchase. Additionally, keep in mind that the color and quality of the outfit may look different in person than it does online.

Conclusion of Bridal Dressing Prices in Sri Lanka

While the cost of bridal dressing in Sri Lanka can be daunting, there are many ways to find affordable options without sacrificing quality. By doing your research, planning ahead, and being open to negotiation, you can find the perfect bridal outfit for your big day that fits within your budget.
