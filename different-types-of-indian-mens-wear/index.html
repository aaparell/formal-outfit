Different Types Of Indian Mens Wear A Guide To Traditional Attire

Written by Robby May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

India is a country known for its rich culture, traditions, and diversity. Indian clothing is no exception, with a wide range of styles and designs that vary from region to region. For men, traditional Indian wear is not only a way to showcase their heritage but also a reflection of their personality and style. In this guide, we will explore different types of Indian mens wear and their significance.

Choosing the right traditional Indian attire can be daunting, especially for those who are not familiar with the culture or have never worn Indian clothing before. It can be challenging to navigate the various styles, fabrics, and colors, and to decide which outfit is appropriate for which occasion. Additionally, finding the perfect fit can be a struggle, as Indian clothing is typically tailored to fit loose and flowy.

The target of this guide is to provide an overview of different types of Indian mens wear, including its history, significance, and how to wear them. We will cover everything from the classic kurta and pajama to the royal sherwani and the sophisticated bandhgala.

In summary, this guide will serve as a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in traditional Indian mens wear. We will explore the various styles, fabrics, and designs available, and provide tips on how to choose the right outfit for any occasion.

Kurta and Pajama

The kurta is a long, loose-fitting shirt that falls below the knee and is typically worn with a pair of pajamas. It is one of the most popular types of Indian mens wear, and it is suitable for both formal and informal occasions. The kurta and pajama are made of cotton, silk, or linen, and are available in a range of colors and designs. The kurta can be plain or embroidered, and it is often paired with a dupatta or a shawl.

As an Indian, I have worn the kurta and pajama on many occasions, from weddings to family gatherings. I find them comfortable, versatile, and elegant. They are perfect for hot summer days, as the lightweight fabric allows air to circulate, keeping me cool and comfortable.

Sherwani

The sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is typically worn over a kurta and pajama. It is made of silk or brocade and is often heavily embroidered with gold or silver thread. The sherwani is a popular choice for formal occasions such as weddings, receptions, and other special events. It is considered to be one of the most elegant types of Indian mens wear, and it is often worn by grooms on their wedding day.

I wore a sherwani on my wedding day, and it made me feel like a king. The intricate embroidery and the luxurious fabric made me stand out from the crowd, and I received many compliments on my outfit.

Bandhgala

The bandhgala, also known as the Jodhpuri suit, is a formal suit that originated in the Jodhpur region of Rajasthan. It consists of a jacket and trousers, both made of the same fabric. The jacket has a high collar and is typically buttoned up to the neck. The bandhgala is a popular choice for weddings and other formal occasions, and it is often worn by politicians and other high-ranking officials.

The bandhgala is my go-to outfit for formal events. I love the sophisticated look and feel of the suit, and I always receive compliments when I wear it.

Dhoti and Kurta

The dhoti is a long piece of cloth that is wrapped around the waist and legs, similar to a skirt. It is typically worn with a kurta and is considered to be one of the oldest types of Indian mens wear. The dhoti and kurta are traditionally worn for religious ceremonies and other cultural events. The dhoti is made of cotton or silk and is available in a range of colors and designs.

While I have never worn a dhoti and kurta myself, I have seen many of my family members and friends wear them for religious ceremonies. They look elegant and traditional, and they add a touch of authenticity to any cultural event.

Question and Answer Section:

Q: What is the significance of traditional Indian mens wear?

A: Traditional Indian mens wear is a reflection of the country's rich culture and heritage. It is a way for men to showcase their identity and personality and to pay homage to their ancestors. Indian clothing is also practical, comfortable, and versatile, making it suitable for a range of occasions.

Q: How do I choose the right type of Indian mens wear for an occasion?

A: The type of Indian mens wear you choose depends on the occasion, the time of day, and your personal style. For formal occasions such as weddings, receptions, and other special events, a sherwani or a bandhgala is a good choice. For more casual events, a kurta and pajama or a dhoti and kurta are suitable. When choosing your outfit, consider the color, fabric, and design, and make sure it fits you well.

Q: How do I care for my Indian mens wear?

A: Indian clothing is typically made of delicate fabrics such as silk, cotton, and linen, and it requires special care. Always check the care label before washing your outfit, and follow the instructions carefully. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, and never wring or twist your clothing. Instead, hang it to dry or lay it flat on a clean surface. Iron your outfit on a low heat setting, and store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Q: Can I wear traditional Indian mens wear if I am not Indian?

A: Of course! Traditional Indian mens wear is not exclusive to Indians and can be worn by anyone who appreciates the culture and style. However, it is essential to respect the traditions and customs associated with the clothing and to wear it appropriately.

Conclusion of Different Types of Indian Mens Wear:

Indian clothing is a unique and beautiful expression of the country's rich culture and heritage. Different types of Indian mens wear offer a range of styles, colors, and designs, and they are suitable for a variety of occasions. Whether you are attending a wedding, a religious ceremony, or a cultural event, there is an Indian outfit that will make you look and feel your best. By following the tips in this guide, you can choose the right Indian mens wear for any occasion and showcase your personality and style.
