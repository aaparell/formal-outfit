How Much Does It Cost To Rent Bridesmaid Dresses

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 · 5 min read

As a bride, you want your bridesmaids to look beautiful and feel comfortable on your big day. However, the cost of bridesmaid dresses can add up quickly, leaving your bridal party in a financial bind. Renting bridesmaid dresses has become a popular alternative to buying, but how much does it cost to rent bridesmaid dresses?

The cost of bridesmaid dresses can be a pain point for many bridesmaids. They want to look beautiful on the wedding day but may not have the budget to purchase a dress they will only wear once. Additionally, bridesmaids may have to pay for alterations or accessories to complete the look, adding even more costs to the dress.

Renting bridesmaid dresses can be a cost-effective solution for both the bride and her bridesmaids. The cost to rent a bridesmaid dress can range from $50 to $200, depending on the designer, style, and rental company. This cost typically includes the rental of the dress for a few days and dry cleaning after use.

In summary, renting bridesmaid dresses can be a cost-saving option for brides and their bridal parties. The cost to rent a dress varies depending on the designer, style, and rental company, but can range from $50 to $200.

Why Renting Bridesmaid Dresses is a Great Option

When I was a bridesmaid in my friend's wedding, I was hesitant to spend a lot of money on a dress I would only wear once. Luckily, the bride suggested we rent our dresses, which saved me a lot of money. I was able to find a beautiful dress that fit me well and didn't break the bank.

Renting bridesmaid dresses is a great option because it allows bridesmaids to wear designer dresses they may not have been able to afford otherwise. It also saves them money, as they don't have to spend a lot of money on a dress they will only wear once. Additionally, rental companies often offer a wide variety of styles and sizes, making it easier for bridesmaids to find a dress that fits them well.

Where to Rent Bridesmaid Dresses

There are several rental companies that specialize in bridesmaid dresses, such as Rent the Runway, Vow to be Chic, and Union Station. These companies offer a wide range of designer dresses in various styles and sizes. Some bridal shops also offer rental options for bridesmaid dresses.

Factors That Affect the Cost of Renting Bridesmaid Dresses

The cost to rent a bridesmaid dress can vary depending on several factors, including the designer, style, and rental company. Designer dresses will typically cost more to rent than non-designer dresses. Additionally, dresses with intricate beading or embroidery may also cost more to rent. The rental company you choose may also affect the cost, as some companies may charge more for shipping or dry cleaning.

Tips for Renting Bridesmaid Dresses

When renting bridesmaid dresses, it's important to read the rental agreement carefully. Make sure you understand the rental period, return policy, and any fees or penalties for late returns or damages. It's also a good idea to order your dress well in advance of the wedding date to ensure it arrives on time and fits properly. Finally, be sure to try on the dress as soon as it arrives to ensure it fits properly and any necessary alterations can be made in a timely manner.
