How Much Does A Game Designer Make In The Uk

Written by Daniel Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're interested in pursuing a career in game design, you're probably wondering how much you can expect to make in the UK. The video game industry is growing rapidly, and game designers are in high demand. However, there are a few things you should know before you start your job search.

According to the National Careers Service, the starting salary for a junior game designer in the UK is around £18,000 to £22,000 per year. However, experienced game designers can earn upwards of £50,000 per year.

In order to earn a higher salary, you will need to gain experience in the industry and develop your skills. This can be done by working on your own game projects, participating in game jams, and networking with industry professionals.

In summary, game designers in the UK can expect to make anywhere from £18,000 to £50,000 per year, depending on their level of experience.

How to Increase Your Salary as a Game Designer in the UK

One way to increase your salary as a game designer in the UK is to gain experience in the industry. This can be done by working on your own game projects and participating in game jams. It's also important to network with industry professionals and attend events such as conferences and meetups.

Another way to increase your salary is to specialize in a particular area of game design. For example, if you become an expert in user interface design, you may be able to command a higher salary than a generalist game designer.

The Importance of Negotiating Your Salary

When it comes to negotiating your salary as a game designer in the UK, it's important to do your research and know your worth. You should have a clear understanding of the average salary for game designers in your area, as well as your level of experience and skills. Don't be afraid to negotiate for a higher salary if you feel you are being undervalued.

Factors That Affect Game Designer Salaries in the UK

There are several factors that can affect game designer salaries in the UK. These include:

  • Level of experience
  • Specialization
  • Type of employer (e.g. indie studio vs. AAA studio)
  • Location

How to Find Game Designer Jobs in the UK

If you're looking for a game designer job in the UK, there are several ways to go about it. You can search online job boards, such as Indeed and Glassdoor, or check out the careers pages of game studios you are interested in. You can also attend industry events and network with professionals in the industry.

Conclusion

In conclusion, game designers in the UK can expect to make anywhere from £18,000 to £50,000 per year, depending on their level of experience and specialization. There are several ways to increase your salary as a game designer, including gaining experience in the industry, specializing in a particular area, and negotiating your salary. By doing your research and networking with professionals in the industry, you can increase your chances of landing a high-paying game designer job in the UK.

A. The average salary for a junior game designer in the UK is around £18,000 to £22,000 per year.
