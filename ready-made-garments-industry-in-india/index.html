Ready Made Garments Industry In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is known for its diverse culture and traditions, and the clothing industry is no exception. The Ready Made Garments (RMG) industry in India is a significant contributor to the country's economy, providing employment to millions of people across the country. From small-scale businesses to large corporations, the RMG industry in India caters to both the domestic and international market. In this blog post, we will explore the ins and outs of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India and its impact on the economy.

What are the pain points related to the Ready Made Garments Industry in India?

Although the Ready Made Garments Industry in India has been thriving for decades, it is not without its challenges. One of the primary issues faced by the industry is the lack of skilled labor. Many workers are untrained, leading to a compromise in the quality of the final product. Additionally, the industry is highly fragmented, with a large number of small businesses operating in the market. This results in a lack of standardization and quality control, leading to inconsistent product quality.

What is the target of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India?

The target of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India is vast. The industry caters to a diverse set of customers, ranging from the lower-income group to the elite class. With the increasing purchasing power of the middle class, there has been a surge in demand for affordable and trendy clothing. The industry also caters to the global market, with exports accounting for a significant portion of the revenue generated.

The Impact of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India

The Ready Made Garments Industry in India is a significant contributor to the country's economy, accounting for approximately 5% of the GDP. The industry provides employment to over 45 million people, making it the second-largest employer in the country after agriculture. Additionally, the industry has helped put India on the global map, with its exports accounting for a significant portion of the country's foreign exchange earnings.

The Evolution of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India

The Ready Made Garments Industry in India has come a long way since its inception. The industry started as a small-scale business, catering to the local market. However, with the liberalization of the economy in the 1990s, the industry saw significant growth, with the entry of international brands and the establishment of large corporations. The industry has since evolved to cater to the changing needs of the market, with a focus on sustainability and ethical business practices.

How to Start a Ready Made Garments Business in India?

Starting a Ready Made Garments Business in India can be a lucrative venture. However, it requires careful planning and execution. Here are some steps to help you get started:

  1. Identify your target market and niche
  2. Create a business plan
  3. Register your business
  4. Secure funding
  5. Source raw materials and machinery
  6. Hire skilled labor
  7. Establish a distribution network
  8. Focus on quality control and standardization

The Future of the Ready Made Garments Industry in India

The Ready Made Garments Industry in India is poised for significant growth in the coming years. With the increasing demand for sustainable and ethical clothing, the industry is focusing on adopting eco-friendly practices and reducing its carbon footprint. Additionally, with the advent of technology, there has been a significant shift towards e-commerce, providing businesses with new opportunities to reach a wider audience.
