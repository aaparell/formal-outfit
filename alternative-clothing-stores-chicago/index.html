Discovering Alternative Clothing Stores In Chicago

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever struggled to find unique and edgy clothing in Chicago? Do you feel like mainstream fashion just doesn't express your individuality? If so, you're not alone. Many people are seeking alternative clothing stores in Chicago that offer a wide range of styles that reflect their personality and taste.

Alternative clothing stores in Chicago cater to those who want to express themselves through fashion. They offer a diverse range of styles that include punk, goth, vintage, and streetwear. But finding these stores can be difficult, especially if you're new to the area or haven't explored the city's fashion scene before.

If you're looking for alternative clothing stores in Chicago, you're in luck. This guide will help you discover the best places to shop for unique and edgy fashion in the city.

Exploring Wicker Park/Bucktown

Wicker Park and Bucktown are two neighborhoods in Chicago that are known for their alternative fashion scene. These areas are home to a variety of independent boutiques and vintage stores that offer one-of-a-kind pieces.

One of the most popular stores in this area is Kokorokoko Vintage. They offer a wide range of vintage clothing from the 80s and 90s that will transport you back in time. Another great option is Shuga Records, which is a record store that also sells vintage clothing and accessories.

Checking Out Logan Square

Logan Square is another neighborhood in Chicago that is worth exploring if you're looking for alternative clothing stores. This area is known for its independent boutiques and thrift stores that offer unique and affordable fashion.

One of the most popular stores in Logan Square is Knee Deep Vintage. They offer a wide range of vintage clothing from the 1920s to the 1990s. Another great option is Wolfbait & B-girls, which is a boutique that features local designers and artists.

Exploring the North Side

If you're looking for alternative clothing stores in the North Side, you'll find a variety of options in neighborhoods like Andersonville and Uptown. These areas are home to a mix of independent boutiques and vintage stores that offer unique and edgy fashion.

One of the most popular stores in Andersonville is The Brown Elephant Resale Shop. They offer a wide range of vintage and contemporary clothing, as well as furniture and home decor. Another great option is Strange Cargo, which is a store that specializes in custom t-shirts and accessories.

Shopping Online

Shopping for alternative clothing stores in Chicago doesn't have to be limited to physical stores. Many independent boutiques and vintage stores have online shops that offer the same unique and edgy fashion. Some popular options include Depop, Etsy, and ASOS Marketplace.

Question and Answer

Q: What kind of styles can I find at alternative clothing stores in Chicago?

A: Alternative clothing stores in Chicago offer a wide range of styles, including punk, goth, vintage, and streetwear.

Q: Where can I find alternative clothing stores in Chicago?

A: Some of the best neighborhoods to explore for alternative clothing stores in Chicago include Wicker Park/Bucktown, Logan Square, and Andersonville/Uptown.

Q: Do alternative clothing stores in Chicago offer affordable fashion?

A: Yes, many independent boutiques and thrift stores in Chicago offer affordable fashion options.

Q: Can I shop for alternative clothing stores in Chicago online?

A: Yes, many independent boutiques and vintage stores in Chicago have online shops that offer the same unique and edgy fashion.

Conclusion of alternative clothing stores chicago

Whether you're a local or just visiting, Chicago has a lot to offer when it comes to alternative fashion. By exploring neighborhoods like Wicker Park/Bucktown, Logan Square, and Andersonville/Uptown, you can discover a wide range of independent boutiques and vintage stores that offer unique and edgy fashion. And if you can't make it to physical stores, many of these stores have online shops that offer the same unique and edgy fashion.
