What To Wear To A Wedding As A Male Guest

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Weddings are joyous occasions where you get to celebrate the union of two people in love. However, for male guests, figuring out what to wear can be a daunting task. You want to look good, but you also don't want to outshine the groom or break any dress codes. In this tutorial, we will guide you on what to wear to a wedding as a male guest.

The pressure to dress appropriately for a wedding can be overwhelming. You don't want to be underdressed or overdressed, and you also don't want to wear the wrong colors or fabrics. It's essential to consider the time of day, location, and dress code specified on the invitation.

First and foremost, the most crucial factor to consider when choosing what to wear to a wedding as a male guest is the dress code. The dress code determines the formality of the event and what attire is appropriate. The most common wedding dress codes are black-tie, semi-formal, and casual.

In summary, when deciding what to wear to a wedding as a male guest, always consider the dress code, location, and time of the event. Choose a suit or blazer in a neutral color, pair it with dress shoes, and accessorize with a tie or pocket square. Avoid wearing white or anything too flashy, and ensure your clothing fits well.

What to Wear to a Black-Tie Wedding

Attending a black-tie wedding calls for the most formal attire. A tuxedo is the most appropriate outfit, paired with a bow tie, black dress shoes, and a formal dress shirt. It's essential to stick to a classic look, avoid loud colors or patterns, and ensure your tuxedo fits well.

What to Wear to a Semi-Formal Wedding

A semi-formal wedding is less formal than a black-tie event but still requires dressy attire. A suit in a neutral color such as navy or charcoal is appropriate, paired with dress shoes and a tie. You can also opt for a blazer with dress pants and a dress shirt.

What to Wear to a Beach Wedding

A beach wedding is usually more casual, and you can opt for lighter fabrics and colors. A linen suit in a light color such as beige or light blue is appropriate, paired with loafers or sandals. Avoid wearing a tie and opt for a dress shirt with rolled-up sleeves.

What to Wear to a Summer Wedding

Summer weddings call for lighter fabrics and colors. Choose a suit in a light color such as khaki or light grey, paired with dress shoes and a tie. You can also opt for a blazer with dress pants and a dress shirt. Avoid wearing dark colors and heavy fabrics.

Accessorizing for a Wedding

Accessorizing is an important aspect of dressing for a wedding. A tie or bow tie is a must for formal events, and you can opt for a pocket square to add some flair. Ensure your belt and shoes match, and avoid wearing too many accessories.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear jeans to a wedding?

A: No, jeans are not appropriate for a wedding, regardless of the dress code. Opt for dress pants or a suit.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to a wedding?

A: Sneakers are not appropriate for a formal or semi-formal wedding. Stick to dress shoes.

Q: Can I wear a white shirt to a wedding?

A: It's best to avoid white clothing as it's traditionally reserved for the bride and groom. Opt for neutral colors such as black, navy, or grey.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing a hat to a wedding unless it's part of the dress code or the venue dictates it.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Wedding as a Male Guest

Attending a wedding as a male guest can be stressful, but it doesn't have to be. Always consider the dress code, location, and time of the event when selecting your outfit. Opt for a classic, well-fitting suit or blazer, paired with dress shoes and accessories. Avoid wearing white or anything too flashy, and ensure your clothing fits well. With these tips, you'll be sure to look sharp and appropriate for any wedding.
