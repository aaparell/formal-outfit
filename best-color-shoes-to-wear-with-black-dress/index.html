Best Color Shoes To Wear With Black Dress A Guide

Written by William Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to dressing up for a special occasion, a black dress is a classic choice. But choosing the right color of shoes to wear with it can be a challenge. You want to look stylish and put-together, but you also don't want to clash or look too matchy-matchy. In this guide, we'll explore the best color shoes to wear with a black dress so that you can make a statement without sacrificing your style.

Whether you're attending a wedding, a cocktail party, or a formal event, pairing your black dress with the right shoes can make all the difference in your overall look. The wrong color of shoes can make your outfit look uncoordinated or clash with your dress, while the right color can complement your dress and bring your entire look together.

So, what are the best color shoes to wear with a black dress? The answer depends on the occasion and your personal style. But don't worry, we've got you covered. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect color of shoes for your black dress.

To summarize, the best color shoes to wear with a black dress are those that complement your dress and don't clash with it. Neutral colors like nude, beige, and metallics are safe choices that will go with any style of black dress. However, if you want to make a statement, you can also choose bold colors like red, blue, or green. Just make sure to keep your accessories and makeup simple to balance out your look.

Neutral Colors: The Classic Choice

When in doubt, go for neutral colors. Neutral shades like nude, beige, and metallics are timeless and versatile, and they complement any style of black dress. Nude shoes, in particular, are a popular choice for a reason: they elongate your legs and create the illusion of height, making you look taller and slimmer. Beige shoes are also a great option, especially if you want to create a monochromatic look or add a pop of color to your outfit. Metallic shoes are another classic choice that can add some sparkle and shine to your look.

Personally, I love wearing nude pumps with my black dress. They make my legs look longer and slimmer, and they go with any style of dress. I also like to add some gold accessories to my outfit to complement my metallic shoes.

Bold Colors: Make a Statement

If you want to stand out from the crowd and make a statement, bold colors are the way to go. Red shoes, for example, add a pop of color to your outfit and make a bold statement. Blue shoes can add a touch of elegance and sophistication, while green shoes can add some fun and quirkiness to your look. Just make sure to choose a shade that complements your dress and doesn't clash with it.

For a recent wedding I attended, I wore a black dress with red pumps. It was a bold choice, but it paid off: I received so many compliments on my outfit, and I felt confident and stylish all night long.

Texture and Material: A Factor to Consider

When choosing the color of your shoes, it's also important to consider the texture and material. Patent leather shoes, for example, are a popular choice for formal events because they add some shine and polish to your outfit. Suede shoes, on the other hand, can add some texture and depth to your look.

Personally, I like to wear suede shoes with my black dress to add some texture and interest. I also love pairing my dress with velvet shoes for a luxurious and elegant look.

How to Choose the Right Color Shoes for Your Black Dress

When choosing the right color of shoes for your black dress, consider the occasion, your personal style, and the texture and material of your shoes. Neutral colors like nude, beige, and metallics are classic choices that will never go out of style, while bold colors like red, blue, and green can add some personality and flair to your outfit. Just remember to balance out your look with simple accessories and makeup.

FAQs: Best Color Shoes to Wear with Black Dress

Q: Can you wear black shoes with a black dress?

A: While you can technically wear black shoes with a black dress, it's not the best choice. Black shoes can be too matchy-matchy with your dress and make your outfit look uncoordinated. It's better to choose a color that complements your dress instead.

Q: Can you wear white shoes with a black dress?

A: Yes, you can wear white shoes with a black dress, but it can be a tricky look to pull off. White shoes can create a high-contrast look that can be overwhelming, so it's better to choose a more muted color like nude or metallics.

Q: What color shoes go with a little black dress?

A: The best color shoes to wear with a little black dress are neutral shades like nude, beige, and metallics. Bold colors like red, blue, and green can also work, depending on the occasion and your personal style.

Q: Can you wear colorful shoes with a black dress?

A: Yes, you can wear colorful shoes with a black dress, but make sure to choose a color that complements your dress and doesn't clash with it. Balance out your look with simple accessories and makeup to avoid looking too over-the-top.

Conclusion of Best Color Shoes to Wear with Black Dress

Choosing the right color of shoes to wear with your black dress can be a challenge, but with these tips, you can create a stylish and put-together look that will turn heads. Whether you choose neutral shades like nude or metallics or bold colors like red or blue, make sure to balance out your look with simple accessories and makeup. With the right pair of shoes, you can take your black dress from basic to beautiful.
