Discover The Best Nordstrom Dress Shirt Brands For Men

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect dress shirt? Finding the right fit, style, and quality can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to Nordstrom dress shirt brands. But fear not! We've got you covered with this guide to the best Nordstrom dress shirt brands for men.

When it comes to dress shirts, there are a few pain points that many men experience. These can include finding the right fit for your body type, selecting the right fabric for the occasion, and choosing a brand that offers both style and quality.

The goal of Nordstrom dress shirt brands is to provide men with a variety of options that meet their needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for a classic, tailored fit or a more modern, slim fit, Nordstrom has a dress shirt brand that can fulfill your needs.

In summary, this article will explore the different Nordstrom dress shirt brands and their target audience. We'll also share personal experiences and provide information on the best fabrics, fits, and styles for each brand.

1. Nordstrom Men's Shop Dress Shirts

Target: Classic, traditional men who prefer a timeless look.

As someone who has purchased multiple Nordstrom Men's Shop dress shirts, I can attest to their quality and style. These dress shirts are made with 100% cotton and come in a variety of classic colors and patterns. The fit is true to size and the shirts are easy to care for.

2. Robert Graham Dress Shirts

Target: Men who prefer unique, bold patterns and colors.

I discovered Robert Graham dress shirts a few years ago and have been a fan ever since. These dress shirts are made with high-quality fabrics and feature intricate designs and bold colors. They're perfect for making a statement at a special event or adding some personality to your work wardrobe.

Best Fabrics for Dress Shirts

When it comes to dress shirts, the fabric is just as important as the fit and style. Here are some of the best fabrics to look for:

- Cotton: This is the most common fabric used for dress shirts. It's breathable, easy to care for, and comes in a variety of weaves and finishes.

- Silk: Silk dress shirts are luxurious and soft to the touch. They're perfect for special occasions, but require more care than cotton shirts.

- Linen: Linen dress shirts are lightweight and breathable, making them perfect for warmer weather. However, they wrinkle easily and require more care than cotton shirts.

How to Find the Right Fit

One of the biggest challenges when it comes to dress shirts is finding the right fit. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect fit:

- Know your measurements: Take your measurements before shopping for dress shirts. This will help you find the right size and fit.

- Try on multiple sizes and styles: Don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes and styles. You may be surprised at what works best for your body type.

- Consider alterations: If you find a dress shirt that you love but it doesn't fit perfectly, consider getting it altered. A few simple alterations can transform a good shirt into a great one.

3. Bonobos Dress Shirts

Target: Men who prefer a more modern, slim fit.

Bonobos dress shirts are known for their modern, slim fit and bold patterns. They're made with high-quality fabrics and come in a variety of colors and styles. If you're looking for a dress shirt that's both stylish and comfortable, Bonobos is a great option.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best fabric for a dress shirt?

A: Cotton is the most common fabric used for dress shirts, but silk and linen are also great options depending on the occasion.

Q: How do I find the right fit for a dress shirt?

A: Take your measurements before shopping and try on multiple sizes and styles. Consider getting alterations if needed.

Q: What are some of the best Nordstrom dress shirt brands?

A: Nordstrom Men's Shop, Robert Graham, and Bonobos are all great options depending on your style and fit preferences.

Q: How do I care for my dress shirts?

A: Follow the care instructions on the label, but in general, wash dress shirts in cold water and hang them to dry.

Conclusion of Nordstrom Dress Shirt Brands

When it comes to finding the perfect dress shirt, Nordstrom has a variety of options to meet your needs. Whether you prefer a classic, timeless look or a more modern, bold style, there's a Nordstrom dress shirt brand for you. Remember to consider the fabric, fit, and care instructions when making your selection, and don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes and styles to find the perfect fit.
