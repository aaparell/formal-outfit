Sherwani Suit Price In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you planning to buy a sherwani suit but not sure about the price? Do you want to know the factors that affect the cost of a sherwani suit in India? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about sherwani suit price in India.

When it comes to buying a sherwani suit in India, the first question that comes to mind is its price. The cost of a sherwani suit varies depending on various factors such as the fabric, embellishments, designer, location, and more. It can be a challenging task to determine the right price for a sherwani suit without proper knowledge. However, we are here to help you understand the target of sherwani suit price in India.

In summary, the price of a sherwani suit in India can range from INR 5,000 to INR 5,00,000 or even more. The cost depends on various factors such as fabric, design, embellishments, designer, and location. In the next few paragraphs, we will discuss each of these factors in detail to help you understand the target of sherwani suit price in India.

Fabric of the Sherwani Suit

The fabric of the sherwani suit plays a crucial role in determining its price. The cost varies depending on the type of fabric used, such as silk, velvet, brocade, cotton, and more. Silk and velvet are the most expensive fabrics for sherwani suits, while cotton is the most affordable. The price also depends on the quality of the fabric, its texture, and the intricacy of the design. For example, a sherwani suit made of pure silk with intricate embroidery will be more expensive than a sherwani suit made of silk blend with minimal design.

Designer and Embellishments

The designer and embellishments used in the sherwani suit can significantly affect its price. A sherwani suit designed by a famous designer will be more expensive than a sherwani suit designed by a local tailor. Similarly, the use of embellishments such as zari work, sequins, stones, and embroidery can increase the cost of the sherwani suit. The more intricate the design and embellishments, the higher the price.

Location

The location where you buy the sherwani suit can also affect its price. In metro cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore, the cost of a sherwani suit can be higher than in other cities or towns. The cost of living and demand for designer clothing can influence the price of a sherwani suit in a particular location.

Customization

The cost of a sherwani suit can also depend on whether it is ready-made or customized. A ready-made sherwani suit will be more affordable than a customized one. If you want a sherwani suit that fits you perfectly and has a unique design, then a customized sherwani suit will be the right choice, but it will be more expensive.

Conclusion of Sherwani Suit Price in India

In conclusion, the price of a sherwani suit in India can vary depending on various factors such as fabric, designer, embellishments, location, and customization. It is essential to consider your budget, style, and occasion before deciding on the right sherwani suit for you. We hope this article has helped you understand the target of sherwani suit price in India.

Sherwani Suit Price in India: Personal Experience

As a fashion enthusiast, I have always been fascinated by the exquisite design and intricate embroidery of sherwani suits. For my cousin's wedding, I decided to buy a sherwani suit that would make me stand out in the crowd. I visited several boutiques and designer stores in Delhi to find the perfect sherwani suit.

After several hours of browsing, I finally found a sherwani suit that caught my eye. It was made of pure silk with intricate embroidery and embellishments. The designer who created the sherwani suit was famous for his exquisite designs and had won several awards for his work. The cost of the sherwani suit was INR 1,50,000, which was way above my budget.

I decided to look for other options and found a local tailor who could create a similar sherwani suit at a lower price. The tailor used a silk blend fabric and minimal design, but the result was still breathtaking. The cost of the customized sherwani suit was INR 50,000, which was within my budget.
