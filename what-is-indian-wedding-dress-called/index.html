What Is The Indian Wedding Dress Called

Written by Luffy Nov 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and vibrancy. The beautiful decorations, delicious food, and traditional customs make them a memorable experience. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire worn by the bride and groom. However, if you are not familiar with Indian culture, you may wonder what the Indian wedding dress is called and how it differs from regular attire.

As a non-Indian, attending an Indian wedding can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to dress code. You may have questions such as "What kind of clothes should I wear?" or "What is considered appropriate attire for an Indian wedding?" These questions can cause anxiety and make you feel out of place. However, with a little understanding of Indian wedding attire, you can dress appropriately and feel confident.

The Indian wedding dress is called a "lehenga" or "sari" for the bride and "sherwani" or "kurta pajama" for the groom. A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a blouse and a dupatta, which is a long scarf that is draped over the head or shoulder. A sari is a long piece of fabric that is draped over the body and paired with a blouse and petticoat. A sherwani is a long coat that is worn over a kurta and pajama, which are loose-fitting pants. The attire is usually heavily embellished with intricate embroidery, beads, and stones, making it a sight to behold.

In summary, the Indian wedding dress consists of a lehenga or sari for the bride and a sherwani or kurta pajama for the groom. These outfits are heavily embellished and reflect the rich cultural heritage of India.

What is the Significance of the Indian Wedding Dress?

As an Indian, wearing traditional attire to a wedding is a way of honoring one's culture and heritage. The Indian wedding dress is not just a piece of clothing; it is a symbol of tradition, values, and customs that have been passed down from generation to generation. The bride and groom's attire represents the union of two families and the beginning of a new chapter in their lives. In addition, the intricate embroidery and embellishments on the dress reflect the richness and opulence of Indian culture.

As an Indian bride, wearing a lehenga or sari is a way of expressing her individuality and personal style. The bride's dress is usually chosen with great care, taking into consideration factors such as color, fabric, and design. The groom's attire also reflects his personal style and is chosen with the same care and attention to detail.

How to Choose the Right Indian Wedding Dress?

Choosing the right Indian wedding dress can be a daunting task, especially if you are not familiar with Indian fashion. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect Indian wedding dress:

1. Consider the occasion: Indian weddings are usually multi-day events with different ceremonies and functions. Each event may require a different dress code, so make sure you know what is expected of you before choosing your attire.

2. Choose the right fabric: Indian wedding dresses are usually made of silk, satin, or chiffon. Make sure you choose a fabric that is comfortable and suits the climate of the location.

3. Pay attention to the details: Indian wedding dresses are known for their intricate embroidery and embellishments. Make sure you choose a dress that has the right amount of bling and suits your personal style.

FAQs

Q. What is the difference between a lehenga and a sari?

A. A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a blouse and a dupatta, while a sari is a long piece of fabric that is draped over the body and paired with a blouse and petticoat.

Q. What is the significance of the dupatta?

A. The dupatta is a long scarf that is draped over the head or shoulder. It is a symbol of modesty and is often used to cover the head during religious ceremonies.

Q. What is a sherwani?

A. A sherwani is a long coat that is worn over a kurta and pajama. It is a traditional Indian outfit that is usually worn by men for formal occasions.

Q. Can I wear a Western outfit to an Indian wedding?

A. While it is not recommended, you can wear a Western outfit to an Indian wedding. However, make sure you choose an outfit that is conservative and respectful of the culture and traditions.

Conclusion of What is Indian Wedding Dress Called

Attending an Indian wedding can be a fun and exciting experience, especially if you know what to wear. The Indian wedding dress consists of a lehenga or sari for the bride and a sherwani or kurta pajama for the groom. These outfits are heavily embellished and reflect the richness and opulence of Indian culture. By following the tips mentioned above, you can choose the perfect Indian wedding dress and make a lasting impression.
