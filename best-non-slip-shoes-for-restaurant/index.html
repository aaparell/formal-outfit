Best Non Slip Shoes For Restaurant A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of slipping and sliding on the wet floors of your restaurant? Do you want to protect yourself from potential accidents and injuries while on the job? Investing in the best non-slip shoes for restaurant is a smart move for any restaurant worker. Not only do they provide safety, but they also offer comfort and durability for long hours on your feet. In this guide, we will explore the benefits of non-slip shoes and provide you with the top options in the market.

Working in a restaurant can be a physically demanding job. From standing for long hours to constantly moving around, it can take a toll on your body. Moreover, the wet floors in the kitchen or dining area can pose a significant danger to workers. Slips and falls are one of the leading causes of workplace injuries in the restaurant industry. Thus, it is crucial to invest in non-slip shoes to prevent these accidents from happening.

The primary target of non-slip shoes is to provide stability and traction on slippery surfaces. They usually come with a slip-resistant sole that has a better grip on wet floors. Additionally, they are made from durable materials that are resistant to spills, stains, and other forms of damage. In other words, they are designed to withstand the harsh conditions of a restaurant environment.

To summarize, the best non-slip shoes for restaurant provide safety, comfort, and durability. They protect you from slips and falls, offer support for your feet and back, and last longer than regular shoes. In the following sections, we will discuss the top options in the market and what makes them stand out.

1. Skechers Work Sure Track

I have been using Skechers Work Sure Track for the past year, and they have been a game-changer for me. Not only do they provide excellent slip-resistance, but they also have memory foam insoles that make them comfortable to wear for long hours. The leather upper is easy to clean and maintains its look even after multiple spills and stains. Moreover, the shoes fit true to size and have a roomy toe box that does not pinch or squeeze your feet. Overall, Skechers Work Sure Track is a reliable and affordable option for anyone looking for non-slip shoes.

2. Crocs Bistro Clog

Crocs Bistro Clog is another great option for restaurant workers. They are made from Croslite foam that is lightweight and odor-resistant. The slip-resistant sole provides excellent traction on wet surfaces, while the enclosed design protects your feet from spills and splatters. The shoes are also easy to clean and come in various colors to match your uniform. However, some users may find them too wide or too loose, so it is recommended to try them on before purchasing.

3. Dansko Professional Clog

Dansko Professional Clog is a premium option for those who want the best of both worlds: style and function. The clogs are made from high-quality leather that is durable and water-resistant. The slip-resistant sole is made from rubber and provides excellent grip on wet floors. The shoes also have a rocker bottom that promotes natural motion and reduces fatigue. However, the shoes may take some time to break in, and they are on the pricier side compared to other options.

4. New Balance Fresh Foam 806

New Balance Fresh Foam 806 is a great option for those who need extra support and cushioning. The shoes feature Fresh Foam midsole that provides a plush feel and absorbs shock. The slip-resistant sole is made from rubber and has a herringbone pattern that enhances traction. The shoes also have a synthetic upper that is easy to clean and maintain. However, some users may find them too heavy or too narrow, so it is recommended to try them on before purchasing.

FAQs

Q: Are non-slip shoes necessary for restaurant workers?

A: Absolutely. Non-slip shoes provide safety and protection for workers in a restaurant environment. They prevent slips and falls, which can lead to serious injuries.

Q: Can I wear regular shoes in a restaurant?

A: It is not recommended. Regular shoes do not have the slip-resistant sole that is necessary for a restaurant environment. They also do not provide the same level of support and durability as non-slip shoes.

Q: How do I know if a shoe is slip-resistant?

A: Look for shoes that have a rubber sole with a slip-resistant pattern. The sole should provide excellent traction on wet and slippery surfaces. You can also check the shoe's rating on the ASTM International F2913-11 standard.

Q: Can I wear non-slip shoes outside of work?

A: Yes. Non-slip shoes are great for any activity that involves wet or slippery surfaces, such as hiking, fishing, or gardening.

Conclusion of Best Non-Slip Shoes for Restaurant

Investing in the best non-slip shoes for restaurant is a smart move for any restaurant worker. They provide safety, comfort, and durability in a harsh environment. Skechers Work Sure Track, Crocs Bistro Clog, Dansko Professional Clog, and New Balance Fresh Foam 806 are some of the top options in the market. Remember to choose a shoe that fits well, provides slip-resistance, and meets your personal preferences. By wearing non-slip shoes, you can focus on your job and enjoy peace of mind knowing that you are protected from potential accidents.
