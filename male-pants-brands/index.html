Levi's is one of the most well-known and iconic male pants brands in the world. Dockers is a great male pants brand for men who are looking for a more casual and comfortable style. The brand is known for its khaki pants, which are perfect for work or a casual day out. Dockers also offers a variety of other styles, including cargo pants and shorts.
