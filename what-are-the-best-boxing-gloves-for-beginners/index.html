What Are The Best Boxing Gloves For Beginners

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start boxing, but not sure what gloves to buy? It can be overwhelming with so many options available. You want gloves that will protect your hands, provide comfort, and fit your budget. In this post, we'll discuss what are the best boxing gloves for beginners.

The pain point for beginners is often not knowing what to look for when purchasing gloves. They may end up with gloves that are too heavy or too light, uncomfortable, or not provide enough protection, which can lead to injuries.

The best boxing gloves for beginners are the ones that fit comfortably and provide adequate protection. You want to consider the weight, material, and style of the gloves. The weight should be appropriate for your size and experience level, the material should be durable, and the style should suit your needs.

In summary, the best boxing gloves for beginners should be comfortable, provide adequate protection, and fit your budget. Consider the weight, material, and style when making your purchase.

Weight of the Gloves

When it comes to weight, beginners should look for gloves between 12oz to 16oz. The weight of the gloves will depend on your size and experience level. If you are a smaller person, you may want to choose lighter gloves, while larger individuals may prefer heavier gloves. It's important to choose the right weight to prevent injury and improve your performance.

As a beginner, I made the mistake of purchasing gloves that were too heavy. This made it difficult to throw punches, and I quickly became fatigued. I recommend choosing a weight that feels comfortable and allows you to move freely.

Material of the Gloves

Boxing gloves are typically made of leather or synthetic materials. Leather gloves are more durable and can last longer, but they are also more expensive. Synthetic gloves are more affordable but may not be as durable. It's important to consider your budget when choosing the material of your gloves.

Personally, I prefer leather gloves because they are more comfortable and provide better protection. However, I understand that they may not be within everyone's budget. If you choose synthetic gloves, make sure to choose a reputable brand that uses high-quality materials.

Style of the Gloves

The style of the gloves will depend on your needs. If you are training for boxing, you may want gloves with more padding to provide more protection. If you are training for Muay Thai or kickboxing, you may want gloves with less padding to allow for more mobility.

I personally use gloves with more padding because I prioritize protection over mobility. It's important to choose a style that suits your needs and goals.

How to Choose the Right Size

Choosing the right size gloves is important to prevent injury and improve performance. To measure the size of your gloves, measure the circumference of your dominant hand around the knuckles. The measurement in inches will correspond with the size of your gloves.

For example, if your hand measures 8 inches, you should choose gloves with a size of 8oz to 10oz. If your hand measures 9 inches, you should choose gloves with a size of 14oz to 16oz.

Conclusion of What Are The Best Boxing Gloves for Beginners

When it comes to choosing the best boxing gloves for beginners, it's important to consider the weight, material, and style of the gloves. Choose a weight that is appropriate for your size and experience level, and a material that fits within your budget. Consider the style that suits your needs and goals. Choosing the right gloves will not only protect your hands but also improve your performance and enjoyment of the sport.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use boxing gloves for other martial arts?

A: Yes, you can use boxing gloves for other martial arts such as Muay Thai, kickboxing, or MMA. However, it's important to consider the style and weight of the gloves that are appropriate for the martial art you are practicing.

Q: What size gloves should I choose as a beginner?

A: As a beginner, you should choose gloves between 12oz to 16oz. It's important to measure your hand's circumference to choose the right size.

Q: Are leather gloves better than synthetic gloves?

A: Leather gloves are more durable and provide better protection, but they are also more expensive. Synthetic gloves are more affordable but may not be as durable.

Q: Can I use gloves with less padding for boxing?

A: Yes, you can use gloves with less padding for boxing, but it's important to consider the protection they provide. Gloves with less padding may be more suitable for martial arts that require more mobility.
