The fashion industry has historically been male-dominated, which has made it challenging for women to break into the industry. However, despite these challenges, some women were determined to make their mark in the industry, and they did.

The first female fashion designer was Jeanne Paquin, who was born in 1869 in France. She founded her fashion house in 1891, and her designs quickly gained popularity. Paquin was known for her innovative designs, which were a mix of traditional and modern styles. She was also the first female designer to launch a fragrance line.
