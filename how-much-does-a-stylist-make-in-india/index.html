How Much Does A Stylist Make In India

Written by Bardi Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in styling and wondering how much a stylist makes in India? The fashion industry in India is growing rapidly, and with it, the demand for stylists is increasing. However, like any other profession, the salary of a stylist in India depends on various factors. In this blog post, we will explore the earning potential of a stylist in India and the factors that affect their salary.

A common pain point for those interested in pursuing a career in styling is the uncertainty around the earning potential. It can be challenging to predict how much you can earn as a stylist, especially in India, where there is no fixed salary structure for this profession.

The salary of a stylist in India can range from INR 10,000 per month for beginners to INR 1,00,000 per month for experienced stylists. However, the earning potential can be much higher for those who work with celebrities, high-end fashion brands, and luxury magazines.

In conclusion, the salary of a stylist in India varies depending on several factors such as experience, location, skillset, and the type of clients they work with. While the earning potential may not be fixed, it is possible to earn a good living as a stylist in India.

Factors Affecting a Stylist's Salary in India

When it comes to styling, several factors can impact a stylist's salary in India. Here are some of the most significant factors:

Experience

Experience is one of the most crucial factors that affect a stylist's salary in India. As you gain more experience, you can demand higher pay rates. Entry-level stylists in India can earn around INR 10,000 per month, while experienced stylists can earn up to INR 1,00,000 per month.

Location

The location of a stylist's work can also impact their salary. Stylists working in metro cities like Mumbai and Delhi tend to earn higher salaries than those working in smaller cities or towns.

Personal Experience

As a stylist, I have been fortunate to work with a wide range of clients, from fashion magazines to celebrities. In my experience, working with high-end clients can significantly impact your earning potential. However, it is essential to build a strong portfolio and network to attract such clients.

Skills and Training

Having a unique skillset or specialized training can also help a stylist earn a higher salary. For example, if you have expertise in bridal styling or celebrity styling, you can charge a premium for your services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is the average salary of a stylist in India?

A. The average salary of a stylist in India can vary from INR 10,000 to INR 1,00,000 per month.

Q. Can a stylist earn a good living in India?

A. Yes, it is possible to earn a good living as a stylist in India, especially if you work with high-end clients and fashion brands.

Q. How can I increase my earning potential as a stylist in India?

A. You can increase your earning potential as a stylist in India by gaining more experience, building a strong portfolio, and specializing in a particular area of styling.

Q. What are the most significant factors that impact a stylist's salary in India?

A. Experience, location, skillset, and the type of clients a stylist works with are the most significant factors that impact their salary in India.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Stylist Make in India

In conclusion, a stylist in India can earn a good living if they have the right skills, experience, and network. While the salary of a stylist in India may not be fixed, there is significant earning potential for those who are willing to put in the work and build their profile. As the fashion industry in India continues to grow, the demand for stylists is set to increase, making it an exciting time to pursue a career in this field.
