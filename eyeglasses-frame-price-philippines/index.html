How to Care for Your Eyeglasses Frames

Caring for your eyeglasses frames is essential to ensure their longevity and durability. Start by cleaning them regularly with a microfiber cloth and lens cleaning solution. Avoid using harsh chemicals or paper products, as they can scratch or damage the lenses and frames.
