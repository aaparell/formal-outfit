Choosing the right shirt brand can be quite challenging, especially with so many options available in the market. So, what should you look for in a shirt brand? Firstly, you need to be clear about your requirements. Do you need a formal shirt or a casual one? Are you looking for a slim fit or a regular fit? Once you have answered these questions, you can start exploring different brands that cater to your needs.

1. Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a popular shirt brand in India known for its quality fabric and modern designs. It offers a wide range of shirts that are suitable for both formal and casual occasions. The brand's logo features a flying bird, which represents freedom and innovation.

Personally, I have been a fan of Van Heusen shirts for a long time. Their slim fit shirts fit me perfectly, and the fabric quality is excellent. They are a bit on the expensive side, but the price is worth it for the quality you get. You can buy Van Heusen shirts online or at their physical stores.
