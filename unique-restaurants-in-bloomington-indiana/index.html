1. Farm Bloomington

One of the best unique restaurants in Bloomington, Indiana is Farm Bloomington. This restaurant is known for its farm-to-table dining experience, with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, so you can always expect fresh and delicious food. I personally recommend trying the roasted beet salad and the pork chop.

2. Anatolia

If you're in the mood for Mediterranean cuisine, look no further than Anatolia. This restaurant offers a wide selection of dishes, including kebabs, hummus, and falafel. The atmosphere is cozy and intimate, making it the perfect spot for a date night. I highly recommend trying the lamb gyro.

3. The Owlery

The Owlery is a vegetarian restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. The menu features a variety of dishes, including vegan burgers, salads, and sandwiches. The restaurant also has a great selection of craft beer and cocktails. I recommend trying the BBQ jackfruit sandwich.

4. Turkuaz Cafe

Turkuaz Cafe is a great spot for those looking for Turkish cuisine. The restaurant offers a variety of dishes, including kebabs, wraps, and salads. The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, making it a great spot for a casual dinner. I recommend trying the lamb kebab.
