Top Australian Suit Brands A Guide To Looking Sharp

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect suit? Do you want to look sharp and confident in your appearance? Look no further than top Australian suit brands! Finding the right suit can be a daunting task, but investing in a quality suit is worth it in the long run. In this guide, we will explore the top Australian suit brands and what makes them stand out.

When it comes to buying a suit, there are several pain points to consider. From the fit to the material, it can be overwhelming to make the right choice. Additionally, finding a suit that is stylish and modern while still remaining timeless can be a challenge. That's where top Australian suit brands come in.

The target of top Australian suit brands is to provide high-quality suits that are both stylish and timeless. These brands use top-of-the-line materials and construction techniques to ensure that their suits not only look good but also last for years to come.

Overall, top Australian suit brands are known for their attention to detail, quality materials, and timeless style. Let's take a closer look at some of the top brands and what makes them stand out.

1. MJ Bale

As one of Australia's most popular suit brands, MJ Bale offers a variety of styles to fit any occasion. I personally love their slim-fit suits, which are both modern and stylish. MJ Bale also uses high-quality materials such as Australian Merino wool to ensure that their suits last for years. Their attention to detail is evident in the small touches like hand-stitched buttonholes and functional sleeve buttons.

2. Calibre

Calibre is known for their contemporary take on classic styles. Their suits are tailored to perfection and offer a modern twist on traditional styles. I love their attention to detail, including unique touches like contrast lapels and pocket squares.

3. Joe Black

Joe Black is a premium menswear brand that offers a range of suits to fit any occasion. Their suits are made from quality materials such as wool and silk blends and are tailored to perfection. I appreciate their focus on classic styles while still incorporating modern details like slim-fit cuts and unique fabrics.

4. Farage

Farage is a luxury menswear brand that offers a range of suits for any occasion. Their suits are made from high-quality materials such as Italian wool and cashmere blends, and their attention to detail is evident in the small touches like horn buttons and hand-stitched lapels. I love their classic styles with unique twists, like their plaid and houndstooth patterns.

Q&A About Top Australian Suit Brands

Q: Are Australian suit brands expensive?

A: While some Australian suit brands can be pricey, investing in a high-quality suit is worth it in the long run. These suits are made from top-of-the-line materials and construction techniques, ensuring that they last for years to come.

Q: Can I wear an Australian suit to any occasion?

A: Absolutely! Australian suit brands offer a range of styles to fit any occasion, from formal events to business meetings to casual outings.

Q: How do I know if a suit fits me correctly?

A: A well-fitting suit should be snug but not tight. The shoulders should fit comfortably without any bunching or sagging, and the sleeves should end at your wrist bone. The jacket should button easily without pulling, and the pants should fit comfortably without being too loose or too tight.

Q: Can I tailor an Australian suit?

A: Yes! Most Australian suit brands offer tailoring services to ensure that your suit fits perfectly. Tailoring can include adjustments to the jacket sleeves, pant length, and waistband.

Conclusion of Top Australian Suit Brands

Investing in a high-quality suit is a smart decision for any man, and Australian suit brands offer some of the best options available. From MJ Bale to Calibre to Joe Black to Farage, these brands offer a range of styles to fit any occasion, all made with attention to detail and high-quality materials. With the right suit, you'll look sharp and confident, ready to take on any challenge that comes your way.
