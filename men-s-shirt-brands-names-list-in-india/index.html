Here are some of the top men's shirt brands in India:

Peter England

Peter England is a well-known and trusted brand that offers a wide range of high-quality shirts. Their shirts are made from premium fabrics and come in a variety of styles, colors, and patterns to suit every occasion.

Van Heusen is one of the most popular and sought-after men's shirt brands in India. Their shirts are known for their classic designs, impeccable fit, and top-quality materials.

Arrow

Arrow is another well-established and popular men's shirt brand in India. They offer a wide range of shirts that are perfect for both casual and formal occasions. Their shirts are known for their superior quality and durability.

Allen Solly

Allen Solly is a trendy and fashionable men's shirt brand in India. They offer a range of shirts that are perfect for the modern, fashion-conscious man. Their shirts come in a variety of colors, patterns, and designs, making them perfect for any occasion.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Men's Shirt Brand in India

When choosing a men's shirt brand in India, there are several factors that you should consider:

Quality of Fabric

The quality of fabric used in the shirt is one of the most important factors to consider. Look for brands that use high-quality materials that are durable and comfortable to wear.

Style and Design

The style and design of the shirt are also important factors to consider. Choose a brand that offers a variety of styles and designs to suit your personal taste and preferences.

Price Range

Men's shirts can come in a wide range of prices, so it's important to choose a brand that fits your budget. Look for brands that offer good value for money without compromising on quality.
