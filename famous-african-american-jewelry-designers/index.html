Meet Some of the Most Famous African American Jewelry Designers

One of the most famous African American jewelry designers is Art Smith. Born in 1917, Smith was a pioneer in the modernist jewelry movement and created bold and abstract pieces that challenged traditional notions of jewelry design. His work has been featured in museums around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City.

Another notable African American jewelry designer is Beverly Johnson. Johnson's work is inspired by her African heritage and often features bold and colorful gemstones. Her work has been worn by celebrities such as Oprah Winfrey and Halle Berry.
