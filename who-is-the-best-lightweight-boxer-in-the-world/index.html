What Makes a Great Lightweight Boxer?

A great lightweight boxer needs to have a combination of speed, agility, power, and endurance. They also need to have a strong mental game and be able to adapt to their opponent's style.

Aside from physical attributes, a great lightweight boxer also needs to have a good work ethic, dedication, and discipline. They need to train regularly and maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle to stay in top shape. They also need to have a good team of coaches and trainers who can help them improve their skills and develop their strategies for each fight.
