Where to Find Plus Size Clothing: A Guide to Stores That Cater to All Sizes

Shopping for plus size clothing can be a daunting task. Many stores don't carry sizes above a certain number or don't offer a wide range of styles for plus size customers. It can feel like the fashion industry has forgotten about those who are not a standard size. However, there are many stores that have plus size clothing and cater to all sizes, making it easier to find fashionable, comfortable clothes that fit well.

If you're tired of searching high and low for plus size clothing that fits your style and budget, don't worry. In this guide, we will help you navigate the world of plus size fashion and provide you with a list of stores that have plus size clothing.

Target

Target is a great option for plus size clothing. They offer stylish and affordable options for sizes 14-26. Their plus size line, Ava & Viv, features trendy pieces that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for work attire or casual weekend wear, Target has you covered. Most of their stores also have a dedicated plus size section, making it easy to find what you need.

Personally, I have found some of my favorite pieces at Target. Their clothes are well-made, comfortable, and always on-trend. I love that I can find everything from basic t-shirts to statement dresses in my size.

Torrid

Torrid is a store that specializes in plus size clothing, offering sizes 10-30. They have a wide range of styles, from edgy to boho, making it easy to find something that fits your personality. They also have an extensive selection of intimates and swimwear, making them a one-stop-shop for all your plus size needs.

As someone who loves alternative fashion, I appreciate that Torrid offers styles that aren't typically available in plus sizes. Their clothes are well-made and designed to flatter curves, so you can feel confident in what you're wearing.

ASOS Curve

ASOS Curve is the plus size line from the popular online retailer, ASOS. They offer sizes 14-28 and have a wide range of styles to choose from. From formalwear to athleisure, ASOS Curve has something for every occasion. They also have a great selection of accessories, allowing you to complete your outfit with the perfect shoes or jewelry.

What I love about ASOS Curve is that they offer the same styles as their straight-sized line, so you don't have to sacrifice fashion for fit. They also have a great selection of unique pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

Old Navy

Old Navy is another great option for plus size clothing. They offer sizes up to 4X and have a wide range of styles for every season. Their clothes are affordable and versatile, making it easy to build a wardrobe that works for you.

Personally, I love Old Navy's denim. They have a great selection of jeans, shorts, and skirts that fit well and are designed to flatter curves. They also have a great selection of basics, like t-shirts and leggings, that are perfect for layering.

Conclusion of Stores That Have Plus Size Clothing

Shopping for plus size clothing doesn't have to be a chore. There are many stores that have plus size clothing and cater to all sizes, making it easier than ever to find fashionable, comfortable clothes that fit well. Whether you prefer to shop in-store or online, there are options available for every budget and style.

Question and Answer

Q: What other stores have plus size clothing?

A: Other stores that have plus size clothing include Lane Bryant, Eloquii, H&M, and Forever 21 Plus.

Q: What should I look for when shopping for plus size clothing?

A: When shopping for plus size clothing, it's important to look for pieces that fit well and flatter your body shape. Look for clothes that are designed specifically for plus sizes and made with stretchy, comfortable fabrics.

Q: Can I find plus size clothing at thrift stores?

A: Yes, you can find plus size clothing at thrift stores. However, it may take more time and effort to find pieces that fit well and are in good condition.

Q: How can I save money on plus size clothing?

A: To save money on plus size clothing, look for sales and clearance items. You can also sign up for store emails to receive discounts and coupons. Additionally, consider shopping at thrift stores or consignment shops for affordable options.
