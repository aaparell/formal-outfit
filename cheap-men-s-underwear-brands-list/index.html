To ensure that your cheap men's underwear lasts as long as possible, it is important to care for it properly. Some tips for caring for your underwear include:

2. Avoid Fabric Softeners

Avoid using fabric softeners when washing your underwear, as they can damage the material and reduce its lifespan.
