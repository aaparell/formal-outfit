Ankle Boots To Wear With Short Dresses A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy May 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old flats or heels with your short dresses? Have you considered ankle boots as an option? Not only are they trendy, but they also add a certain edge to your outfit. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about ankle boots to wear with short dresses.

When it comes to ankle boots, many people may feel intimidated or unsure about how to wear them with short dresses. Common concerns include whether they will look too bulky or make the legs appear shorter. However, with the right styling, ankle boots can be a versatile and chic addition to any outfit.

The main target of wearing ankle boots with short dresses is to create a balanced and proportionate look. Since short dresses already show off a lot of leg, ankle boots can add coverage and balance out the outfit. They also provide warmth and comfort during the colder months.

In summary, ankle boots can be a great option to wear with short dresses, as long as they are styled correctly. They add balance and edge to your outfit while providing warmth and comfort. Now, let's dive into the specifics of how to style ankle boots with short dresses.

Choosing the Right Ankle Boots

The first step in styling ankle boots with short dresses is choosing the right pair. Here are a few things to consider:

1. Heel Height: If you want to elongate your legs, choose ankle boots with a higher heel. For a more casual look, opt for a lower heel or flat pair.

2. Boot Cut: Ankle boots come in various cuts, such as pointy, rounded, or square. Consider the shape of your foot and leg when choosing the right cut.

3. Color: Ankle boots come in a variety of colors, from classic black and brown to bold colors or prints. Choose a color that complements your outfit and personal style.

Personally, I love wearing a pair of black leather ankle boots with a chunky heel. They add a touch of edginess to any outfit and are versatile enough to wear with different styles of short dresses.

Styling Ankle Boots with Short Dresses

Now that you have chosen the perfect pair of ankle boots, it's time to style them with your short dresses. Here are a few tips:

1. Length of the Dress: When pairing ankle boots with short dresses, make sure the dress hits above the knee. If the dress is too long, it will compete with the boots and create an unbalanced look.

2. Tights or No Tights: Depending on the weather and your personal preference, you can pair ankle boots with or without tights. For a more casual look, skip the tights. For a dressier outfit, consider wearing opaque tights in a similar color to your boots.

3. Layering: If it's chilly outside, layer a jacket or coat over your short dress and ankle boots. This will create a cohesive and stylish look while keeping you warm.

One of my favorite ways to style ankle boots with short dresses is by pairing a flowy bohemian dress with a pair of suede ankle boots. It's a perfect combination for a concert or festival, and the ankle boots add a touch of edge to the outfit.

Tips for Different Types of Short Dresses

Depending on the style of your short dress, there are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Bodycon Dress: When wearing a bodycon dress with ankle boots, make sure the boots are fitted and hug your ankle. This will create a streamlined and flattering look.

2. Shift Dress: For a shift dress, consider wearing ankle boots with a higher heel to elongate your legs. You can also add a belt to cinch in your waist and create a more defined silhouette.

3. A-Line Dress: A-line dresses look great with ankle boots because they balance out the flared shape of the dress. Opt for ankle boots with a low heel or no heel for a more casual look.

Caring for Your Ankle Boots

To ensure your ankle boots last for many seasons, make sure to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips:

1. Protect the Leather: Apply a leather protectant spray to your boots to prevent water damage and scratches.

2. Store Properly: When not in use, store your ankle boots in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

3. Clean Regularly: Wipe down your boots with a damp cloth after each use to remove any dirt or debris.

Personally, I love investing in a high-quality pair of ankle boots that will last for many years. With proper care, they can be a staple in your wardrobe for multiple seasons.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a maxi dress?

A: Ankle boots tend to work better with short dresses because they create a balanced look. However, if you want to wear ankle boots with a maxi dress, make sure the dress has a high slit or is slightly shorter in the front to show off the boots.

Q: Should I tuck my jeans into ankle boots?

A: Yes, tucking your jeans into ankle boots is a great way to show off your boots and create a streamlined look. Make sure the jeans are fitted and not too baggy.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a pencil skirt?

A: Yes, ankle boots work well with pencil skirts, especially if the skirt hits just above the knee. Choose a pair of ankle boots with a skinny heel to elongate your legs.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a mini skirt?

A: Yes, ankle boots are a great option to wear with mini skirts. Make sure the skirt is not too short and hits mid-thigh or just above the knee.

Conclusion of Ankle Boots to Wear with Short Dresses

Ankle boots can be a versatile and chic addition to any short dress outfit. By choosing the right pair and styling them correctly, you can create a balanced and edgy look that is perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your ankle boots properly, and they will be a staple in your wardrobe for many seasons to come.
