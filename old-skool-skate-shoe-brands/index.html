Old Skool Skate Shoe Brands: The Classics That Never Go Out of Style

Skateboarding is a sport that has been around for decades, and with it comes a rich history of old skool skate shoe brands. These brands have stood the test of time and have been worn by skaters of all ages and skill levels. However, finding the perfect pair of skate shoes can be a challenge, especially if you're new to the sport or don't know where to start.

If you're looking for a classic, reliable skate shoe, then old skool skate shoe brands are the way to go. These brands have been around for years and have gained a loyal following of skaters who swear by their quality and durability. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, these brands have something to offer everyone.

In this post, we'll take a closer look at some of the most popular old skool skate shoe brands and what makes them so special. From Vans to Adidas, we'll explore the history and features of each brand to help you find the perfect pair of skate shoes for your needs.

Vans: The Classic Skate Shoe

When it comes to old skool skate shoe brands, Vans is perhaps the most iconic. Originally founded in 1966 as the Van Doren Rubber Company, Vans began as a small, family-owned business that manufactured shoes on-site and sold them directly to the public.

Over the years, Vans has become synonymous with skateboarding and has earned a reputation for producing high-quality, durable shoes that can stand up to the rigors of the sport. Today, Vans offers a wide range of skate shoes in a variety of styles and colors, including the classic Slip-On and Old Skool models.

My personal experience with Vans has been nothing short of amazing. I've been wearing Vans shoes for years and have never been disappointed with their performance. The shoes are comfortable, stylish, and provide excellent grip on the board, making them ideal for skating.

Adidas: A Classic with a Twist

While Adidas may not be as closely associated with skateboarding as Vans, the brand has a long history of producing high-quality athletic shoes that are perfect for the sport. In recent years, Adidas has expanded its line of skate shoes to include a variety of styles and colors.

One of the things that sets Adidas apart from other old skool skate shoe brands is its incorporation of modern technology into its shoes. For example, many of Adidas' skate shoes feature a unique sole design that provides excellent grip and board feel, while also offering superior shock absorption.

I recently picked up a pair of Adidas skate shoes and have been blown away by their performance. The shoes are comfortable, lightweight, and provide excellent grip on the board, making them ideal for skating.

DC Shoes: Built for Skaters, by Skaters

DC Shoes is another old skool skate shoe brand that has gained a loyal following among skaters. Founded in 1994 by professional skateboarders Ken Block and Damon Way, DC Shoes was originally created as a brand that catered specifically to the needs of skaters.

Today, DC Shoes offers a wide range of skate shoes, apparel, and accessories, all designed with the needs of skaters in mind. The brand's shoes are known for their durability, flexibility, and board feel, making them a favorite among skaters of all skill levels.

Personally, I've been a fan of DC Shoes for years and have owned several pairs over the course of my skating career. The shoes are comfortable, stylish, and provide excellent grip on the board, making them a great choice for anyone looking for a reliable skate shoe.

Emerica: A Skater-Owned Brand

Founded in 1996 by professional skateboarder Andrew Reynolds, Emerica is a skater-owned brand that has become a favorite among skaters thanks to its commitment to producing high-quality, durable skate shoes.

Emerica's shoes are known for their simple, classic designs, as well as their excellent board feel and grip. The brand offers a variety of styles and colors to choose from, making it easy to find the perfect pair of shoes for your needs.

As someone who values supporting skater-owned brands, I recently picked up a pair of Emerica shoes and have been thoroughly impressed with their performance. The shoes are comfortable, lightweight, and provide excellent grip on the board, making them a great choice for anyone looking for a reliable skate shoe.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying old skool skate shoes?

A: When buying old skool skate shoes, it's important to consider factors such as durability, grip, board feel, and comfort. Look for shoes that are made from high-quality materials and have a sole design that provides excellent grip on the board.

Q: Are old skool skate shoes more expensive than other types of skate shoes?

A: Old skool skate shoes can be more expensive than other types of skate shoes, but they are often worth the investment due to their high-quality construction and durability. Additionally, many old skool skate shoe brands offer a wide range of styles and price points to choose from.

Q: Can I wear old skool skate shoes for activities other than skating?

A: Yes! While old skool skate shoes are designed with skating in mind, they can be worn for a variety of activities, including casual wear and other sports. The durable construction and comfortable design of these shoes make them a versatile choice for any occasion.

Q: How do I care for my old skool skate shoes?

A: To keep your old skool skate shoes looking and performing their best, it's important to clean them regularly with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials, as these can damage the shoes. Additionally, store your shoes in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent damage from moisture or heat.

Conclusion of Old Skool Skate Shoe Brands

Old skool skate shoe brands are a classic choice for anyone looking for a reliable, high-quality skate shoe. From Vans to Adidas, these brands have stood the test of time and have earned a loyal following among skaters of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're new to the sport or a seasoned pro, there's a pair of old skool skate shoes out there that's perfect for you.
