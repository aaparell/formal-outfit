What To Wear To College Formal Guys A Guide To Look Dapper And Comfortable

Written by Daniel May 21, 2023 · 5 min read

College formals can be intimidating for guys who are not used to dressing up. Choosing the right outfit that looks good and feels comfortable can be a challenge. But worry not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will give you tips on what to wear to college formal guys so you can look dapper and confident.

When it comes to college formals, the dress code is usually semi-formal or formal. That means you need to dress up, but not necessarily in a tuxedo. The key is to find the right balance between dressing up and feeling comfortable. Here are some tips on what to wear to college formal guys:

First, start with a suit or a blazer. If you’re going for a formal look, a suit is your best bet. Choose a dark-colored suit like navy or black. If you want to go for a less formal look, a blazer paired with dress pants or chinos is a good option. Stick to neutral colors like gray, navy, or beige.

Second, choose the right shirt. A dress shirt is a must for a formal look. Make sure it’s crisp and ironed. Stick to classic colors like white or light blue. If you’re going for a less formal look, a button-down shirt is a good option. You can go for patterns or colors that suit your style.

Third, choose the right shoes. Dress shoes are a must for a formal look. Stick to classic styles like Oxford or Derby shoes. Black or brown leather shoes are the best options. If you’re going for a less formal look, you can wear loafers or dress boots.

In summary, when it comes to what to wear to college formal guys, the key is to find the right balance between dressing up and feeling comfortable. Start with a suit or a blazer, choose the right shirt, and choose the right shoes.

Accessorizing for College Formal Guys

Accessories can make or break your outfit, so it’s important to choose them wisely. Here are some tips on how to accessorize for college formal guys:

First, choose the right tie. A tie is a must for a formal look. Stick to classic colors like black, navy, or burgundy. If you’re going for a less formal look, you can go for a bow tie or a skinny tie.

Second, choose the right pocket square. A pocket square is a great way to add some color and personality to your outfit. Stick to classic colors like white or light blue. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can go for patterns or bold colors.

Third, choose the right watch. A watch is a great accessory that can add some sophistication to your outfit. Stick to classic styles like leather or metal bands. Avoid sports or casual watches.

In summary, when it comes to accessorizing for college formal guys, the key is to keep it simple and classic. Choose the right tie, pocket square, and watch to add some personality to your outfit.

Casual Alternatives for College Formal Guys

If you’re not comfortable wearing a suit or a blazer to a college formal, there are some casual alternatives that you can consider. Here are some options:

First, a sweater and dress pants. This is a great option if you want to go for a less formal look. Choose a sweater in a classic color like gray or navy, and pair it with dress pants or chinos.

Second, a dress shirt and jeans. This is a great option if you want to keep it casual but still look put-together. Choose a dress shirt in a classic color like white or light blue, and pair it with dark jeans.

Third, a blazer and jeans. This is a great option if you want to dress up a little bit but still keep it casual. Choose a blazer in a neutral color like gray or navy, and pair it with dark jeans.

In summary, if you’re not comfortable wearing a suit or a blazer to a college formal, there are some casual alternatives that you can consider. Choose a sweater and dress pants, a dress shirt and jeans, or a blazer and jeans.

Q&A Section

Q: Can I wear a tuxedo to a college formal?

A: It depends on the dress code. If the dress code is formal, then a tuxedo is appropriate. But if the dress code is semi-formal, then a suit or a blazer is a better option.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to a college formal?

A: No. Sneakers are too casual for a college formal. Stick to dress shoes like Oxford or Derby shoes.

Q: Can I wear a polo shirt to a college formal?

A: No. A polo shirt is too casual for a college formal. Stick to a dress shirt or a button-down shirt.

Q: Can I wear a vest to a college formal?

A: Yes. A vest is a great way to add some sophistication to your outfit. Stick to a vest that matches your suit or blazer.

Conclusion of What to Wear to College Formal Guys

In conclusion, when it comes to what to wear to college formal guys, the key is to find the right balance between dressing up and feeling comfortable. Start with a suit or a blazer, choose the right shirt, and choose the right shoes. Accessorize wisely, and consider casual alternatives if you’re not comfortable wearing a suit or a blazer. Follow these tips, and you’ll look dapper and confident at your next college formal.
