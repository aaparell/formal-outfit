How to Measure for Men's Dress Pants Size 44x36

Measuring for men's dress pants size 44x36 is relatively simple. You will need a measuring tape and a friend to help you with the measurements. Follow these steps:

  1. Measure your waist at the point where you want your pants to sit. Make sure the measuring tape is snug but not too tight.
  2. Measure your inseam from the crotch to the bottom of your ankle. Make sure you are standing up straight and that the measuring tape is snug but not too tight.
  3. If you are uncertain about your measurements, it is always best to consult a professional tailor who can take accurate measurements for you.
