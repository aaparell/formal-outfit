Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands A Guide To The Best Ones

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you love vintage fashion but find it difficult to incorporate it into your modern wardrobe? Are you tired of searching through thrift stores for the perfect retro-inspired piece? Look no further than vintage inspired fashion brands. These brands take the best elements of vintage fashion and infuse them with modern styles, creating unique and wearable pieces for any occasion.

If you’re someone who loves vintage fashion but struggles to find pieces that fit your modern lifestyle or budget, vintage inspired fashion brands are the answer. These brands offer a wide range of styles, from 1920s flapper dresses to 1970s bohemian tops, all with a modern twist. Whether you’re looking for a statement piece for a special occasion or everyday wear, there’s a vintage inspired fashion brand out there for you.

In this article, we’ll explore the top vintage inspired fashion brands and what sets them apart. We’ll also discuss the benefits of shopping with these brands and how to incorporate vintage inspired pieces into your wardrobe.

The Best Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands

One of the best vintage inspired fashion brands out there is Unique Vintage. This brand offers a wide range of retro-inspired styles, from 1920s flapper dresses to 1950s swing skirts. Their pieces are perfect for special occasions or everyday wear, and they offer a range of sizes to fit all body types. Another great brand is ModCloth, which offers vintage inspired clothing as well as modern styles with a vintage twist. Their pieces are perfect for everyday wear and offer a range of sizes for all body types.

My personal favorite vintage inspired fashion brand is Collectif. They offer a wide range of retro-inspired styles, from 1940s tea dresses to 1960s mod styles. Their pieces are high-quality and fit like a dream, and they offer a range of sizes to fit all body types. I love incorporating their pieces into my everyday wardrobe, and they’re always my go-to for special occasions.

The Benefits of Shopping Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands

Shopping with vintage inspired fashion brands offers a number of benefits. First, you can find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces that you won’t find anywhere else. These brands take the best elements of vintage fashion and infuse them with modern styles, creating unique and wearable pieces for any occasion. Second, shopping with these brands is often more affordable than buying true vintage pieces. While true vintage pieces can be expensive and difficult to find, vintage inspired fashion brands offer affordable and accessible options. Finally, shopping with these brands is a sustainable choice. By buying vintage inspired pieces, you’re supporting brands that prioritize sustainability and ethical practices.

Incorporating Vintage Inspired Pieces into Your Wardrobe

When it comes to incorporating vintage inspired pieces into your wardrobe, the possibilities are endless. One easy way to start is by pairing a vintage inspired top with modern jeans or trousers. This creates a fun and unexpected look that’s perfect for everyday wear. Another option is to mix and match vintage inspired pieces with true vintage pieces. This creates a unique and eclectic look that’s perfect for special occasions or fashion-forward events. Finally, don’t be afraid to play with accessories. Vintage inspired accessories, like scarves and hats, can instantly add a retro touch to any outfit.

Tips for Shopping Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands

When shopping with vintage inspired fashion brands, it’s important to pay attention to sizing. These brands often have their own size charts, which can differ from traditional sizing. Be sure to measure yourself and consult the brand’s size chart before making a purchase. It’s also important to read reviews and check the brand’s return policy before making a purchase. Finally, be sure to shop around and compare prices. While vintage inspired fashion brands are often more affordable than true vintage pieces, prices can still vary from brand to brand.

Q&A about Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands

Q: What is the difference between vintage inspired fashion and true vintage fashion?

A: Vintage inspired fashion refers to clothing that is designed to look like vintage pieces, but is made with modern materials and techniques. True vintage fashion refers to clothing that was actually produced during a specific time period, usually between the 1920s and 1980s.

Q: Are vintage inspired fashion brands sustainable?

A: Many vintage inspired fashion brands prioritize sustainability and ethical practices. By shopping with these brands, you can feel good about making a sustainable choice.

Q: Can vintage inspired pieces be worn for special occasions?

A: Yes! Vintage inspired fashion brands offer a wide range of styles, from 1920s flapper dresses to 1970s bohemian tops, all with a modern twist. Many of these pieces are perfect for special occasions.

Q: How do I know which vintage inspired fashion brand is right for me?

A: The best way to know which vintage inspired fashion brand is right for you is to do your research. Look at different brands’ styles and price points, and read reviews from other customers. You can also look at the brand’s sizing chart to ensure a good fit.

Conclusion of Vintage Inspired Fashion Brands

Vintage inspired fashion brands offer a unique and sustainable way to incorporate vintage fashion into your modern wardrobe. By shopping with these brands, you can find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces at an affordable price. Whether you’re looking for a statement piece for a special occasion or everyday wear, there’s a vintage inspired fashion brand out there for you.
