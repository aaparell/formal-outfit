Top Textile Companies in India 2021: A Closer Look

One of the top textile companies in India 2021 is Arvind Limited. With a rich history of over 90 years, Arvind Limited is a leading textile manufacturer in India. The company offers a wide range of products, including denim, knits, woven fabrics, and garments. They have a strong focus on sustainability and have implemented various initiatives to reduce their environmental impact.

Another top textile company in India 2021 is Welspun India Limited. They are the largest home textile manufacturer in the world and offer a wide range of products, including towels, bedding, and rugs. They are known for their high-quality products, innovative designs, and customer-centric approach.
