Vintage Suit Brands A Guide To Timeless Style

Written by Eveline Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the fast fashion trends that come and go like the seasons? Do you crave a more timeless and classic look? Look no further than vintage suit brands. These brands not only offer high-quality garments that have stood the test of time, but they also provide a unique sense of style and sophistication.

Many people may shy away from vintage suit brands due to their perceived expense or lack of availability. However, with a little bit of research and knowledge, you can find the perfect vintage suit to fit your budget and style.

The Target of Vintage Suit Brands

Vintage suit brands cater to those who are looking for a more classic and timeless look. These brands offer a level of quality and craftsmanship that is often lacking in modern fashion. They also provide a unique sense of style that sets the wearer apart from the crowd.

Whether you are looking for a statement piece for a special occasion or a classic suit that can be worn for years to come, vintage suit brands have something to offer everyone.

My Personal Experience with Vintage Suit Brands

As someone who values quality and timeless style, I have always been drawn to vintage suit brands. One of my favorite brands is Brooks Brothers, which has been around since 1818 and is known for its classic American style.

I was able to find a beautiful navy blue suit from Brooks Brothers at a vintage store for a fraction of the cost of a new suit. The fit and quality were impeccable, and I received countless compliments on the unique style.

The Best Vintage Suit Brands to Look For

When it comes to vintage suit brands, there are a few names that stand out. Some of the most popular brands include:

Brooks Brothers

As mentioned before, Brooks Brothers is a classic American brand that has been around for over 200 years. Their suits are known for their impeccable quality and timeless style.

Hickey Freeman

Hickey Freeman is another American brand that has been around for over 100 years. They are known for their high-quality fabrics and attention to detail.

Savile Row

Savile Row is a street in London that is famous for its bespoke tailoring. Many of the world's top fashion designers have shops on this street, and their suits are known for their exceptional quality and craftsmanship.

Gieves & Hawkes

Gieves & Hawkes is a British brand that has been around for over 200 years. They are known for their classic English style and attention to detail.

Tips for Buying Vintage Suits

Buying a vintage suit can be a daunting task, especially if you are new to the world of vintage fashion. Here are a few tips to help you find the perfect suit:

1. Know Your Measurements

Before you start shopping, make sure you know your measurements. Vintage sizes can vary greatly from modern sizes, so it's important to measure yourself and compare your measurements to the garment's measurements.

2. Check the Condition

When buying a vintage suit, it's important to check the condition of the garment. Look for any stains, tears, or signs of wear and tear. If you're buying online, make sure to ask for detailed photos of the suit.

3. Research the Brand

Do some research on the brand before you buy. Look for reviews and information on the brand's history and reputation. This will give you a better idea of what to expect from the garment.

4. Try it On

When possible, try the suit on before you buy it. This will give you a better idea of how it fits and whether or not it needs any alterations.

Question and Answer

Q: Are vintage suits expensive?

A: The cost of a vintage suit can vary greatly depending on the brand, condition, and rarity of the garment. However, many vintage suits can be found for a fraction of the cost of a new suit.

Q: Can vintage suits be altered?

A: Yes, vintage suits can be altered to fit your body. However, it's important to find a skilled tailor who has experience working with vintage garments.

Q: Are vintage suits still in style?

A: Yes, vintage suits are still in style and are often sought after for their timeless look and quality.

Q: Where can I buy vintage suits?

A: Vintage suits can be found at vintage stores, thrift shops, and online marketplaces such as eBay and Etsy.

Conclusion of Vintage Suit Brands

When it comes to timeless style and quality, vintage suit brands are hard to beat. With a little bit of research and knowledge, you can find the perfect vintage suit to fit your budget and style. Whether you're looking for a statement piece or a classic suit that can be worn for years to come, vintage suit brands have something to offer everyone.
