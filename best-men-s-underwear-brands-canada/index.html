Discover The Best Men s Underwear Brands In Canada

Written by Daniel Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Let's face it - choosing the right underwear can be a challenge for any man. From the fit to the fabric, there are a lot of factors to consider when making a purchase. Plus, with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to know which brands to trust. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best men's underwear brands in Canada.

When it comes to men's underwear, comfort and durability are key. You want a pair of underwear that will keep you feeling fresh and comfortable all day long, without falling apart after a few washes. Additionally, you want underwear that fits well and doesn't bunch or ride up, causing discomfort and irritation.

So, what are the best men's underwear brands in Canada? Here are some of our top picks:

1. SAXX

One of the most popular men's underwear brands in Canada, SAXX is known for their innovative design that features a patented BallPark Pouch. This pouch helps to keep everything in place, reducing chafing and irritation. Additionally, SAXX underwear is made from high-quality, moisture-wicking fabrics and comes in a variety of styles and colors.

2. Stanfield's

For over 160 years, Stanfield's has been a trusted name in men's underwear. Their products are made from soft, breathable fabrics and are designed to provide all-day comfort. Plus, Stanfield's offers a wide range of styles, from traditional boxers to modern briefs and trunks.

3. MyPakage

Another popular brand in Canada, MyPakage is known for their patented Keyhole Comfort Technology. This design features a separate pouch for your package, providing support and reducing the need for constant adjustments. MyPakage underwear is also made from premium fabrics that are both breathable and moisture-wicking.

4. Ethika

If you're looking for bold, colorful designs, Ethika is the brand for you. Their underwear is made from a blend of cotton and spandex, providing a comfortable, stretchy fit. Plus, Ethika offers a wide range of styles, from briefs to boxer briefs and beyond.

5. BN3TH

Designed with comfort and sustainability in mind, BN3TH offers a range of men's underwear made from eco-friendly materials. Their patented MyPakage Pouch provides support and comfort, while the use of Modal fabric made from sustainably-harvested beech trees makes these underwear a great choice for the eco-conscious consumer.

6. Under Armour

While primarily known for their athletic apparel, Under Armour also offers a range of men's underwear that is designed for active lifestyles. Their underwear is made from moisture-wicking fabrics that help to keep you cool and dry, making them a great choice for workouts or other physical activities.

Why is it important to choose the right underwear?

Choosing the right underwear is important for a number of reasons. First and foremost, wearing the wrong underwear can be uncomfortable and even painful, leading to chafing, irritation, and other issues. Additionally, wearing the wrong underwear can affect your confidence and overall sense of well-being.

How do I know if my underwear fits properly?

Properly-fitting underwear should be snug but not too tight, with enough room to move comfortably. The waistband should sit comfortably on your hips, without digging in or rolling down. If you're experiencing discomfort, chafing, or other issues, it may be time to try a new size or style.

How often should I replace my underwear?

While it may be tempting to hold onto your favorite pair of underwear for years, it's important to replace them regularly to ensure that they are still providing the support and comfort you need. It's generally recommended that you replace your underwear every 6-12 months.

What should I look for when choosing men's underwear?

When choosing men's underwear, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you want to choose a brand that uses high-quality fabrics that are breathable and moisture-wicking. Additionally, you want to look for a design that provides support and comfort, whether that's through a separate pouch or other features. Finally, you want to choose a style that fits your personal preferences and needs.

Conclusion of Best Men's Underwear Brands Canada

Choosing the right men's underwear is an important decision that can affect your comfort and confidence throughout the day. By choosing one of the top men's underwear brands in Canada, such as SAXX, Stanfield's, or MyPakage, you can ensure that you're getting high-quality, comfortable underwear that will last. So, take the time to explore your options and find the perfect pair of underwear for your needs.
