How Much Does It Cost To Hire A Formal Dress

Written by Petter May 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you attending a fancy event and wondering how much it would cost to hire a formal dress? It can be frustrating to search for the perfect dress only to find out that it's outside your budget. Fortunately, there are several options available that can help you find the perfect dress without breaking the bank.

The cost of hiring a formal dress can vary depending on several factors such as the type of dress, the designer, the location, and the duration of the hire. Many people find that hiring a dress is more cost-effective than purchasing one, especially if they only plan to wear it once. Additionally, hiring a dress allows you to wear a designer dress that you might not be able to afford otherwise.

The average cost of hiring a formal dress ranges from $100 to $400, but it can go up to $1000 or more for designer dresses. Some websites offer rental services where you can browse through a collection of dresses and choose the one that suits you best. The rental price usually includes delivery, dry cleaning, and return shipping.

In conclusion, hiring a formal dress can be a great option if you want to save money and still look fantastic. It's important to do your research and compare prices between different rental services to find the best deal. By following these tips, you can find the perfect dress that fits your budget and makes you feel like a million dollars.

Personal Experience

I attended a black-tie event last year and didn't want to spend a fortune on a dress that I would only wear once. I decided to look into hiring a formal dress and found a website that offered a wide range of affordable dresses. I was able to find a beautiful designer dress for only $150, which was a fraction of the cost of purchasing it. The dress was delivered to my doorstep, and I returned it after the event without any hassle. I received many compliments on my dress and felt confident and comfortable all night.

Where to Hire a Formal Dress?

There are several websites where you can hire a formal dress, such as Rent the Runway, Glam Corner, and Designer Dress Hire. These websites offer a vast collection of dresses from various designers and price points. You can browse through the collection, select your size, and book the dress for a specific date. The dress will be delivered to your doorstep, and you can return it after the event. Some websites also offer the option to try on the dress before hiring it.

Factors that Affect the Cost of Hiring a Formal Dress

The cost of hiring a formal dress can vary depending on several factors, such as:

  • The type of dress
  • The designer
  • The location
  • The duration of the hire
  • The accessories included

Designer dresses are usually more expensive to hire than non-designer dresses. The location can also affect the cost, as some areas may have a higher demand for formal dresses. The duration of the hire can also impact the cost, as some rental services offer discounts for longer hires. Accessories such as jewelry and handbags may also be available for hire at an additional cost.

Tips for Saving Money when Hiring a Formal Dress

If you're on a tight budget, there are several ways to save money when hiring a formal dress:

  • Look for discounts and promo codes
  • Book in advance
  • Choose a non-designer dress
  • Shorten the hiring duration
  • Accessorize with items you already own

Some rental services offer discounts and promo codes for first-time customers or during certain periods. Booking in advance can also help you save money, as some websites offer early bird discounts. Non-designer dresses are usually less expensive to hire than designer dresses. Shortening the hiring duration can also reduce the cost, as some rental services offer discounts for shorter hires. Lastly, accessorizing with items you already own can save you money on hiring additional accessories.

FAQs

Q: How long can I hire a formal dress?

A: The hiring duration can vary depending on the rental service, but it usually ranges from 4 to 8 days.

Q: Can I try on the dress before hiring it?

A: Some rental services offer the option to try on the dress before hiring it, either in-store or by mail.

Q: What happens if I damage the dress?

A: Most rental services offer insurance that covers minor damages. However, significant damages or loss may result in additional fees.

Q: Can I cancel my dress hire?

A: Most rental services have a cancellation policy that allows you to cancel your booking up to a certain date. However, cancellation fees may apply.

Conclusion

In conclusion, hiring a formal dress can be a cost-effective and convenient option for those attending a fancy event. By considering the factors that affect the cost and following the tips for saving money, you can find the perfect dress that fits your budget and makes you look and feel fantastic. With so many rental services available, it's easy to find the dress that suits your style and occasion.
