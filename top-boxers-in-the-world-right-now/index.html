As one of the most popular fighters in the world, Canelo Alvarez has built a reputation as a fierce competitor with an impressive record. With 56 wins and only two losses under his belt, he's widely regarded as one of the best pound-for-pound boxers in the game today.

What sets Canelo apart from other fighters is his unique fighting style. He's known for his powerful punches and quick footwork, which allow him to move around the ring with ease and catch his opponents off guard.

Tyson Fury is another top boxer who's made a name for himself in the sport. With a record of 30 wins and one draw, he's proven himself to be a skilled fighter with a lot of heart.

4. Terence Crawford

Terence Crawford is another top boxer who's known for his impressive record and unique fighting style. With 37 wins and no losses, he's one of the most dominant fighters in the game today.
