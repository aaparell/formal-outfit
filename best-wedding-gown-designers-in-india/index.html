Top Wedding Gown Designers In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning your wedding and searching for the perfect wedding gown? Are you feeling overwhelmed with the abundance of options available in the market? Choosing the right wedding gown designer can be a daunting task, but it is essential to ensure that your special day is everything you dream of. In this article, we will guide you through the top wedding gown designers in India and help you find the perfect designer for your big day.

When it comes to wedding gowns, every bride has her unique style, and it is crucial to find a designer who can cater to your specific needs. With so many options to choose from, selecting the right wedding gown designer can be a challenge. It is essential to consider factors such as the designer's style, experience, and price range when making your decision.

If you are looking for the best wedding gown designers in India, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide to help you find the perfect designer for your special day. We will cover the top designers in India, their signature styles, and their price range.

Sabyasachi Mukherjee

Sabyasachi Mukherjee is one of the most renowned designers in India and is known for his exquisite bridal collections. His designs are a fusion of traditional and contemporary styles, and he is famous for his intricate handcrafted embroidery work. His designs reflect the rich Indian heritage and are perfect for brides who want to make a statement on their special day. Sabyasachi's bridal collection starts from INR 1.5 lakhs and can go up to INR 10 lakhs.

Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra is another leading designer in India and is famous for his Bollywood-inspired designs. His work is a blend of contemporary and traditional styles, and he is known for his luxurious fabrics and intricate embroidery work. His bridal collection is perfect for brides who want to make a bold statement on their wedding day. Manish Malhotra's bridal collection starts from INR 1 lakh and can go up to INR 15 lakhs.

Anita Dongre

Anita Dongre is a leading designer in India and is known for her elegant and chic designs. Her work is a fusion of traditional Indian styles and modern western influences. Her bridal collection is perfect for brides who want to keep their look simple yet elegant. Anita Dongre's bridal collection starts from INR 50,000 and can go up to INR 5 lakhs.

Tarun Tahiliani

Tarun Tahiliani is a celebrated designer in India and is known for his exquisite and luxurious designs. His work is a fusion of traditional Indian styles and modern contemporary designs. His bridal collection is perfect for brides who want to make a statement on their special day. Tarun Tahiliani's bridal collection starts from INR 1.5 lakhs and can go up to INR 10 lakhs.

Conclusion of Best Wedding Gown Designers in India

Choosing the right wedding gown designer can be a challenge, but it is essential to ensure that your special day is everything you dream of. In this article, we have provided a comprehensive guide to the top wedding gown designers in India. Whether you are looking for traditional or contemporary designs, these designers have something for everyone. We hope this article has helped you find the perfect designer for your big day.

Question and Answer

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a wedding gown from a top designer in India?

A: Prices for wedding gowns from top designers in India vary widely depending on the designer and the style of the gown. Prices can range from INR 50,000 to INR 15 lakhs.

Q: How far in advance should I book my wedding gown designer?

A: It is recommended that you book your wedding gown designer at least six months in advance to ensure that you get the designer and style that you want.

Q: Can I customize my wedding gown with a top designer in India?

A: Yes, most top wedding gown designers in India offer customization services to ensure that your gown is tailored to your specific needs.

Q: Do top wedding gown designers in India offer fittings?

A: Yes, most top wedding gown designers in India offer fittings to ensure that your gown fits perfectly.
