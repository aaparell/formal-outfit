What Does An Indian Bride Wear

Written by William Nov 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their extravagant celebrations and colorful outfits. However, for a bride, choosing the perfect outfit can be overwhelming. With so many options available, it can be difficult to decide what to wear on the big day.

One of the biggest pain points for Indian brides is finding an outfit that is both traditional and modern. The outfit has to be comfortable, easy to move around in, and most importantly, it has to be stunning. On top of that, there are cultural and religious norms that have to be taken into consideration.

An Indian bride typically wears a traditional outfit consisting of a saree or a lehenga. A saree is a long piece of fabric that is draped over the body, while a lehenga is a long skirt paired with a blouse and a scarf. Both the saree and lehenga are usually adorned with intricate embroidery, beading, and other embellishments. The color of the outfit is also important, with red being the most popular choice for a bride.

In addition to the outfit, an Indian bride also wears jewelry and accessories. This includes a necklace, earrings, bangles, and a maang tikka (a piece of jewelry worn on the forehead). The jewelry is often made of gold and is also adorned with precious stones and intricate designs.

To sum up, an Indian bride wears a traditional outfit such as a saree or a lehenga that is embellished with intricate embroidery, beading, and other embellishments. The outfit is usually in the color red, and is accompanied by jewelry and accessories such as a necklace, earrings, bangles, and a maang tikka.

Choosing the Perfect Outfit

Choosing the perfect outfit for your wedding day can be a daunting task, but it's also one of the most exciting parts of getting married. As an Indian bride, you have a plethora of options to choose from.

When I got married, I knew I wanted to wear a saree, but I wasn't sure about the color or the design. I spent hours going through magazines and online stores, trying to find the perfect outfit. Eventually, I found a beautiful red saree with gold embroidery and beading. It was traditional yet modern, and it made me feel like a princess.

When choosing your outfit, it's important to consider your personal style, cultural norms, and religious practices. You should also take into account the time of day and the season of your wedding. For example, if you're getting married in the summer, you may want to choose a lighter fabric that's more breathable.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

Accessorizing your outfit is just as important as choosing the outfit itself. The right jewelry and accessories can take your outfit to the next level.

For my wedding, I wore a gold necklace, earrings, and bangles that were adorned with precious stones. I also wore a maang tikka that was studded with diamonds. The jewelry complemented my saree perfectly and made me feel like a queen.

When choosing your jewelry, it's important to consider the design and the color of your outfit. You want your jewelry to complement your outfit, not clash with it.

The Significance of the Color Red

The color red is considered to be the most auspicious color in Indian culture. It symbolizes love, passion, and fertility. That's why it's the most popular color choice for Indian brides.

Red also has cultural and religious significance. In Hindu weddings, the bride wears a red saree or lehenga as it is believed to be the color of Mars, the planet that represents marriage.

How to Choose the Right Jewelry

Choosing the right jewelry can be just as important as choosing the right outfit. When choosing your jewelry, consider the design and the color of your outfit. You want your jewelry to complement your outfit, not clash with it.

If you're wearing a heavily embellished outfit, you may want to opt for simpler jewelry. On the other hand, if your outfit is simple, you can go for more elaborate jewelry.

Personal Experience

On my wedding day, I wore a red saree that was embroidered with gold thread and beading. I paired the saree with a gold necklace, earrings, and bangles that were adorned with precious stones. I also wore a maang tikka that was studded with diamonds.

The outfit made me feel like a queen. I was comfortable and could move around easily, which was important as I had a lot of rituals to perform. The jewelry complemented my saree perfectly and made me feel like the most beautiful bride in the world.

Question and Answer

Q: Can an Indian bride wear a white wedding dress?

A: While it's not traditional for an Indian bride to wear a white wedding dress, there are no hard and fast rules. Some Indian brides choose to wear a white wedding dress as a way to incorporate western traditions into their wedding.

Q: Can an Indian bride wear a saree and a lehenga on her wedding day?

A: While it's not common for an Indian bride to wear both a saree and a lehenga on her wedding day, there are no rules against it. If you want to wear both, you should choose outfits that complement each other.

Q: Can an Indian bride wear a veil?

A: It's not traditional for an Indian bride to wear a veil, but some brides choose to wear one as a way to incorporate western traditions into their wedding.

Q: Can an Indian bride wear pants on her wedding day?

A: While it's not traditional for an Indian bride to wear pants on her wedding day, there are no rules against it. Some brides choose to wear pants as a way to incorporate their personal style into their wedding outfit.

Conclusion of What Does an Indian Bride Wear

Choosing the perfect outfit for your wedding day can be overwhelming, but it's also one of the most exciting parts of getting married. As an Indian bride, you have a plethora of options to choose from. Whether you decide to wear a saree, a lehenga, or a combination of both, make sure to choose an outfit that makes you feel comfortable, confident, and beautiful.
