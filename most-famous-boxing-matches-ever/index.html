The Fight of the Century took place on March 8, 1971, between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier. This was the first time that two undefeated heavyweight champions had faced each other in the ring. The Rumble in the Jungle took place on October 30, 1974, in Kinshasa, Zaire (now the Democratic Republic of Congo). The fight was between Muhammad Ali and George Foreman, who was the heavyweight champion at the time. This was one of the most famous boxing matches ever because Ali was considered the underdog going into the fight.

The Thrilla in Manila

The Thrilla in Manila took place on October 1, 1975, in Manila, Philippines. This fight was between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier, who had already fought twice before. This fight was the third and final meeting between the two boxers.

The Thrilla in Manila was an incredibly brutal fight, with both Ali and Frazier taking a lot of punishment. In the 14th round, Frazier's trainer stopped the fight, and Ali was declared the winner.
