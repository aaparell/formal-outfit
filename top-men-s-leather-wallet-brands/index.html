1. Bellroy

As someone who personally owns a Bellroy wallet, I can attest to the quality and durability of their products. Bellroy is an Australian brand that was founded in 2010. They specialize in slim and minimalist wallets that are perfect for those who want to carry their essentials without the bulk of a traditional wallet. One of the things that sets Bellroy apart is their focus on sustainability. They use environmentally-friendly materials and processes to create their products, making them a great choice for eco-conscious consumers. In addition, their products are backed by a three-year warranty, so you can trust that your Bellroy wallet will last for years to come.

2. Saddleback Leather

Saddleback Leather is an American brand that was founded in 2003. They're known for their rugged and durable leather goods, including wallets, bags, and accessories. All of their products are made from high-quality full-grain leather and are designed to last a lifetime. One of the unique things about Saddleback Leather is their commitment to transparency. They provide detailed information about the materials and processes used to create their products, so you can feel confident in your purchase. In addition, they offer a 100-year warranty on all of their products, which is a testament to their quality and durability.

3. Shinola

Shinola is a Detroit-based brand that was founded in 2011. They specialize in luxury leather goods, including wallets, watches, and bags. What sets Shinola apart is their dedication to American-made products and craftsmanship. All of their products are made in the USA by skilled artisans, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail. In addition to their focus on quality, Shinola is also committed to social responsibility. They partner with local organizations to create job opportunities and support the community. By purchasing a Shinola wallet, you're not only getting a high-quality product, but you're also supporting a brand that gives back.

4. Filson

Filson is a Seattle-based brand that was founded in 1897. They're known for their rugged and durable outdoor gear, including wallets, bags, and clothing. All of their products are made from high-quality materials and are designed to withstand even the toughest conditions. One of the things that sets Filson apart is their commitment to heritage and tradition. Many of their products are inspired by classic designs and are made using traditional techniques. This gives their products a timeless quality that you won't find with many other brands.
