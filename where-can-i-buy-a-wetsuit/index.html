Where Can I Buy A Wetsuit A Beginner s Guide

Written by Eveline May 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you planning to try out surfing or diving for the first time? Or maybe you're a seasoned water sports enthusiast looking for a new wetsuit? One of the most important things you'll need for any water activity is a good wetsuit. But where can you buy one?

Shopping for a wetsuit can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to water sports. You might not even know where to start or what to look for. It's essential to find the right wetsuit that fits well and is comfortable to wear, so you can enjoy your water activities without any worries.

There are several places where you can buy a wetsuit, both online and offline. Let's explore some of the most popular options:

1. Online Retailers
One of the easiest and most convenient ways to shop for a wetsuit is through online retailers. You can browse through a vast selection of wetsuits from the comfort of your home, compare prices and features, and read reviews from other customers. Some popular online retailers that offer wetsuits include Amazon, REI, and Backcountry.

2. Surf Shops
If you prefer to see and try on wetsuits in person, surf shops are a great place to go. Most surf shops carry a range of wetsuits, from beginner to professional level, and can provide expert advice on which wetsuit is best for you. Some popular surf shop chains include Surf Ride, Jack's Surfboards, and Hansen Surfboards.

3. Dive Shops
If you're into scuba diving, dive shops are your go-to place for wetsuits. Dive shops carry wetsuits that are specifically designed for diving and can provide advice on which wetsuit is best for your diving needs. Some popular dive shop chains include Scuba.com, Leisure Pro, and Divers Direct.

4. Sporting Goods Stores
Finally, sporting goods stores are another option for buying wetsuits. Stores like Dick's Sporting Goods, Sports Authority, and Academy Sports carry wetsuits for various water sports, including surfing, diving, and swimming.

My Personal Experience Buying a Wetsuit Online

When I decided to try surfing for the first time, I knew I needed to buy a wetsuit. However, I had no idea where to start since I had never bought one before. I did some research online and found that Amazon had a great selection of wetsuits at reasonable prices.

I was hesitant to buy a wetsuit online since I couldn't try it on first. But Amazon had a good return policy, and I found a wetsuit with many positive reviews from other customers. I ordered the wetsuit, and when it arrived, I was pleasantly surprised at how well it fit and how comfortable it was to wear. I've been using the same wetsuit for several years now!

Tips for Buying the Right Wetsuit

When shopping for a wetsuit, keep the following tips in mind:

1. Know Your Measurements
Wetsuits come in different sizes, so it's essential to know your measurements before buying one. Measure your chest, waist, hips, and inseam to find the right size.

2. Consider the Water Temperature
Wetsuits come in different thicknesses, which affects the level of warmth they provide. Consider the water temperature of the place where you'll be using the wetsuit and choose a thickness accordingly.

3. Check for Comfort
Make sure the wetsuit fits well and is comfortable to wear. You'll be wearing it for several hours, so it's essential to choose one that doesn't restrict your movement or cause any discomfort.

How to Take Care of Your Wetsuit

Proper care and maintenance of your wetsuit can extend its lifespan and keep it in good condition. Here are some tips:

1. Rinse it with Fresh Water
After using your wetsuit, rinse it with fresh water to remove any sand, salt, or chlorine. This will prevent any buildup that can damage the wetsuit over time.

2. Hang it to Dry
Hang your wetsuit to dry in a shaded area, away from direct sunlight. Do not use a dryer or hang it in direct sunlight, as this can damage the wetsuit's material.

3. Store it Properly
Store your wetsuit in a dry and cool place, away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Do not fold or crease the wetsuit, as this can damage the material.

Conclusion of Where Can I Buy a Wetsuit

Shopping for a wetsuit can seem daunting, but it doesn't have to be. Whether you choose to buy online or in-store, make sure to consider your measurements, water temperature, and comfort level when choosing a wetsuit. With proper care and maintenance, your wetsuit can last for several years and keep you comfortable during your water activities.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I rent a wetsuit instead of buying one?
A: Yes, many surf and dive shops offer wetsuit rentals. This can be a good option if you're trying out a new water sport or don't want to invest in a wetsuit just yet.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a wetsuit?
A: The cost of a wetsuit can vary depending on the brand, quality, and features. You can find wetsuits for as low as $50 and as high as $500. It's important to choose a wetsuit that fits your budget and needs.

Q: Can I wear a wetsuit for swimming?
A: Yes, you can wear a wetsuit for swimming. Wetsuits provide extra buoyancy and keep you warm in cold water, which can be beneficial for open water swimming.

Q: How do I know if my wetsuit fits well?
A: Your wetsuit should fit snugly but not be too tight. You should be able to move your arms and legs freely without any restrictions. The wetsuit should also cover your entire torso and fit comfortably around your neck, wrists, and ankles.
