How To Sell Clothes Second Hand A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to declutter your closet and make some extra cash? Selling your clothes second hand is a great way to do just that. However, the process can seem daunting if you've never done it before. From deciding which items to sell to finding the right platform to sell on, there are several pain points to consider when it comes to selling clothes second hand.

If you're wondering how to sell clothes second hand, you've come to the right place. In this beginner's guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to successfully sell your clothes online.

To start, let's summarize the main points of this article. We'll cover the following topics related to how to sell clothes second hand:

  • Why sell clothes second hand
  • Choosing which items to sell
  • Where to sell clothes second hand
  • Pricing your items
  • Shipping and handling

Why Sell Clothes Second Hand?

Before we dive into the nitty-gritty of how to sell clothes second hand, it's important to understand why it's a good idea. First of all, selling your clothes second hand is an eco-friendly choice. By keeping your clothes in circulation, you're reducing waste and carbon emissions. Additionally, selling your clothes can help you earn some extra money and free up space in your closet. It's a win-win situation!

Personally, I started selling my clothes second hand when I noticed my closet was overflowing with items I no longer wore. I didn't want to just donate everything, as some pieces were still in great condition and worth some money. Selling my clothes online allowed me to earn some extra cash while also decluttering my space.

Choosing Which Items to Sell

When it comes to choosing which items to sell, there are a few factors to consider. First of all, only sell items that are in good condition. If an item is stained, ripped, or overly worn, it's not likely to sell. Additionally, consider the seasonality of the item. For example, a heavy winter coat is more likely to sell in the fall or winter than in the summer.

Personally, I like to go through my closet and make two piles: one for items I will keep, and one for items I want to sell. I take into consideration the condition and seasonality of each item when making my decisions.

Where to Sell Clothes Second Hand

There are several online platforms you can use to sell your clothes second hand. Some popular options include:

  • Poshmark
  • Depop
  • ThredUp
  • eBay

Each platform has its own pros and cons, so it's worth doing some research to decide which one is right for you. Personally, I've had the most success with Poshmark. The interface is user-friendly and there's a large community of buyers and sellers.

Pricing Your Items

When it comes to pricing your items, it's important to be realistic. Take into consideration the brand, condition, and original price of the item. Additionally, look at what similar items are selling for on the platform you've chosen. Remember, buyers are looking for a good deal, so don't overprice your items.

Personally, I like to price my items slightly higher than what I'm hoping to get for them. This allows for some wiggle room for negotiations.

Shipping and Handling

Once you've made a sale, it's time to ship your item. Make sure to package it carefully and securely to avoid any damage during transit. Additionally, consider offering free shipping to entice buyers. If you do charge for shipping, make sure to factor in the cost of shipping materials and postage.

Personally, I like to use recycled materials for packaging whenever possible. It's an eco-friendly choice and also saves money on shipping materials.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I take good photos of my clothes?

A: Make sure to take photos in good lighting and from multiple angles. It's also helpful to include a photo of the tag with the brand and size information.

Q: How do I handle returns?

A: Make sure to include your return policy in your listing. If a buyer wants to return an item, be prompt and courteous in handling their request.

Q: How do I build a following on my chosen platform?

A: Engage with other users on the platform by liking and commenting on their listings. Additionally, share your listings on social media to reach a wider audience.

Q: Do I need to pay taxes on my earnings?

A: Yes, you will need to report your earnings on your taxes. Make sure to keep track of all sales and expenses related to selling your clothes second hand.

Conclusion of How To Sell Clothes Second Hand

Selling your clothes second hand can be a great way to earn some extra cash and declutter your space. By following these tips, you'll be well on your way to successfully selling your clothes online. Remember to choose items in good condition, research your platform options, price your items realistically, and package them carefully for shipping. Happy selling!
