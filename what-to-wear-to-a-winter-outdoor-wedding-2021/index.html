What to Wear to a Winter Outdoor Wedding 2021: Tips and Ideas

Winter weddings are magical, but they can also be challenging when it comes to choosing the right outfit. Attending a winter outdoor wedding in 2021 can be even more challenging, as you need to balance style, comfort, and warmth, while also considering the outdoor setting. If you're wondering what to wear to a winter outdoor wedding 2021, read on for tips and ideas that will help you look and feel your best.

When it comes to winter outdoor weddings, the key is to dress in layers. You want to be warm enough to brave the cold, but not so bundled up that you can't move or dance. Start with a warm and cozy base layer, such as a silk or thermal top and leggings, and then add a dress or skirt and blouse on top. Choose fabrics that are warm and cozy, such as velvet, wool, or cashmere, and opt for darker colors that are appropriate for winter, such as burgundy, navy, or emerald green.

To keep your feet warm, consider wearing closed-toe shoes or boots with a low heel. You can also wear tights or leggings underneath a dress or skirt to keep your legs warm. Accessorize with scarves, gloves, and hats that match your outfit, and don't forget a warm coat or jacket to wear outside. A cape or wrap can also be a stylish and practical option.

Personal Experience: Finding the Perfect Winter Wedding Outfit

When I attended a winter outdoor wedding last year, I knew I wanted to stay warm without sacrificing style. I opted for a long-sleeved velvet dress in a deep burgundy color, paired with black tights, ankle boots, and a faux fur stole. I also wore a wool coat with a fur-trimmed hood, which kept me warm and cozy during the outdoor ceremony. The key was to choose fabrics that were warm and cozy, but also elegant and chic.

Tips for Men: What to Wear to a Winter Outdoor Wedding 2021

Men can also face the challenge of dressing for a winter outdoor wedding. The key is to choose a suit or jacket in a warm fabric, such as wool or tweed, and pair it with a shirt and tie in a darker color, such as navy or charcoal. You can also add a vest or sweater for extra warmth, and wear dress shoes with thick socks. A wool overcoat or pea coat can also be a stylish and practical addition.

Accessories and Makeup Tips for Winter Outdoor Weddings

When it comes to accessories and makeup for a winter outdoor wedding, the key is to keep it simple and elegant. Opt for classic jewelry pieces, such as pearl earrings or a simple necklace, and choose a natural makeup look with a pop of color on the lips or cheeks. You can also add a touch of sparkle with a glittery clutch or hair accessory, but don't overdo it.

How to Stay Warm and Comfortable at a Winter Outdoor Wedding

Staying warm and comfortable at a winter outdoor wedding is essential for enjoying the day. In addition to dressing in layers and choosing warm fabrics, you can also bring hand warmers, a thermos of hot cocoa or tea, and a blanket to snuggle up in during the outdoor ceremony. You can also take breaks indoors if the reception is in a heated venue.

Personal Experience: Choosing the Right Winter Wedding Accessories

When I attended a winter outdoor wedding, I opted for a simple gold necklace and earrings, and a red lip for a pop of color. I also brought a cozy blanket to wrap around me during the outdoor ceremony, and a thermos of hot tea to stay warm. The key was to choose accessories that were practical and stylish, without being too over-the-top.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a sleeveless dress to a winter outdoor wedding?

A: It depends on the temperature and the dress code. If it's a formal wedding and you'll be spending a lot of time outdoors, it's better to choose a dress with sleeves or bring a wrap or coat to wear on top.

Q: Can I wear white to a winter wedding?

A: It's best to avoid white or ivory if you're attending a winter wedding, as these colors are traditionally reserved for the bride. Opt for darker colors or jewel tones instead.

Q: What kind of coat should I wear to a winter outdoor wedding?

A: A wool or cashmere coat, a fur cape or wrap, or a puffer coat with a stylish design can all be good options for a winter outdoor wedding. Choose a coat that complements your outfit and keeps you warm.

Q: Can I wear boots to a winter outdoor wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear boots to a winter outdoor wedding, as long as they're dressy and appropriate for the occasion. Opt for ankle boots or knee-high boots in leather or suede, and pair them with tights or leggings.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Winter Outdoor Wedding 2021

Attending a winter outdoor wedding can be a fun and memorable experience, as long as you're dressed appropriately. The key is to balance style, comfort, and warmth, by choosing warm fabrics, layering your outfit, and accessorizing wisely. With these tips and ideas in mind, you'll be ready to enjoy the winter wedding season in style.
