Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage dress is authentic?

A: You can look for labels or tags in the garment that indicate the era or designer. Additionally, you can research the style and era of the dress to ensure that it is authentic.

Q: How do I care for a vintage dress?

A: It is important to read the care instructions on the label of the garment. Additionally, you should avoid washing vintage dresses in the washing machine and opt for handwashing or dry cleaning.

Q: How do I know if a vintage dress will fit me?

A: It is important to check the measurements of the vintage dress before making a purchase. Additionally, you can contact the seller and ask for additional measurements or photos of the garment.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a vintage dress?

A: The price of a vintage dress can vary depending on the era, designer, and condition of the garment. You should expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $500 for a quality vintage dress.
