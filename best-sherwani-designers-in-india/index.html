Discovering The Best Sherwani Designers In India A Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 02, 2023 · 5 min read

India is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and traditional clothing, and one such attire that is gaining popularity among men is the sherwani. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is usually worn for special occasions like weddings, festivals, and traditional ceremonies. However, finding the perfect sherwani that fits your style, budget, and occasion can be challenging. In this article, we will explore the best sherwani designers in India, their specialties, and how you can choose the right one for you.

When it comes to finding the best sherwani designers in India, the process can be overwhelming as there are numerous options available. From classic designs to contemporary styles, each designer has their unique touch that sets them apart. Moreover, choosing the right designer can also depend on your budget, the occasion, and your personal preference. However, with the right information, you can make an informed decision and find the perfect sherwani that suits your needs.

If you are looking for the best sherwani designers in India, start by researching online. Many designers have an online presence, and you can view their portfolio, read customer reviews and compare prices. Additionally, you can also visit local markets like Chandni Chowk in Delhi, or Colaba Causeway in Mumbai, to explore local designers and find affordable options.

Top Sherwani Designers in India

Here are some of the best sherwani designers in India that you can consider:

Sabyasachi Mukherjee

Sabyasachi Mukherjee is one of India's most renowned fashion designers and has designed sherwanis for many high-profile weddings. His designs are a blend of traditional and modern styles that appeal to the modern Indian groom. From intricate embroidery to elegant prints, his sherwanis are a perfect fit for a lavish wedding.

Tarun Tahiliani

Tarun Tahiliani is another popular designer who is known for his fusion of traditional and contemporary styles. His sherwanis are a perfect blend of Indian and Western designs and are ideal for those who want to experiment with their wedding attire.

Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra is a favorite among Bollywood celebrities and is known for his exquisite designs that are perfect for a grand wedding. His sherwanis are a blend of traditional and modern styles and are ideal for those who want to make a statement.

Abu Jani Sandeep Khosla

Abu Jani Sandeep Khosla is a designer duo who has designed for many high-profile weddings. Their sherwanis are a fusion of traditional and modern designs and are perfect for those who want to make a lasting impression.

When choosing the best sherwani designer in India, it is essential to keep in mind your budget, the occasion, and your personal style. Additionally, you should also try on different styles and designs to see what suits you the best. With the right designer, you can make a statement and look your best on your special day.

How to Choose the Best Sherwani Designer in India

Choosing the best sherwani designer in India can be a daunting task. However, here are a few things that you can keep in mind when making your decision:

1. Budget - Before you start your search, it is essential to set a budget for your sherwani. This will help you narrow down your options and find a designer that suits your budget.

2. Occasion - Sherwanis are usually worn for special occasions like weddings or festivals. Therefore, it is essential to choose a designer that specializes in occasion wear and can provide you with the perfect sherwani for your special day.

3. Personal Style - Each designer has their unique touch when it comes to designing sherwanis. Therefore, it is essential to choose a designer that resonates with your personal style and can provide you with a sherwani that reflects your personality.

FAQs: Best Sherwani Designers in India

Q1. How much does a sherwani from a top designer in India cost?

A. The cost of a sherwani from a top designer in India can range from INR 50,000 to INR 5,00,000 or more, depending on the designer and the design.

Q2. How long does it take to get a sherwani designed by a top designer in India?

A. The time taken to design a sherwani by a top designer in India can range from a few weeks to a few months, depending on the designer's schedule and the complexity of the design.

Q3. Can I customize my sherwani design with a top designer in India?

A. Yes, most top designers in India offer customization services where you can choose your design, fabric, and embroidery details.

Q4. Where can I find the best sherwani designers in India?

A. You can find the best sherwani designers in India by researching online, visiting local markets, or asking for recommendations from friends or family.

Conclusion of Best Sherwani Designers in India

Choosing the right sherwani designer in India is crucial to making a lasting impression on your special day. By keeping in mind your budget, the occasion, and your personal style, you can find the perfect sherwani designer that suits your needs. With the right designer, you can make a statement and look your best on your big day.
