The Most Valuable Wwf Magazines A Guide For Wrestling Fans

Written by Christine Jul 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a wrestling fan looking to add some rare items to your collection? Do you want to know which WWF magazines are worth the most money? If so, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll take a deep dive into the world of valuable WWF magazines and give you all the information you need to know.

When it comes to valuable WWF magazines, there are a few things to consider. First, the magazine should be in excellent condition with no tears, creases, or markings. Second, it should feature a popular wrestler on the cover or have an interesting story inside. Third, it should be a rare edition that is hard to find.

Overall, the most valuable WWF magazines are those that are in high demand among collectors. These magazines can fetch hundreds or even thousands of dollars at auction, depending on their rarity and condition.

The History of WWF Magazines

WWF magazines have been around since the 1980s and were a popular way for fans to keep up with their favorite wrestlers and storylines. These magazines featured exclusive interviews, behind-the-scenes looks, and in-depth coverage of the biggest matches and events.

Over the years, the format of WWF magazines changed, with some editions featuring more photos and less text, while others focused on specific wrestlers or storylines. Despite these changes, WWF magazines remained a staple of wrestling fandom and continue to be popular among collectors today.

The Most Valuable WWF Magazines

So, which WWF magazines are the most valuable? Here are a few to look out for:

1. WWF Magazine, November 1987

This edition of WWF Magazine features Hulk Hogan and Andre the Giant on the cover and includes an exclusive interview with Hogan. It is also known for its rare poster of the Mega Powers (Hogan and Randy Savage) inside. This magazine can sell for up to $500 in excellent condition.

2. WWF Magazine, February 1988

Another edition featuring Hulk Hogan, this magazine includes a rare poster of Hogan and Andre the Giant. It also includes an article about the WrestleMania IV tournament, which Hogan won. This magazine can sell for up to $400 in excellent condition.

3. WWF Magazine, May 1990

This edition of WWF Magazine features The Ultimate Warrior on the cover and includes an exclusive interview with him. It also includes a poster of The Ultimate Warrior and a pull-out centerfold of Miss Elizabeth. This magazine can sell for up to $300 in excellent condition.

4. WWF Magazine, December 1991

This edition of WWF Magazine features Ric Flair on the cover and includes an exclusive interview with him. It also includes a poster of Flair and a pull-out centerfold of Miss Elizabeth. This magazine can sell for up to $200 in excellent condition.

My Personal Experience with Valuable WWF Magazines

As a lifelong wrestling fan and collector, I have had the pleasure of owning a few valuable WWF magazines over the years. One of my favorites is the November 1987 edition featuring Hulk Hogan and Andre the Giant. Not only is it a rare and valuable edition, but it also holds a special place in wrestling history as the magazine that helped build the hype for WrestleMania III.

Tips for Finding Valuable WWF Magazines

If you're looking to add some valuable WWF magazines to your collection, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Check online auction sites like eBay for rare editions.

Many collectors sell their rare WWF magazines on eBay, so it's a great place to start your search. Just be sure to read the seller's description carefully and ask any questions you may have before making a purchase.

2. Attend wrestling conventions and flea markets.

Wrestling conventions and flea markets are a treasure trove of wrestling memorabilia, including rare WWF magazines. Be sure to bring cash and be prepared to haggle for the best price.

3. Ask other wrestling fans and collectors for recommendations.

Networking with other wrestling fans and collectors can be a great way to find rare and valuable WWF magazines. Join online forums and groups and ask for recommendations.

Question and Answer

Q: Are all old WWF magazines valuable?

A: No, not all old WWF magazines are valuable. The value of a magazine depends on its rarity, condition, and demand among collectors.

Q: How can I tell if a WWF magazine is valuable?

A: Look for editions featuring popular wrestlers, rare posters or centerfolds, and unique stories. Check online auction sites and wrestling conventions for pricing information.

Q: Can I sell my old WWF magazines?

A: Yes, you can sell your old WWF magazines. Check online auction sites and wrestling memorabilia dealers for pricing information.

Q: What other wrestling memorabilia is valuable?

A: Other valuable wrestling memorabilia includes autographed items, championship belts, and rare action figures.

Conclusion of Most Valuable WWF Magazines

Valuable WWF magazines can be a great addition to any wrestling fan's collection. By keeping an eye out for rare editions featuring popular wrestlers, rare posters or centerfolds, and unique stories, you can add some rare and valuable items to your collection. Whether you're a casual fan or a serious collector, there's never been a better time to start your search for valuable WWF magazines.
