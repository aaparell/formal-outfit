The Best Places To Eat In Columbus A Guide To Delicious Dining

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in Columbus and looking for a great place to eat, you're in luck! Columbus is home to some of the best restaurants in Ohio, serving up everything from classic American fare to international cuisine. But with so many options, it can be hard to know where to start. In this post, we'll highlight some of the best places to eat in Columbus, so you can spend less time searching and more time enjoying delicious food.

When it comes to finding the best places to eat in Columbus, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider what type of cuisine you're in the mood for. Columbus has everything from Italian to Japanese to Mexican, so you're sure to find something that suits your tastes. Second, think about your budget. While there are plenty of high-end restaurants in Columbus, there are also plenty of affordable options. Finally, don't be afraid to try something new! Some of the best dining experiences in Columbus come from trying a new restaurant or dish.

Overall, the best places to eat in Columbus are those that offer delicious food, great service, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're in the mood for a casual lunch or a fancy dinner, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city.

The Top Italian Restaurant: Marcella's

Marcella's is a beloved Italian restaurant in the Short North neighborhood of Columbus. The menu features classic Italian dishes like spaghetti carbonara and chicken parmigiana, as well as more unique offerings like goat cheese and fig pizza. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, with dim lighting and rustic decor. Prices are reasonable, with most entrees under $20. Overall, Marcella's is a must-visit for anyone looking for delicious Italian food in Columbus.

The Best Mexican Restaurant: Condado Tacos

Condado Tacos is a popular chain restaurant with several locations throughout Columbus. The menu features build-your-own tacos and burritos, with a wide variety of toppings to choose from. The atmosphere is casual and fun, with colorful decor and upbeat music. Prices are reasonable, with most tacos and burritos under $10. Overall, Condado Tacos is a great choice for anyone looking for delicious Mexican food in a laid-back setting.

More Great Places to Eat in Columbus

While Marcella's and Condado Tacos are two of the best places to eat in Columbus, there are plenty of other great options to choose from. Here are a few more restaurants to check out:

1. Northstar Cafe

Northstar Cafe is a popular restaurant with several locations throughout Columbus. The menu features healthy, organic dishes like salads, sandwiches, and smoothies. The atmosphere is bright and airy, with plenty of natural light and colorful decor. Prices are reasonable, with most entrees under $15.

2. The Pearl

The Pearl is a trendy restaurant in the Short North neighborhood of Columbus. The menu features classic American dishes like burgers and fried chicken, as well as more upscale offerings like seafood and steak. The atmosphere is chic and modern, with dim lighting and sleek decor. Prices are on the higher side, with most entrees over $20.

3. Katalina's

Katalina's is a cozy cafe in the German Village neighborhood of Columbus. The menu features breakfast dishes like pancakes and waffles, as well as unique offerings like pancake balls and sweet and spicy bacon. The atmosphere is charming and rustic, with brick walls and vintage decor. Prices are reasonable, with most dishes under $15.

Conclusion of Best Places to Eat in Columbus

Whether you're a Columbus local or just passing through, there's no shortage of great places to eat in this vibrant city. From classic Italian to trendy American, there's something for every taste and budget. So why not try something new and explore the culinary delights of Columbus?
