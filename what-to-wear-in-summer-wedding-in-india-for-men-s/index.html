What To Wear In Summer Wedding In India For Men s A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer weddings in India are a big deal. With the soaring heat and humidity, it can be quite a challenge to dress up and look good without feeling uncomfortable. As a result, selecting the right attire for a summer wedding in India can be a daunting task for men. However, with the right guidance, you can easily ace your summer wedding look without breaking a sweat. In this post, we'll guide you through what to wear in summer wedding in India for men's.

Pain Points of What to Wear in Summer Wedding in India for Men's

When it comes to summer weddings in India, there are a few common pain points that men face while selecting their attire. Firstly, the heat and humidity can make it challenging to choose an outfit that is comfortable and breathable. Secondly, men often struggle with selecting the right colors and fabrics that are appropriate for a summer wedding. Lastly, finding the right accessories to complement the outfit can also be a challenge.

What to Wear in Summer Wedding in India for Men's: The Guide

When it comes to summer wedding attire for men in India, the key is to strike a balance between style and comfort. Here's a breakdown of what you should wear:

The Outfit

For a summer wedding in India, opt for light-colored and breathable fabrics such as cotton, linen, and khadi. A linen or cotton suit in light colors such as beige, light grey, or pastel shades is a perfect choice for a summer wedding. You can also opt for a kurta-pyjama or a bandhgala suit in light colors and fabrics. Avoid wearing black or dark-colored attire as it can absorb heat and make you uncomfortable.

The Accessories

When it comes to accessories, keep it minimalistic and functional. Opt for a light-colored pocket square that complements your outfit. For footwear, opt for sandals or loafers in light colors. Avoid wearing closed shoes or boots as they can make your feet sweaty and uncomfortable. Lastly, don't forget to wear a hat or sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun.

Personal Experience with What to Wear in Summer Wedding in India for Men's

As someone who has attended several summer weddings in India, I can vouch for the importance of selecting the right attire. Last year, I attended a wedding in Jaipur, and the temperature was soaring. I opted for a beige linen suit and paired it with a light-colored kurta. I accessorized my outfit with a light-colored pocket square and a pair of brown leather sandals. Not only did I look stylish, but I also felt comfortable throughout the wedding.

Tips for What to Wear in Summer Wedding in India for Men's

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting your summer wedding attire:

Stay Hydrated

Make sure you stay hydrated throughout the wedding. Carry a bottle of water with you and sip on it frequently.

Avoid Heavy Fragrances

During a summer wedding, heavy fragrances can be overpowering. Opt for light fragrances or colognes that are refreshing and subtle.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I wear a tuxedo to a summer wedding in India?

A. No, a tuxedo is not appropriate for a summer wedding in India. Opt for a lighter and breathable fabric such as cotton or linen.

Q. Can I wear shorts to a summer wedding in India?

A. No, shorts are not appropriate for a summer wedding in India. Stick to traditional Indian attire such as a kurta-pyjama or a bandhgala suit.

Q. Can I wear a hat to a summer wedding in India?

A. Yes, wearing a hat or sunglasses is a great way to protect yourself from the sun during a summer wedding in India.

Q. Can I wear sneakers to a summer wedding in India?

A. No, sneakers are not appropriate for a summer wedding in India. Opt for sandals or loafers in light colors.

Conclusion of What to Wear in Summer Wedding in India for Men's

Dressing up for a summer wedding in India can be overwhelming, but with the right guidance, you can easily ace your summer wedding look. Remember to opt for light-colored and breathable fabrics, keep your accessories minimalistic and functional, and stay hydrated throughout the wedding. By following these tips, you'll look stylish and feel comfortable throughout the wedding.
