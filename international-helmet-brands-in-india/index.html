International Helmet Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine May 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Riding a bike or a scooter is a popular mode of transportation in India. However, it is important to ensure that you stay safe while riding. One of the most important safety measures is wearing a helmet. While there are many Indian brands that offer helmets, some people prefer international helmet brands in India.

People who opt for international helmet brands in India are often looking for the best quality and safety features. They may also be looking for a specific style or design that is not available in Indian brands. However, there are some pain points associated with international helmet brands in India, such as availability and price.

The target of international helmet brands in India is to provide high-quality helmets with advanced safety features. These brands often invest heavily in research and development to create helmets that offer maximum protection to the riders. They also focus on designing helmets that are comfortable to wear and provide good visibility.

In summary, international helmet brands in India offer superior quality and safety features, although they may be more expensive and less widely available than Indian brands.

Top International Helmet Brands in India

One of the most popular international helmet brands in India is Shoei. This Japanese brand is known for its high-quality helmets that offer excellent protection and comfort. Another popular brand is AGV, an Italian brand that offers a range of helmets for different types of riders. Arai is another Japanese brand that is known for its advanced safety features and innovative design.

Personally, I have been using a Shoei helmet for the past few years and I am extremely satisfied with its performance. It is lightweight and comfortable to wear, and provides excellent visibility on the road.

Availability of International Helmet Brands in India

One of the challenges of buying international helmet brands in India is the limited availability. While some brands may be available at select dealerships, others may only be available online. This can make it difficult to try on helmets before purchasing them, which is an important factor in ensuring a good fit.

Price Range of International Helmet Brands in India

International helmet brands in India are generally more expensive than Indian brands. The price range can vary depending on the brand and the specific model. For example, a Shoei helmet can range from Rs. 25,000 to Rs. 50,000, while an AGV helmet can range from Rs. 15,000 to Rs. 40,000. However, it is important to remember that a high-quality helmet is an investment in your safety.

How to Choose the Right International Helmet Brand in India

When choosing an international helmet brand in India, it is important to consider the following factors:

  • Safety features
  • Comfort and fit
  • Visibility
  • Design and style
  • Price

It is also important to ensure that the helmet meets the safety standards set by the government. Look for certification marks such as ISI or DOT before making a purchase.

My Experience with International Helmet Brands in India

As mentioned earlier, I have been using a Shoei helmet for the past few years and I am extremely satisfied with its performance. The helmet is very comfortable to wear and provides excellent visibility. The only downside is the price, which is on the higher side. However, I believe that it is worth the investment for my safety.

Question and Answer

Q. Are international helmet brands in India better than Indian brands?

A. International helmet brands in India offer superior quality and safety features, although they may be more expensive and less widely available than Indian brands.

Q. Which is the best international helmet brand in India?

A. Some of the best international helmet brands in India include Shoei, AGV, and Arai.

Q. Are international helmets worth the investment?

A. Yes, a high-quality helmet is an investment in your safety and can provide superior protection in the event of an accident.

Q. How can I ensure that my international helmet fits properly?

A. It is important to try on the helmet before purchasing it to ensure a good fit. Look for helmets with adjustable padding and straps to customize the fit.

Conclusion of International Helmet Brands in India

International helmet brands in India offer superior quality and safety features, although they may be more expensive and less widely available than Indian brands. When choosing an international helmet brand, it is important to consider safety features, comfort and fit, visibility, design and style, and price. Remember to look for certification marks to ensure that the helmet meets safety standards set by the government.
