Web Blossom

Web Blossom is one of the best suit mods in Spider-Man PS4. It allows you to perform a powerful area-of-effect attack that can knock down enemies and deal significant damage. With this suit mod, you can quickly take out groups of enemies or clear out an area when you're surrounded.

Bullet Proof is another excellent suit mod that can increase your survivability in combat. What is the best way to unlock suit mods?

The best way to unlock suit mods in Spider-Man PS4 is by completing missions and collecting tokens. Once you have enough tokens, you can purchase suit mods from the in-game store.
