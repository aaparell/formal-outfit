What are the Most Sustainable Companies in the Netherlands?

The most sustainable companies in the Netherlands are those that prioritize environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. Royal DSM is a global science-based company that is committed to sustainability. The company has set ambitious goals to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions and improve the sustainability of its supply chain. In addition, Royal DSM has been recognized for its commitment to promoting diversity and inclusion in the workplace.

2. Triodos Bank

Triodos Bank is a sustainable bank that only invests in projects and companies that have a positive impact on society and the environment. The bank is committed to transparency and ethical banking practices, and it has been recognized for its efforts to promote sustainability in the financial sector.

3. Interface

Interface is a global commercial flooring company that is committed to sustainability. The company has set a goal to become carbon negative by 2040, and it has implemented a number of sustainable practices throughout its supply chain. In addition, Interface is committed to promoting social equity and has been recognized for its efforts to improve the lives of people in the communities where it operates.

4. Tony's Chocolonely

Tony's Chocolonely is a Dutch chocolate company that is committed to ending slavery and child labor in the cocoa industry. The company sources its cocoa beans directly from farmers in Ghana and the Ivory Coast, and it pays them a fair price for their products. In addition, Tony's Chocolonely is committed to reducing its environmental impact and promoting sustainable farming practices.
