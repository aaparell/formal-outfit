The Ultimate Guide To Wedding Suit Brands In India

Written by Bardi Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Weddings are a special occasion that require special attire. As a groom, you want to feel confident, stylish, and comfortable on your big day. However, with so many wedding suit brands in India to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect suit that fits your budget, style, and preferences.

In this article, we will discuss the top wedding suit brands in India, their target audience, price range, and unique selling points. By the end of this guide, you'll have a better understanding of what to look for when shopping for a wedding suit and which brands are worth considering.

Let's dive in!

Raymond

Raymond is a well-known brand in India that has been producing high-quality suits for over 90 years. Their target audience is men who want a classic, timeless look that never goes out of style. Their suits are made with premium fabrics such as wool, silk, and linen, and are available in a variety of colors, patterns, and styles. Raymond suits are priced between Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 50,000, making them accessible to a wide range of budgets.

My personal experience with Raymond was excellent. Their customer service was top-notch, and they helped me find the perfect suit that complemented my skin tone and body shape. I felt confident and comfortable throughout the wedding ceremony and reception.

Manyavar

Manyavar is a popular brand in India that specializes in men's ethnic wear. Their target audience is men who want to showcase their cultural heritage and personality through their wedding attire. Manyavar suits are made with high-quality fabrics such as silk, brocade, and velvet, and are adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments. Manyavar suits are priced between Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 50,000, making them affordable for all budgets.

When I got married, I chose Manyavar for my wedding suit. I wanted to honor my cultural heritage and wear something that reflected my personal style. The suit was comfortable, breathable, and looked stunning in the wedding photos.

Peter England

Peter England is a popular brand in India that offers a wide range of men's clothing, including suits. Their target audience is men who want a modern, trendy look that is affordable and accessible. Peter England suits are made with high-quality fabrics such as wool and polyester, and are available in a variety of colors, patterns, and styles. Peter England suits are priced between Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 20,000, making them a great value for the price.

How to Choose the Right Wedding Suit Brand in India

Choosing the right wedding suit brand in India can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some factors to consider when choosing a wedding suit brand:

1. Budget - Determine how much you're willing to spend on a suit and choose a brand that fits your budget.

2. Style - Determine what style of suit you want and choose a brand that specializes in that style.

3. Quality - Look for a brand that uses high-quality fabrics and materials to ensure your suit looks and feels great.

4. Customer service - Choose a brand that offers excellent customer service and can help you find the perfect suit for your needs.

Conclusion of Wedding Suit Brands in India

When it comes to choosing a wedding suit brand in India, there are many options to consider. Whether you want a classic, timeless look or a modern, trendy style, there is a brand out there that can meet your needs. By considering factors such as budget, style, quality, and customer service, you can find the perfect suit that makes you feel confident, stylish, and comfortable on your big day.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a wedding suit brand is right for me?

A: Consider factors such as budget, style, quality, and customer service when choosing a wedding suit brand. Look for a brand that meets your needs and makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Q: Can I customize my wedding suit?

A: Many wedding suit brands offer customization options such as choosing the fabric, color, pattern, and style of your suit. Contact the brand to see what customization options they offer.

Q: How far in advance should I buy my wedding suit?

A: It's recommended to buy your wedding suit at least two months in advance to allow enough time for alterations and fittings.

Q: Can I rent a wedding suit instead of buying one?

A: Yes, many wedding suit brands offer rental options for grooms who don't want to purchase a suit. Contact the brand to see what rental options they offer.
