Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands A Guide To Eco Friendly Fashion

Written by Oliver May 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to make a difference in the world by supporting eco-friendly fashion? Are you tired of fast fashion and its negative impact on the environment? If yes, then sustainable Indian clothing brands are the way to go!

Many people are unaware of the environmental and social impact of the fashion industry. From the use of toxic chemicals to the exploitation of workers, fast fashion has a long list of negative effects. Sustainable Indian clothing brands aim to address these issues by creating eco-friendly and socially responsible fashion.

The target of sustainable Indian clothing brands is to create clothing that is made with organic and natural materials, such as cotton and linen. These materials are free from harmful chemicals and pesticides, making them safe for the environment and the people who wear them. Additionally, sustainable Indian clothing brands prioritize fair labor practices, ensuring that workers are paid a fair wage and work in safe conditions.

In this article, we will introduce you to some of the best sustainable Indian clothing brands and provide you with a guide to eco-friendly fashion.

Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands: Fabindia

One of the most popular sustainable Indian clothing brands is Fabindia. Founded in 1960, Fabindia is dedicated to preserving Indian crafts and textiles while promoting sustainable and ethical practices. They use natural and organic materials and work with skilled artisans across India to create high-quality and unique pieces. I personally own several pieces from Fabindia and have always been impressed by the quality and style.

Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands: The Good Loom

Another great sustainable Indian clothing brand is The Good Loom. Founded by Himani and Rupal, two sisters who were born into a family of textile manufacturers, The Good Loom is committed to creating handcrafted and eco-friendly clothing. They use natural dyes and sustainable fabrics, such as khadi and linen, to create beautiful and timeless pieces.

The Benefits of Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands

By supporting sustainable Indian clothing brands, you are making a positive impact on the environment and on the lives of workers. Sustainable clothing is made with natural and organic materials, which are free from harmful chemicals and pesticides. This means that the clothes are safe for you to wear and for the environment. Additionally, sustainable Indian clothing brands prioritize fair labor practices, ensuring that workers are paid a fair wage and work in safe conditions.

How to Shop for Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands

When shopping for sustainable Indian clothing brands, it's important to look for brands that prioritize eco-friendly and fair labor practices. Look for clothes made with natural and organic materials, such as cotton and linen, and avoid clothes made with synthetic materials, such as polyester. You can also look for certifications, such as GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), which ensure that the clothes are made using sustainable and ethical practices.

Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands: Anita Dongre

Anita Dongre is another popular sustainable Indian clothing brand. Known for her beautiful and feminine designs, Anita Dongre is committed to creating sustainable and ethical fashion. She uses natural and organic materials and works with skilled artisans in India to create unique and high-quality pieces.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some other sustainable Indian clothing brands?

A: Other sustainable Indian clothing brands include Bhu:Sattva, No Nasties, and Ethicus.

Q: Are sustainable Indian clothing brands more expensive?

A: Sustainable Indian clothing brands can be more expensive than fast fashion brands, but they are often higher quality and made to last. Additionally, by investing in sustainable clothing, you are supporting ethical and eco-friendly practices.

Q: How can I learn more about sustainable fashion?

A: There are many resources available online, such as the Sustainable Apparel Coalition and the Good On You app, that can provide information on sustainable fashion brands and practices.

Q: How can I support sustainable Indian clothing brands?

A: You can support sustainable Indian clothing brands by choosing to shop from them and spreading awareness about eco-friendly and fair labor practices in the fashion industry.

Conclusion of Sustainable Indian Clothing Brands

Sustainable Indian clothing brands are leading the way in eco-friendly and socially responsible fashion. By choosing to support these brands, you are making a positive impact on the environment and on the lives of workers. So next time you're looking to refresh your wardrobe, consider shopping from sustainable Indian clothing brands.
