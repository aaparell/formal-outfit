Good Man Brand Shoes Squeak A Tutorial On How To Fix Them

Written by William May 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever experienced wearing a new pair of Good Man brand shoes, only to find out they squeak with every step you take? It can be frustrating and embarrassing, especially when you're in a quiet place. But worry not, as there are ways to fix this problem.

The squeaking sound of Good Man brand shoes can be a common issue for many wearers. It can happen due to various reasons, such as the sole rubbing against the insole, the shoe material not being broken in, or even the type of floor you're walking on. Whatever the cause may be, it's essential to get rid of the squeaking noise to enjoy your footwear comfortably.

The target of Good Man brand shoes squeak is to eliminate the annoying sound that can cause discomfort and embarrassment. Many people struggle with this problem, but there are practical solutions that can help fix it.

In summary, Good Man brand shoes can squeak due to various reasons such as the sole rubbing against the insole, the shoe material not being broken in, or the type of floor you're walking on. To fix this problem, you can try the following solutions:

Check the Fit of Your Shoes

One of the most common reasons why Good Man brand shoes squeak is that they don't fit correctly. When the shoes are too tight, the material rubs against each other, causing the squeaking noise. On the other hand, if the shoes are too loose, your feet can slide inside, causing the same problem. So, make sure that your shoes fit well and adjust the laces accordingly.

Personally, I had the same experience with my new Good Man brand shoes. I noticed that the squeaking sound started when I walked on a smooth surface like tiles or marble. However, when I walked on a rough surface like asphalt, the noise disappeared. It was frustrating, especially when I had to attend meetings in a quiet room. But then I remembered a trick my friend told me about using baby powder to eliminate the sound. And it worked like magic!

Use Baby Powder

Baby powder is an excellent solution to eliminate the squeaking sound of your Good Man brand shoes. It works by reducing the friction caused by the sole and the insole. All you have to do is sprinkle a small amount of baby powder inside your shoes and distribute it evenly. Be sure to remove any excess powder before wearing your shoes.

Other Alternatives to Baby Powder

If you don't have any baby powder, you can use other alternatives such as cornstarch, baking soda, or talcum powder. These products work the same way as baby powder, reducing the friction between the sole and the insole.

How to Apply Baby Powder to Your Shoes

To apply baby powder to your shoes, follow these simple steps:

  1. Clean the inside of your shoes using a dry cloth.
  2. Sprinkle a small amount of baby powder inside your shoes.
  3. Spread the powder evenly using your fingers or a soft-bristled brush.
  4. Shake off any excess powder.
  5. Wear your shoes and enjoy the silence!

Replace the Insole

If the squeaking sound persists after trying the above solutions, it may be time to replace the insole. Sometimes, the insole can get worn out, causing the material to rub against the sole and produce the noise. You can purchase new insoles from the same brand or a different one that fits your shoes well.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it normal for Good Man brand shoes to squeak?

A: No, it's not normal for any shoes to squeak. If you experience this problem, it's best to fix it before it gets worse.

Q: Can I use other types of powder besides baby powder?

A: Yes, you can use cornstarch, baking soda, or talcum powder as alternatives to baby powder.

Q: Can I still wear my Good Man brand shoes if they squeak?

A: Yes, you can still wear them, but it can be uncomfortable and embarrassing. It's best to fix the problem to enjoy your shoes comfortably.

Q: Can I return my Good Man brand shoes if they squeak?

A: It depends on the store's return policy. Check with the store where you purchased your shoes to know their policy on returns.

Conclusion of Good Man Brand Shoes Squeak

Fixing the squeaking sound of your Good Man brand shoes is essential to enjoy them comfortably and avoid embarrassment. You can try using baby powder or other alternatives, check the fit of your shoes, or replace the insole. Whatever solution you choose, make sure to apply it correctly and enjoy your shoes in silence.
