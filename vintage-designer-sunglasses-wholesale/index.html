How To Buy Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale A Guide

Are you a fashion retailer looking to stock up on unique and high-quality sunglasses for your store? Vintage designer sunglasses wholesale might be the answer you've been looking for. Not only do they add an air of sophistication to any outfit, but they also have a timeless appeal that never goes out of style.

However, finding vintage designer sunglasses wholesale can be a challenge. You might have to sift through numerous online retailers, attend auctions, or scour vintage shops to find the right pair. And even when you do find them, they can come with a hefty price tag that might not be feasible for your business.

But don't worry, in this guide, we'll show you everything you need to know about buying vintage designer sunglasses wholesale at an affordable price.

What is Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale?

Vintage designer sunglasses wholesale refers to the practice of buying original, pre-owned designer sunglasses in bulk at a discounted price. These sunglasses can be from any era, but they are usually from the 60s, 70s, and 80s. They are sourced from different locations such as estate sales, auctions, and vintage shops, and then sold in bulk to retailers who are looking to stock up on these unique pieces for their store.

Buying vintage designer sunglasses wholesale allows retailers to offer their customers something unique and hard to find. It also allows them to sell these sunglasses at a higher price point, thereby increasing their profit margin.

Where to Find Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale?

There are several ways to find vintage designer sunglasses wholesale. Here are a few options:

1. Online Retailers

There are several online retailers that specialize in vintage designer sunglasses wholesale. These retailers source their sunglasses from different locations and offer them at a discounted price to retailers. Some popular online retailers include Fossil Era, Retrospecced, and Vintage Frames Company.

2. Estate Sales and Auctions

Estate sales and auctions are a great way to find vintage designer sunglasses wholesale. These events are usually held when someone passes away or is downsizing, and they are a great way to find unique pieces at an affordable price. You can check websites like EstateSales.net and AuctionZip.com to find events near you.

3. Vintage Shops

Vintage shops are another great option for finding vintage designer sunglasses wholesale. These shops usually have a wide variety of vintage pieces, including sunglasses. You can search for vintage shops near you or check online marketplaces like Etsy and Depop.

How to Choose Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale?

When choosing vintage designer sunglasses wholesale, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Condition

Make sure the sunglasses are in good condition. They should be free from scratches, cracks, and other damage. If you're buying online, make sure to ask for detailed photos of the sunglasses before making a purchase.

2. Brand

Choose sunglasses from popular brands like Ray-Ban, Gucci, and Dior. These brands have a timeless appeal and are always in demand.

3. Style

Choose sunglasses that are on-trend and will appeal to your customers. Look for popular styles like aviators, cat-eye, and oversized sunglasses.

How to Care for Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale?

When you receive your vintage designer sunglasses wholesale, make sure to take good care of them. Here are a few tips:

1. Store Them Properly

Store your sunglasses in a case or a cloth bag to protect them from scratches and damage.

2. Clean Them Regularly

Clean your sunglasses regularly with a soft cloth and mild soap and water. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.

3. Avoid Extreme Temperatures

Avoid exposing your sunglasses to extreme temperatures or leaving them in a hot car. This can cause the frames to warp or the lenses to crack.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I sell vintage designer sunglasses wholesale online?

A: Yes, you can sell vintage designer sunglasses wholesale online. However, make sure to check the laws and regulations in your state or country regarding the sale of vintage items.

Q: Are vintage designer sunglasses wholesale authentic?

A: Yes, vintage designer sunglasses wholesale are authentic. They are sourced from different locations and are pre-owned, but they are still original pieces from popular brands.

Q: Can I return vintage designer sunglasses wholesale?

A: It depends on the retailer or seller. Make sure to check their return policy before making a purchase.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for vintage designer sunglasses wholesale?

A: The price of vintage designer sunglasses wholesale varies depending on the brand, style, and condition. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from $20 to $100 per pair.

Conclusion of Vintage Designer Sunglasses Wholesale

Buying vintage designer sunglasses wholesale is a great way for fashion retailers to offer something unique and hard to find. By following the tips in this guide, you can find the right pair of sunglasses at an affordable price and increase your profit margin. Just remember to choose sunglasses from popular brands, check their condition, and take good care of them once you receive them.
