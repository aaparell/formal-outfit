Question and Answer

Q: What is the most important factor to consider when buying sports shoes?

Q: How do I know if the sports shoes fit me well?

A: When trying on sports shoes, make sure to walk around in them to ensure a good fit. The shoes should feel snug but not tight, and there should be enough room in the toe box for your toes to move around comfortably.

Q: How do I care for my sports shoes?

A: To care for your sports shoes, make sure to clean them regularly with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid using harsh chemicals or putting them in the washing machine. Additionally, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
