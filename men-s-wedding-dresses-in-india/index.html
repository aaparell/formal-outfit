Men s Wedding Dresses In India A Guide To Finding The Perfect Outfit

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Weddings are a big deal in India, and finding the perfect wedding dress is just as important for men as it is for women. However, the process of finding the right outfit can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. In this post, we'll provide a guide to men's wedding dresses in India, including what to look for, where to shop, and more.

When it comes to men's wedding dresses in India, there are several pain points to consider. First, there are so many different styles and designs to choose from, which can make it difficult to narrow down your options. Additionally, the process of finding the right tailor or store can be challenging, especially if you're not familiar with the area.

The target of men's wedding dresses in India is to provide a comprehensive guide to finding the perfect outfit for your big day. Whether you're looking for a traditional sherwani or a more modern suit, we'll cover everything you need to know to make the right choice.

In summary, this post will provide a guide to men's wedding dresses in India, including what to look for, where to shop, and more. We'll cover everything from traditional outfits to modern designs, as well as tips for finding the right tailor or store.

Traditional Men's Wedding Dresses in India

When it comes to traditional men's wedding dresses in India, the sherwani is one of the most popular options. This long coat-like garment is typically worn with a pair of fitted pants and a scarf, and is often adorned with intricate embroidery or beading.

Personally, I opted for a traditional sherwani for my wedding day. I wanted to honor my cultural heritage and wear something that would make me feel connected to my roots. However, there are many other traditional options to choose from, including the kurta pajama, dhoti kurta, and more.

Modern Men's Wedding Dresses in India

If you're looking for something more modern, there are plenty of options available as well. Many designers are now incorporating traditional elements into more contemporary designs, creating a unique blend of old and new.

I know a friend who wore a modern suit for his wedding day, and he looked incredibly sharp. The suit was tailored to fit him perfectly, and he accessorized with a traditional turban to honor his heritage.

Where to Shop for Men's Wedding Dresses in India

When it comes to shopping for men's wedding dresses in India, there are several options available. Many cities have dedicated marketplaces or shopping districts where you can find a variety of different designers and tailors.

One of the most popular places to shop is Chandni Chowk in Delhi. This bustling marketplace is home to hundreds of shops and tailors, and you can find everything from traditional sherwanis to modern suits.

How to Choose the Right Tailor or Designer

Choosing the right tailor or designer is crucial when it comes to finding the perfect men's wedding dress in India. You want to work with someone who has experience creating outfits for weddings, and who can help guide you through the process.

One tip is to look for reviews and recommendations online. Many tailors and designers have websites or social media pages where you can see examples of their work and read reviews from previous customers.

Accessories for Men's Wedding Dresses in India

Finally, it's important to consider accessories when choosing a men's wedding dress in India. This can include everything from shoes to turbans to jewelry.

For my wedding day, I chose to accessorize with a traditional turban and a pair of juttis, which are traditional Indian shoes. I also wore a simple gold chain and bracelet to add a touch of elegance to my outfit.
