Online Selling Platforms

One of the most popular ways to sell your old shoes is through online selling platforms. These websites allow you to create a listing for your shoes and sell them to buyers all around the world. Some popular online platforms include:

1. eBay: eBay is a popular online marketplace where you can sell just about anything, including shoes. You can create a listing for your shoes, set a price, and wait for buyers to bid on them or buy them outright.

2. Poshmark: Poshmark is a popular app-based platform that allows you to sell clothing, shoes, and accessories. You can create a listing for your shoes, set a price, and wait for buyers to purchase them.

3. Mercari: Mercari is another popular app-based platform that allows you to sell just about anything, including shoes. You can create a listing for your shoes, set a price, and wait for buyers to purchase them.

Personally, I have had the most success with Poshmark. It is easy to use, and I have sold several pairs of shoes through the app. However, it's important to do your research and find the platform that works best for you.

In-Person Selling Options

If you prefer to sell your shoes in-person, there are plenty of options available as well. Some popular in-person selling options include:

1. Consignment shops: Consignment shops are stores that sell gently used clothing, shoes, and accessories on behalf of their owners. You can bring your shoes to a consignment shop, and they will sell them for you. You'll receive a percentage of the sale price, and the shop will keep the rest.

2. Thrift stores: Thrift stores are stores that sell used clothing, shoes, and accessories. You can donate your shoes to a thrift store, or you can sell them to the store for a small profit.

3. Garage sales: Garage sales are a great way to sell your old shoes and other items. You can set up a table in your driveway and sell your shoes to people in your neighborhood.

I personally have had success with consignment shops. They typically offer a higher payout than thrift stores, and they take care of the selling process for you. However, it's important to do your research and find the in-person selling option that works best for you.

Things to Consider When Selling Your Old Shoes

Whether you choose to sell your shoes online or in-person, there are a few things to consider to get the most out of your sales:

1. Condition: The condition of your shoes will play a big role in how much you can sell them for. Be sure to clean them up and make any necessary repairs before selling them.

2. Pricing: Do your research and find out what similar shoes are selling for on the platform or in-store you choose to sell on. Make sure your prices are competitive, but also make sure you're making a profit.

3. Shipping: If you choose to sell your shoes online, be sure to factor in the cost of shipping. Shipping costs can vary depending on the size and weight of the shoes, as well as the destination.

Tips for Selling Your Old Shoes

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your shoe sales:

1. Take good photos: Be sure to take clear, well-lit photos of your shoes. Show them from different angles so buyers can get a good idea of what they're buying.

2. Be honest: Be upfront about any flaws or damage to the shoes. This will help you build trust with potential buyers.

3. Promote your sales: If you're selling your shoes online, be sure to promote your listings on social media and other platforms. This will help you reach a larger audience and increase your chances of making a sale.
