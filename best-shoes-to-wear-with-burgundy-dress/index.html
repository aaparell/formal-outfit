Best Shoes To Wear With Burgundy Dress A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the right shoes to wear with your burgundy dress? You're not alone. Choosing the perfect shoe to complement your dress can be challenging, but fear not, we're here to help.

When it comes to finding the best shoes to wear with burgundy dress, it's essential to consider the occasion, style, and comfort. You don't want to wear uncomfortable shoes all day or night, and you also don't want to wear shoes that clash with your dress.

So, what are the best shoes to wear with burgundy dress? Let's explore some options.

Classic Black Pumps

One of the most versatile shoes you can wear with a burgundy dress is classic black pumps. They're elegant, stylish, and comfortable. Black pumps can complement your dress and add a touch of sophistication to your overall look. You can wear them to work, a formal event, or even a night out with friends.

Personally, I love pairing my burgundy dress with a pair of black pumps. They make me feel confident and chic. Plus, I can wear them for hours without any discomfort.

Nude Heels

If you're looking for a shoe that will elongate your legs and complement your dress, nude heels are an excellent option. They provide a subtle contrast to the burgundy dress and can make you look taller and slimmer. Nude heels are perfect for a wedding, formal event, or even a date night.

I wore a pair of nude heels with my burgundy dress to a wedding, and I received so many compliments. They were comfortable and gave my legs a flattering shape.

Strappy Sandals

If you're attending a summer wedding or a casual event, strappy sandals are a great choice. They're comfortable, stylish, and can add a pop of color to your outfit. You can choose a metallic or nude color to complement your burgundy dress.

I wore a pair of gold strappy sandals with my burgundy dress to a beach wedding, and they were perfect. They were comfortable and added a touch of glam to my look.

Ankle Boots

If you're looking for a shoe to wear with your burgundy dress in the fall or winter, ankle boots are an excellent option. They're stylish, comfortable, and can keep your feet warm. You can choose a black or brown ankle boot to complement your dress.

I love wearing ankle boots with my burgundy dress in the fall. They add a cozy and stylish touch to my outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear flats with a burgundy dress?

A: Yes, you can wear flats with a burgundy dress. However, make sure the style of the flat complements your dress. Ballet flats or loafers can work well with a burgundy dress.

Q: What color shoes should I avoid wearing with a burgundy dress?

A: You should avoid wearing shoes that clash with your burgundy dress. Avoid wearing bright or neon colors, as they can overpower your outfit. Also, avoid wearing shoes with too much embellishment or detail, as they can detract from your dress.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a burgundy dress?

A: Yes, you can wear sandals with a burgundy dress, especially if you're attending a summer event. However, make sure the style of the sandal complements your dress. Strappy sandals or gladiator sandals can work well with a burgundy dress.

Q: What shoes should I wear with a casual burgundy dress?

A: You can wear a variety of shoes with a casual burgundy dress, such as sneakers, ankle boots, or sandals. It all depends on the occasion and your personal style.

Conclusion of Best Shoes to Wear with Burgundy Dress

Choosing the best shoes to wear with burgundy dress can be a daunting task, but with the right knowledge, you can find the perfect pair. Consider the occasion, style, and comfort when choosing your shoes. Classic black pumps, nude heels, strappy sandals, and ankle boots are all great options to complement your dress. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors to find the perfect shoe for your burgundy dress.
