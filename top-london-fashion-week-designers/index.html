Top London Fashion Week Designers A Guide

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 · 5 min read

London Fashion Week is one of the most anticipated events in the fashion industry. It showcases the latest trends and designs from top fashion designers in London. However, with so many designers to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which ones to follow. In this post, we will guide you through some of the top London Fashion Week designers to keep an eye on.

If you are a fashion enthusiast, you know how important it is to keep up with the latest trends. However, it can be frustrating to see a beautiful outfit on the runway, only to find out that it is not available in your local stores. This is where knowing the top London Fashion Week designers comes in handy. By following these designers, you can stay up-to-date with the latest trends and get a sense of what to expect in the upcoming seasons.

Alexander McQueen

Alexander McQueen is a legendary fashion designer who is known for his avant-garde designs and dramatic runway shows. His designs often feature intricate embroidery, embellishments, and bold prints. McQueen's designs are not for the faint of heart, but they are perfect for those who want to make a statement.

Personally, I have always been a fan of Alexander McQueen's designs. I remember watching one of his runway shows and being completely blown away by the creativity and attention to detail. His designs are truly works of art, and it is no surprise that he is still considered one of the top London Fashion Week designers.

Burberry

Burberry is a British luxury fashion brand that is known for its iconic trench coats and checkered prints. The brand has been around since the 1850s and has since become a staple in the fashion industry. Burberry's designs are timeless and classic, yet they always manage to stay on-trend.

I personally own a Burberry trench coat, and it has been a staple in my wardrobe for years. The quality of the fabric and craftsmanship is unmatched, and I always feel stylish and sophisticated whenever I wear it. Burberry is a brand that will never go out of style, and it is definitely worth investing in.

Vivienne Westwood

Vivienne Westwood is a British fashion designer who is known for her punk-inspired designs and rebellious attitude. She has been in the fashion industry for over 40 years and is still considered one of the top London Fashion Week designers. Westwood's designs often feature bold prints, bright colors, and unconventional silhouettes.

I have always been drawn to Vivienne Westwood's designs because of their edginess and uniqueness. Her designs are not for everyone, but for those who dare to be different, they are the perfect way to make a statement. I recently purchased a Vivienne Westwood dress, and I always receive compliments whenever I wear it.

How to Follow Top London Fashion Week Designers

If you want to stay up-to-date with the latest trends from top London Fashion Week designers, there are several ways to do so. One way is to follow them on social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter. You can also sign up for their newsletters to receive updates on their latest collections and runway shows.

Another way to stay up-to-date is to attend London Fashion Week in person. While this may not be possible for everyone, it is definitely worth considering if you are a fashion enthusiast. You will have the opportunity to see the latest collections up close and personal, and you may even get the chance to meet some of your favorite designers.

Conclusion of Top London Fashion Week Designers

In conclusion, knowing the top London Fashion Week designers is essential for any fashion enthusiast. By following these designers, you can stay up-to-date with the latest trends and get a sense of what to expect in the upcoming seasons. Whether you prefer avant-garde designs or classic silhouettes, there is a designer for everyone. So, start following these designers today and elevate your fashion game to the next level.
