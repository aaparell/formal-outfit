Best Badminton Shoes In India 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of using badminton shoes that don't offer enough support or cushioning? Are you looking for the best badminton shoes in India 2020 that can help you improve your game? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the top badminton shoes in India and help you make an informed decision.

Playing badminton can put a lot of stress on your feet, ankles, and knees. The right shoes can make a world of difference in terms of comfort, performance, and injury prevention. Unfortunately, with so many options available in the market, choosing the best badminton shoes in India 2020 can be a daunting task.

The target of this guide is to help you choose the best badminton shoes in India 2020 that suit your needs and budget. We will look at the key features you should consider when selecting badminton shoes, the different types of shoes available, and some of the best brands to choose from.

The Importance of Choosing the Right Badminton Shoes

Before we dive into the specifics of choosing the best badminton shoes in India 2020, let me share my personal experience with you. A few years ago, I suffered from a severe ankle sprain while playing badminton. It took several weeks to recover, and I had to take a break from the game. It was only after consulting with a sports doctor that I realized the importance of using the right shoes.

Badminton shoes are designed to provide support, stability, and cushioning to your feet, which can significantly reduce the risk of injuries. They also offer better grip and traction on the court, allowing you to move quickly and change direction with ease.

Key Features to Consider When Choosing Badminton Shoes

When looking for the best badminton shoes in India 2020, here are some key features you should consider:

Cushioning

Badminton shoes should have adequate cushioning to absorb shocks and reduce the impact on your feet. Look for shoes with thicker soles and insoles made of materials like EVA or foam.

Support

Good badminton shoes should provide ample support to your feet, ankles, and knees. Look for shoes with sturdy uppers, ankle support, and reinforced soles.

Grip

Badminton shoes should have a good grip on the court to prevent slipping and sliding. Look for shoes with non-marking rubber soles with a pattern that offers good traction.

Weight

Lightweight badminton shoes can help you move quickly and reduce fatigue. Look for shoes made of lightweight materials like mesh or synthetic leather.

Types of Badminton Shoes

There are different types of badminton shoes available in the market. Here are some of the most common types:

Indoor Court Shoes

Indoor court shoes are designed for playing on wooden or synthetic courts. They have a non-marking sole and offer good grip and traction.

Outdoor Court Shoes

Outdoor court shoes are designed for playing on concrete or asphalt courts. They have a more durable sole and offer better shock absorption.

Cross-Trainer Shoes

Cross-trainer shoes are designed for multiple sports and activities. They offer good support, cushioning, and stability, but may not be as specialized as badminton shoes.

Best Badminton Shoe Brands in India 2020

FAQs

Q: Can I use running shoes for badminton?

A: Running shoes may not be suitable for playing badminton as they are designed for forward motion and not lateral movement. Badminton shoes offer better support and grip for the court.

Q: How often should I replace my badminton shoes?

A: It is recommended to replace your badminton shoes every 6-12 months, depending on how frequently you play and the condition of the shoes.

Q: Are expensive badminton shoes worth it?

A: Expensive badminton shoes may offer better quality materials, better cushioning, and better performance. However, it is possible to find good quality shoes at a reasonable price as well.

Q: Can I wear badminton shoes for other sports?

A: Badminton shoes may not be suitable for other sports as they are designed for specific needs like grip, support, and cushioning. Cross-trainer shoes may be a better option for multiple sports.

Conclusion of Best Badminton Shoes in India 2020

Choosing the best badminton shoes in India 2020 can be a challenging task, but it is essential for your comfort, performance, and injury prevention. Consider the key features like cushioning, support, grip, and weight when selecting your shoes. Look for specialized badminton shoe brands like Yonex, Adidas, Nike, or Li-Ning that offer quality shoes at different price points. With the right shoes, you can enjoy your game and improve your skills. Happy playing!
