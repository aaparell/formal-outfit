High End Men s Sneaker Brands A Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a fashion-forward man looking for the perfect pair of sneakers to complete your wardrobe? Look no further than high end men's sneaker brands. With their premium materials and unique designs, these sneakers are the perfect way to elevate any outfit. But with so many options on the market, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at high end men's sneaker brands and what makes them so special.

When it comes to high end men's sneaker brands, the biggest pain point for many consumers is the price. These sneakers can often cost hundreds or even thousands of dollars, making them a significant investment. However, for many fashion-conscious men, the quality and style of these sneakers make them well worth the cost.

So what is the target of high end men's sneaker brands? These sneakers are designed for men who want to make a statement with their footwear. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just want to elevate your everyday look, high end men's sneaker brands offer a unique blend of style and comfort that can't be found in traditional sneakers.

In this article, we'll be discussing some of the top high end men's sneaker brands on the market, what makes them special, and why they're worth the investment. We'll also be sharing some personal experiences and insights to help you make an informed decision when it comes to choosing the perfect pair of high end sneakers.

Common Projects

One of the most popular high end men's sneaker brands on the market today is Common Projects. Known for their minimalist designs and high-quality materials, Common Projects sneakers are the perfect choice for men who want a classic, understated look.

Personally, I own a pair of Common Projects Achilles Low sneakers and they are some of the most comfortable shoes I've ever worn. The leather is soft and supple, and the gold numbering on the side adds a touch of luxury to any outfit. While they were definitely an investment, I know that they'll last me for years to come and are well worth the cost.

Golden Goose

If you're looking for something a little more unique, Golden Goose is a great option. These sneakers are known for their vintage-inspired designs and distressed detailing, giving them a one-of-a-kind look that's sure to turn heads.

I recently purchased a pair of Golden Goose Superstar sneakers and they quickly became one of my favorite pairs of shoes. The distressed detailing adds a cool, edgy vibe to any outfit, and the comfortable insoles mean I can wear them all day without any discomfort. While they are definitely on the pricier side, I know that I'll get a ton of wear out of them and they're a great conversation starter whenever I wear them out.

Balenciaga

Another popular high end men's sneaker brand is Balenciaga. Known for their chunky, oversized designs, Balenciaga sneakers are a great choice for men who want to make a bold statement with their footwear.

While I don't personally own a pair of Balenciaga sneakers, I've tried them on in stores and they are incredibly comfortable. The chunky sole provides a lot of support and cushioning, making them a great option for long days on your feet. And with a wide range of colors and designs to choose from, there's sure to be a pair of Balenciaga sneakers that fits your personal style.

How to Care for High End Men's Sneakers

When you invest in a pair of high end men's sneakers, it's important to take good care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are a few tips for keeping your sneakers looking their best:

  • Invest in a good quality shoe cleaner and use it regularly to remove dirt and stains.
  • Store your sneakers in a cool, dry place to prevent mold and mildew.
  • Avoid wearing your sneakers in inclement weather or on rough terrain to prevent damage.
  • Consider investing in shoe trees to help your sneakers maintain their shape.

Conclusion of High End Men's Sneaker Brands

While high end men's sneakers may be a significant investment, they're well worth the cost for fashion-conscious men who want to make a statement with their footwear. Whether you opt for minimalist designs or bold, oversized styles, there's a high end men's sneaker brand out there that's sure to fit your personal style. By taking good care of your sneakers and investing in timeless designs, you can enjoy your high end sneakers for years to come.
