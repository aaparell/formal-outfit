How to Care for Your Small London Jewellery Brands

When you invest in a piece of jewellery from a small London jewellery brand, you want to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your jewellery:

  • Store your jewellery in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Remove your jewellery before showering or swimming
  • Avoid spraying perfume or hairspray directly onto your jewellery
  • Use a soft cloth to clean your jewellery
