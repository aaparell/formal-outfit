The Best Of All Time Anime A Guide For Beginners

If you're new to anime, you might be overwhelmed with the number of choices available. Which series should you start with? What makes a great anime? In this guide, we'll explore the best of all time anime and help you discover your new favorite series.

The pain point for many new anime viewers is the sheer number of options. With so many series to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. Additionally, many anime series have complex storylines and characters, which can be difficult to follow for those who are new to the genre.

So, what is the target of the best of all time anime? The goal is to help new viewers discover some of the most popular and critically acclaimed anime series. By recommending these series, we hope to provide a starting point for those who are new to anime and help them discover what makes this medium so great.

In this article, we'll cover some of the best of all time anime series, including their plots, characters, and why they're so popular. We'll also touch on some of the common themes found in anime series, such as coming of age stories, action and adventure, and romance.

One Piece

One Piece is one of the best-selling manga and anime series of all time, and for good reason. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber, as he searches for the legendary One Piece treasure. Along the way, he gathers a crew of misfits, including a swordsman, a cook, and a reindeer, and they battle other pirates and the World Government.

I first discovered One Piece when I was in college, and I was immediately hooked. The characters are well-developed and lovable, and the story is full of action, adventure, and humor. The themes of friendship and following your dreams are also present throughout the series.

Death Note

Death Note is a psychological thriller that follows Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. He uses the notebook to rid the world of criminals, but his actions attract the attention of a mysterious detective known only as "L."

I was drawn to Death Note because of its unique premise and complex characters. The series explores themes of power, morality, and justice, and the battle of wits between Light and L is both intense and engaging.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood is a fantasy series that takes place in a world where alchemy is a powerful force. The story follows two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, as they search for the Philosopher's Stone, a legendary artifact that can restore their bodies after a failed attempt to bring their mother back to life using alchemy.

This series is widely regarded as one of the best of all time anime, thanks to its rich world-building, complex characters, and emotional storytelling. The themes of sacrifice, family, and redemption are prevalent throughout the series.

How to Choose the Best of All Time Anime for You

When choosing an anime series to watch, it's important to consider your personal preferences. Do you prefer action and adventure, or romance and drama? Are you interested in exploring complex themes and characters, or do you prefer something more light-hearted?

One way to discover new anime series is to read reviews and recommendations from other viewers. Websites like MyAnimeList and Anime-Planet are great resources for finding new series to watch.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy series that takes place in a world where humanity lives in fear of giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager, a young man who vows to destroy the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother.

I was drawn to Attack on Titan because of its intense action scenes and thrilling plot twists. The series also explores themes of survival, freedom, and the human condition.

Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a classic anime series that has been entertaining viewers since the 1980s. The story follows Goku, a powerful warrior who protects the Earth from a variety of threats, including intergalactic conquerors and powerful androids.

Dragon Ball Z is known for its epic battles, colorful characters, and unique blend of humor and action. The series also explores themes of family, sacrifice, and the pursuit of strength.

Conclusion of Best of All Time Anime

There are countless anime series to choose from, but the best of all time anime are the ones that capture your imagination and make you fall in love with the medium.
