How to Dress Appropriately for a Funeral: A Guide on What to Wear with Black Dress

Attending a funeral can be a challenging and emotional experience, and figuring out what to wear can add to the stress. You want to make sure you are dressed appropriately to show respect for the deceased and their family, but you may also be unsure of what is considered appropriate funeral attire.

When it comes to what to wear with a black dress for a funeral, there are a few guidelines to follow that can help you make an appropriate choice.

First and foremost, it is important to remember that a funeral is a solemn occasion, so your attire should reflect this. This means avoiding anything too flashy or attention-grabbing. You want to dress in a way that is understated and respectful.

Choosing the Right Attire

When it comes to what to wear with a black dress for a funeral, the most important thing is to keep it simple. Stick to darker colors, such as black, navy, or dark gray, and avoid anything that is overly bright or patterned.

For women, a black dress is always a safe choice. Make sure it is knee-length or longer, and avoid anything too low-cut or revealing. You can pair it with simple black pumps or flats and minimal jewelry.

For men, a dark suit and tie is appropriate. If you do not have a suit, a dark dress shirt and dress pants can work as well. Avoid anything too casual, such as jeans or sneakers.

Accessories and Makeup

When it comes to accessories and makeup, the same rules apply - keep it simple and understated. Avoid anything too flashy or distracting.

For accessories, opt for minimal jewelry, such as a simple necklace or stud earrings. Avoid anything too big or bold.

For makeup, keep it natural and subtle. Avoid anything too bold or colorful. Stick to neutral shades and keep it simple.

Shoes and Bags

When it comes to shoes and bags, the key is to keep it simple and practical.

For shoes, opt for simple black pumps or flats. Avoid anything too high or strappy.

For bags, a simple black clutch or shoulder bag is appropriate. Avoid anything too big or bold.

Hair and Grooming

When it comes to hair and grooming, it is important to keep it clean and well-maintained.

For hair, keep it neat and tidy. Avoid anything too flashy or distracting. Stick to simple styles, such as a ponytail or bun.

For grooming, keep it simple and clean. Avoid anything too bold or colorful. Stick to natural shades and keep it simple.

Conclusion of what to wear with black dress for funeral

When attending a funeral, it is important to dress appropriately to show respect for the deceased and their family. By following these guidelines and keeping it simple and understated, you can ensure that you are dressed in a way that is appropriate and respectful.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a white dress to a funeral?

A: It is generally considered inappropriate to wear white to a funeral, as it is a color associated with celebration and joy rather than mourning.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a funeral?

A: It is generally considered inappropriate to wear jeans to a funeral, as it is a casual attire and not appropriate for a solemn occasion like a funeral.

Q: Can I wear open-toed shoes to a funeral?

A: It is generally considered inappropriate to wear open-toed shoes to a funeral, as they are too casual and not appropriate for a solemn occasion like a funeral. Closed-toed shoes are a more appropriate choice.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a funeral?

A: It is generally considered appropriate to wear a hat to a funeral, as long as it is understated and not too flashy. A simple black hat can work well as a respectful accessory.
