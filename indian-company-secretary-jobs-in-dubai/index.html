Indian Company Secretary Jobs In Dubai A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you an Indian company secretary looking for job opportunities in Dubai? With the city's booming economy and increasing demand for skilled professionals, Dubai can be a great destination to grow your career. However, navigating the job market in a foreign country can be daunting, especially if you're not familiar with the local rules and regulations. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide to Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai, including the requirements, challenges, and opportunities.

If you're an Indian company secretary looking for job opportunities in Dubai, you may face several challenges. Firstly, you need to ensure that your qualifications and experience are recognized by the local authorities. Secondly, you need to understand the local laws and regulations related to company secretarial work, which may differ from your home country. Thirdly, you need to compete with other skilled professionals in a highly competitive job market. Lastly, you need to adapt to the local culture and work environment, which may be different from what you're used to.

The target of Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai is to provide support to the board of directors and ensure that the company complies with legal and regulatory requirements. To become a company secretary in Dubai, you need to have a relevant qualification such as a degree in law, finance, or business administration, and a professional certification such as the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). You also need to have relevant experience in company secretarial work, such as drafting board resolutions, maintaining statutory records, and ensuring compliance with corporate governance standards.

In summary, Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai can be a great opportunity for skilled professionals looking to grow their career in a booming economy. However, you need to be aware of the challenges and requirements involved, and prepare yourself accordingly. In the next sections, we'll provide you with more information about the job market, the qualifications and certifications required, the skills and competencies needed, and some tips for finding job opportunities in Dubai.

The Job Market for Indian Company Secretary Jobs in Dubai

Based on our personal experience and research, the job market for Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai is highly competitive, with many skilled professionals vying for a limited number of vacancies. However, if you have the right qualifications, experience, and skills, you can stand out from the crowd and secure a job that matches your profile and aspirations.

One way to increase your chances of finding a job in Dubai is to network with other professionals in your field, attend job fairs and conferences, and join online groups and forums related to company secretarial work. You can also approach recruitment agencies that specialize in placing professionals in Dubai, and create a strong online presence through platforms like LinkedIn and other social media channels.

The Qualifications and Certifications Required for Indian Company Secretary Jobs in Dubai

As mentioned earlier, to become a company secretary in Dubai, you need to have a relevant qualification such as a degree in law, finance, or business administration, and a professional certification such as the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). In addition, you need to have relevant experience in company secretarial work, such as drafting board resolutions, maintaining statutory records, and ensuring compliance with corporate governance standards.

It's also important to note that the local laws and regulations related to company secretarial work may differ from your home country, so you need to familiarize yourself with the local rules and requirements. You may need to undergo additional training or certification to meet the local standards, such as the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) Academy's Company Secretary Diploma program.

The Skills and Competencies Needed for Indian Company Secretary Jobs in Dubai

In addition to the qualifications and certifications mentioned above, you also need to have certain skills and competencies to succeed in Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai. These include:

- Strong communication skills, both verbal and written, to interact with the board of directors, senior management, and other stakeholders.

- Good analytical and problem-solving skills, to identify and resolve issues related to corporate governance, compliance, and risk management.

- Attention to detail and accuracy, to ensure that statutory records and other documents are maintained correctly and up-to-date.

- Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines, as company secretarial work involves multiple tasks and deadlines that need to be managed efficiently.

Tips for Finding Indian Company Secretary Jobs in Dubai

Here are some tips to help you find Indian company secretary jobs in Dubai:

- Update your CV and cover letter to reflect your qualifications, experience, and skills, and tailor them to the job requirements.

- Create a strong online presence through LinkedIn and other social media channels, and showcase your achievements and expertise.

- Network with other professionals in your field, attend job fairs and conferences, and join online groups and forums related to company secretarial work.

- Approach recruitment agencies that specialize in placing professionals in Dubai, and keep in touch with them regularly.
