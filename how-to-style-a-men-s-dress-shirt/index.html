How To Style A Men s Dress Shirt A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever stood in front of your closet, trying to figure out how to style a men's dress shirt? It can be a daunting task, especially if you're not used to dressing up. But fear not, we've got you covered with this comprehensive guide on how to style a men's dress shirt.

Many men struggle with how to style a men's dress shirt because they don't know what to pair it with. They may also be intimidated by the idea of dressing up or worry about looking too formal. But with a few simple tips and tricks, you can easily style a men's dress shirt to fit any occasion.

Understanding the Basics of Dress Shirts

Before we dive into how to style a men's dress shirt, let's first understand the basics. A dress shirt is typically made of cotton and has a collar, buttons down the front, and long sleeves with cuffs. It's a versatile piece of clothing that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

When it comes to choosing a dress shirt, there are a few things to keep in mind. The fit should be tailored but not too tight, and the collar should fit comfortably around your neck without being too loose or too tight. The cuffs should also be snug but not too constricting.

Pairing a Dress Shirt with Pants

One of the most important things to consider when styling a men's dress shirt is what pants to wear with it. Dress shirts can be paired with a variety of pants, including dress pants, chinos, or even jeans.

If you're dressing up for a formal occasion, dress pants are the way to go. Choose a pair in a neutral color like black, navy, or gray to keep the focus on the shirt. For a more casual look, chinos or jeans can be paired with a dress shirt. Just be sure to choose a pair of pants that complements the color and style of the shirt.

Layering a Dress Shirt with a Blazer or Sweater

If you want to take your dress shirt to the next level, consider layering it with a blazer or sweater. A blazer instantly elevates the look of a dress shirt, making it perfect for more formal occasions. Opt for a blazer in a complementary color to the shirt and pair it with dress pants or chinos.

If you're going for a more casual look, a sweater can be a great option. A crewneck sweater in a neutral color like gray or navy pairs well with a dress shirt and jeans or chinos. Just be sure to choose a sweater that fits well and isn't too bulky.

Accessorizing a Dress Shirt

Accessories can take a men's dress shirt from basic to stylish. A tie is a classic accessory that pairs well with a dress shirt and can add a pop of color or pattern to an outfit. If you're going for a more casual look, consider skipping the tie and opting for a pocket square instead.

A watch is another accessory that can elevate the look of a dress shirt. Choose a watch with a leather strap in a complementary color to the shirt or opt for a classic metal watch. Just be sure to keep the watch simple and understated.

Tips for Proper Dress Shirt Care

Proper care and maintenance of a dress shirt is key to keeping it looking great. Always follow the care instructions on the shirt's tag, and be sure to iron or steam the shirt before wearing it. Hang dress shirts on a hanger to prevent wrinkles and avoid over-washing them.

Conclusion of How to Style a Men's Dress Shirt

Styling a men's dress shirt doesn't have to be complicated. By following these simple tips and tricks, you can easily put together a stylish and sophisticated outfit for any occasion. Just remember to choose pants and accessories that complement the shirt and always take proper care of your dress shirts to keep them looking great.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a dress shirt with shorts?

A: It's not recommended to wear a dress shirt with shorts as it can look mismatched and unbalanced.

Q: How many dress shirts should I own?

A: It's recommended to have at least five dress shirts in your wardrobe to ensure you have enough options for different occasions.

Q: Can I wear a dress shirt untucked?

A: It's generally not recommended to wear a dress shirt untucked as it can look sloppy. However, some casual dress shirts are designed to be worn untucked, so be sure to check the style of the shirt before wearing it this way.

Q: Can I wear a patterned dress shirt with a patterned tie?

A: It's generally not recommended to wear a patterned dress shirt with a patterned tie as it can look busy and overwhelming. Instead, opt for a solid tie or a tie with a subtle pattern.
