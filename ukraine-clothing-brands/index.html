I personally own a pair of Ksenia Schnaider demi-denims, and they are one of my favorite items in my wardrobe. They are comfortable, stylish, and unique.

2. Lake Studio

Lake Studio is a Ukraine clothing brand that was founded by two sisters. The brand is known for its feminine designs and high-quality materials. Lake Studio's designs are inspired by vintage clothing, but they are updated for a modern audience. The brand's signature piece is the "romantic dress," which is a long, flowy dress that is perfect for summer.

I recently purchased a dress from Lake Studio, and I was impressed by the quality of the materials and the attention to detail in the design. The dress is comfortable, stylish, and unique.

3. Bevza

Bevza is a Ukraine clothing brand that was founded by Svetlana Bevza. The brand is known for its minimalist designs and high-quality materials. Bevza's designs are inspired by the beauty of nature, and they are perfect for people who appreciate simplicity and elegance.

Litkovskaya is a Ukraine clothing brand that was founded by Lilia Litkovskaya. The brand is known for its avant-garde designs and high-quality materials. Litkovskaya's designs are inspired by art and architecture, and they are perfect for people who want to make a bold statement with their clothing.
