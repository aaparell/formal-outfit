Discovering The Best Clothing Brands In Seoul

Written by Daniel May 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you planning to visit Seoul and wondering where to shop for the latest fashion trends? Look no further because Seoul is home to some of the best clothing brands in the world. From high-end designer labels to streetwear brands, Seoul has it all. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most popular clothing brands in Seoul and what makes them stand out.

When it comes to shopping for clothes in Seoul, there are a few pain points that travelers might face. Firstly, the language barrier can make it difficult to communicate with store employees, especially if you're looking for a specific item or size. Secondly, the sheer number of clothing brands can be overwhelming, making it challenging to decide where to start. Lastly, some stores might not accept international credit cards, so it's essential to carry cash or check beforehand.

The target of this blog post is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best clothing brands in Seoul, highlighting their unique features and what sets them apart from others.

In summary, this blog post will take you through some of the most popular clothing brands in Seoul and what makes them stand out. We will cover high-end designer labels such as Gentle Monster and Stylenanda, streetwear brands like Ader Error and 99%IS-, and affordable fashion brands like Mixxmix and Chuu. Let's dive in and discover the best clothing brands Seoul has to offer!

Gentle Monster - For High-End Designer Eyewear

One of the most popular clothing brands in Seoul is Gentle Monster, known for its avant-garde eyewear designs. The brand has collaborated with high-end fashion houses like Ambush and Alexander Wang and has stores in major cities like New York, Beijing, and Tokyo. I visited the flagship store in Seoul's trendy district of Garosu-gil, and I was blown away by the store's futuristic interior design. The eyewear collection is divided into unique themes, each with its own story and visual display. The quality of the glasses is exceptional, and the customer service was top-notch. I highly recommend checking out Gentle Monster if you're looking for high-end designer eyewear.

Ader Error - For Quirky Streetwear

If you're looking for unique streetwear brands, Ader Error is a must-visit. The brand combines quirky designs with high-quality fabrics to create one-of-a-kind pieces. The flagship store in Hongdae is a sight to behold, with each room in the store designed to represent a different theme. The clothes' prices are on the higher side, but the quality and design make up for it. I bought a pair of denim pants with quirky patchwork designs that have become a staple in my wardrobe. If you're a fan of streetwear fashion, Ader Error is the perfect brand for you.

Mixxmix - For Affordable Korean Fashion

If you're on a budget but still want to shop for the latest Korean fashion trends, Mixxmix is the brand for you. The brand offers affordable but stylish clothing options, with prices ranging from $10 to $50. The clothes are trendy and comfortable, and the store also offers a range of accessories and shoes to complete your look. I visited the store in Myeong-dong and was impressed by the variety of options available at such reasonable prices. If you're looking for affordable Korean fashion, Mixxmix is the perfect brand for you.

Stylenanda - For High-End Designer Clothing

Stylenanda is another popular clothing brand in Seoul, known for its high-end designer clothing and accessories. The brand has collaborated with major fashion houses like MAC and has stores in major cities like Tokyo and Bangkok. The flagship store in Myeong-dong is a five-story building that houses a cafe, beauty salon, and clothing store. The clothes are a mix of elegant and edgy, with unique designs and high-quality fabrics. I bought a black blazer from Stylenanda, and it's become a staple in my wardrobe. If you're looking for high-end designer clothing, Stylenanda is the perfect brand for you.

99%IS - For Edgy Streetwear

If you're looking for edgy streetwear brands, 99%IS is a must-visit. The brand's designs are influenced by punk rock and heavy metal, and the clothes feature bold prints and unique designs. The flagship store in Hongdae is a sight to behold, with a graffiti-covered exterior that's hard to miss. The clothes are on the pricier side, but the quality and design make up for it. I bought a black leather jacket from 99%IS, and it's become one of my favorite pieces in my wardrobe. If you're a fan of edgy streetwear, 99%IS is the perfect brand for you.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Are the prices of clothes in Seoul expensive?

A: It depends on the brand you're shopping for. High-end designer labels like Stylenanda and Gentle Monster can be expensive, but streetwear brands like Ader Error and 99%IS offer more affordable options.

Q: Do clothing stores in Seoul accept international credit cards?

A: Some stores might not accept international credit cards, so it's essential to carry cash or check beforehand. However, major department stores like Lotte and Shinsegae accept most international credit cards.

Q: Can I get tax refunds when shopping for clothes in Seoul?

A: Yes, most stores offer tax refunds for international visitors. You can ask for a tax refund receipt when making your purchase and claim your refund at the airport when leaving Korea.

Q: Are there any clothing brands in Seoul that offer plus-size options?

A: Yes, some brands like Chuu and Aland offer plus-size options. However, the sizes available might be limited, so it's best to check beforehand.

Conclusion of Popular Clothing Brands in Seoul

Seoul is a fashion hub, and it's no surprise that the city is home to some of the best clothing brands in the world. From high-end designer labels to streetwear brands and affordable fashion brands, Seoul has something for everyone. In this blog post, we've explored some of the most popular clothing brands in Seoul and what makes them stand out. Whether you're looking for high-end designer clothing or quirky streetwear, Seoul has it all.
