What s On A Classic Italian Sub

Written by Bardi May 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring sandwich for lunch? Do you want to try something new and delicious? Look no further than the classic Italian sub! This mouth-watering sandwich is packed with flavor and will leave your taste buds wanting more.

The thought of creating the perfect Italian sub may seem overwhelming, but fear not. With the right ingredients and a little know-how, you can easily create a masterpiece that will make your taste buds sing.

So, what exactly is on a classic Italian sub? Let's break it down.

The classic Italian sub typically includes a variety of Italian meats such as salami, ham, and pepperoni. It also features provolone cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and peppers. All of these delicious ingredients are then piled high on a freshly baked sub roll and finished off with a drizzle of oil and vinegar dressing.

In summary, a classic Italian sub is a mouth-watering sandwich filled with Italian meats, cheese, and vegetables, all nestled between a fresh sub roll and topped off with a tangy dressing.

Why is the Italian Sub so Popular?

The Italian sub is a popular choice for many because of its amazing flavor and variety of ingredients. It's also a great option for those who are looking for a hearty and filling lunch.

Personally, I love the Italian sub because it's the perfect combination of salty meats, creamy cheese, and fresh veggies. It's a sandwich that truly satisfies all of my cravings.

How to Make the Perfect Italian Sub

To make the perfect Italian sub, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Pepperoni
  • Provolone cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Sub roll
  • Oil and vinegar dressing

Once you have all of your ingredients, it's time to assemble your sandwich. Begin by slicing your sub roll in half and layering your meats and cheese on top of one half. Then, add your lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and peppers. Finally, drizzle your oil and vinegar dressing on top and place the other half of your roll on top.

Voila! You now have the perfect Italian sub that is sure to impress.

Where to Find the Best Italian Sub

While making your own Italian sub is always a great option, sometimes you just want to sit back and let someone else do the work for you. If you're looking for the best Italian sub, look no further than your local deli or sandwich shop.

Personally, I love going to my local Italian deli where they make the most amazing Italian subs. The meats are always fresh and the vegetables are perfectly crisp. Plus, they always give you a generous portion of oil and vinegar dressing which really takes the sub to the next level.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use different types of cheese on my Italian sub?
A: Absolutely! While provolone cheese is the most traditional option, you can also use other types of cheese such as mozzarella or even a blend of Italian cheeses.

Q: Can I make my own oil and vinegar dressing?
A: Yes, you can make your own dressing by combining equal parts oil and vinegar with some Italian seasoning and a pinch of salt and pepper.

Q: What type of bread is best for an Italian sub?
A: A fresh sub roll or hoagie roll is the best option as it is sturdy enough to hold all of the delicious fillings.

Q: Can I add other ingredients to my Italian sub?
A: Absolutely! You can add any additional ingredients that you like such as olives, pickles, or even jalapenos.

Conclusion of What's on a Classic Italian Sub

The classic Italian sub is a delicious sandwich that is packed with flavor and is sure to satisfy your hunger. Whether you make it yourself or visit your local deli, the Italian sub is a lunchtime favorite that will never go out of style.
