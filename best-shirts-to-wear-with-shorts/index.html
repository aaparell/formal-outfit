Best Shirts To Wear With Shorts A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Summer is here and it's time to break out the shorts! But, what shirts should you wear with them? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll explore the best shirts to wear with shorts and how to style them for any occasion.

When it comes to finding the perfect shirt to wear with shorts, there are a few pain points to consider. You want to find something that will keep you cool in the heat, but also looks stylish and put together. It can be tough to strike that balance, but with a little guidance, you'll be able to find the perfect shirt in no time.

The target of this guide is to provide you with a wide range of options for shirts to wear with shorts. Whether you're heading to the beach, a barbecue, or a casual night out, we've got you covered.

In summary, we'll cover the best shirts to wear with shorts and related keywords, including:

  • Short-sleeve button-downs
  • Polo shirts
  • T-shirts
  • Hawaiian shirts
  • Camp shirts

Short-Sleeve Button-Downs

Short-sleeve button-downs are a classic choice for pairing with shorts. They're versatile, comfortable, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Personally, I love wearing a white or light blue short-sleeve button-down with a pair of khaki or navy shorts. It's a timeless look that works for just about any summer event.

If you want to switch things up, try a patterned or printed short-sleeve button-down. Florals, stripes, and gingham are all great options. Just be sure to keep the rest of your outfit simple so that the shirt remains the focal point.

Polo Shirts

Polo shirts are another classic option for pairing with shorts. They're comfortable, breathable, and come in a variety of colors and styles. A solid-colored polo shirt is always a safe bet, but if you're feeling bold, try a patterned or color-blocked polo shirt.

When it comes to styling a polo shirt with shorts, you can't go wrong with a pair of classic khaki or navy shorts. If you want to mix things up, try a pair of colored or printed shorts. Just be sure to keep the rest of your outfit simple so that the shirt and shorts don't clash.

T-Shirts

T-shirts are a go-to for casual summer days. They're comfortable, easy to wear, and come in a variety of styles and colors. To elevate your t-shirt and shorts look, opt for a well-fitted t-shirt in a solid color like white, black, or gray. This will give you a sleek, put-together look without sacrificing comfort.

If you want to add a little personality to your look, try a graphic or printed t-shirt. Just be sure to keep the rest of your outfit simple so that the shirt remains the focal point.

Hawaiian Shirts

Hawaiian shirts are a fun and playful option for pairing with shorts. They come in a variety of prints and colors, so you're sure to find one that suits your style. When it comes to styling a Hawaiian shirt with shorts, keep the rest of your outfit simple. Opt for a pair of solid-colored shorts in a neutral color like khaki or navy.

Camp Shirts

Camp shirts are a great option for pairing with shorts if you're looking for something a little more elevated than a t-shirt or polo shirt. They're typically made from lightweight, breathable fabrics like linen or cotton, making them perfect for summer. To style a camp shirt with shorts, opt for a pair of chino shorts in a neutral color like khaki or navy.

Tips for Pairing Shirts with Shorts

Now that you have a better idea of the best shirts to wear with shorts, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Stick to lightweight, breathable fabrics like cotton and linen.
  • Avoid wearing shirts that are too baggy or too tight. Opt for a well-fitted shirt instead.
  • When in doubt, stick to solid colors or subtle patterns.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles to create a unique look.

How to Dress Up Your Shorts and Shirt Look

If you want to dress up your shorts and shirt look, there are a few things you can do. First, swap out your shorts for a pair of chinos or dress shorts. Then, opt for a well-fitted, button-down shirt in a solid color or subtle pattern. Finally, add a pair of dress shoes or loafers to complete the look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you wear a long-sleeve shirt with shorts?

A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to roll up the sleeves to keep cool.

Q: What shoes should I wear with my shorts and shirt?

A: For a casual look, opt for sneakers or sandals. For a dressier look, go for loafers or dress shoes.

Q: Can I wear a tie with my shorts and shirt?

A: It's not recommended, as ties are typically reserved for more formal occasions.

Q: Can I wear a blazer with my shorts and shirt?

A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to opt for a lightweight, breathable blazer and pair it with dress shorts.

Conclusion of Best Shirts to Wear with Shorts

When it comes to finding the perfect shirt to wear with shorts, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic short-sleeve button-down or a playful Hawaiian shirt, there's a shirt out there for you. Just remember to stick to lightweight, breathable fabrics and opt for well-fitted shirts to keep your look stylish and comfortable.
