The Perfect Guide: What Colour Tights to Wear With Navy Dress and Black Shoes

It's a common dilemma for many women - what colour tights to wear with a navy dress and black shoes? It can be a tricky decision to make, especially if you want to look stylish and put-together. Nobody wants to be the fashion disaster at the party, right?

So, what's the answer to this age-old question?

The best colour tights to wear with a navy dress and black shoes are black or sheer nude. These colours will complement your outfit and make you look chic and elegant. However, there are a few more things to consider when making this decision.

In this article, we will dive deeper into the topic of what colour tights to wear with navy dress and black shoes. We'll cover everything you need to know to make the right choice and look fabulous.

The Classic Combination: Black Tights

When in doubt, black tights are always a good choice. They are versatile and can work with any outfit. If you're wearing a navy dress and black shoes, black tights will complement the look perfectly. They will also elongate your legs and make you look taller.

Personally, I love pairing my navy dress with black tights and black shoes. It's a classic combination that never goes out of style. You can add some statement jewelry or a scarf to make the outfit more interesting.

The Elegant Choice: Sheer Nude Tights

If you want to look elegant and sophisticated, sheer nude tights are a great option. They will blend in with your skin tone and create a seamless look. This is especially important if you're wearing open-toe shoes or sandals.

I remember attending a wedding where the dress code was navy and black. I decided to wear a navy dress and black heels with sheer nude tights. I received so many compliments on my outfit, and I felt confident and beautiful.

Matching Your Tights to Your Dress

Another option to consider is matching your tights to your dress. This can create a monochromatic look that is both stylish and trendy. However, it's important to choose the right shade of navy to avoid looking too matchy-matchy.

If you decide to go this route, I recommend choosing a darker shade of navy for your dress and a lighter shade for your tights. This will create a subtle contrast and add some dimension to your outfit.

Experimenting with Coloured Tights

If you're feeling adventurous, you can experiment with coloured tights. However, this can be a tricky look to pull off. The key is to choose a colour that complements your navy dress and black shoes without overpowering them.

For example, you could try burgundy, forest green, or mustard yellow tights with a navy dress and black shoes. These colours are bold but still chic and sophisticated. Just make sure to keep the rest of your outfit simple to avoid looking too busy.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear patterned tights with a navy dress and black shoes?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's important to choose the right pattern. Small, subtle patterns like polka dots or stripes can work well. Avoid bold or busy patterns that can clash with your outfit.

Q: Are white tights a good choice for a navy dress and black shoes?

A: No, white tights are not a good choice for this outfit. They will create a stark contrast that can look unflattering. Stick to black or sheer nude tights instead.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with tights and a navy dress?

A: Yes, you can. Ankle boots can look great with tights and a navy dress, especially if you're going for a more casual look. Just make sure to choose the right style of boots to complement your outfit.

Q: Can I wear fishnet tights with a navy dress and black shoes?

A: It depends on the occasion and your personal style. Fishnet tights can add some edge and texture to your outfit. However, they may not be appropriate for formal events or conservative workplaces.

Conclusion of What Colour Tights to Wear With Navy Dress and Black Shoes

Choosing the right colour tights to wear with a navy dress and black shoes can be a daunting task. However, by following these tips and experimenting with different options, you can find the perfect pair of tights to complement your outfit and make you feel confident and stylish.

Remember, black and sheer nude tights are always a safe choice, but don't be afraid to try something new and daring. Happy styling!
