Mens Suits Price In Kenya A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to purchase a new suit but unsure about the price range in Kenya? Well, you are not alone. Many men struggle with finding the perfect suit at a reasonable price, especially in Kenya. In this post, we will cover everything you need to know about mens suits price in Kenya, including where to buy, how to choose the right suit, and more.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to mens suits price in Kenya is the lack of affordable options. Many men struggle to find a suit that fits their budget without compromising on quality. Additionally, there is a lack of information available about where to find the best deals, leaving many feeling lost and overwhelmed.

So, what is the average price for a mens suit in Kenya? The answer is not straightforward as it depends on various factors such as the brand, fabric, and style. Generally, a good quality mens suit in Kenya can cost anywhere from Ksh 10,000 to Ksh 50,000 or more. However, you can find cheaper options if you are willing to compromise on quality or go for a ready-made suit.

In summary, when it comes to mens suits price in Kenya, there is a range of options available for different budgets, styles, and occasions. It is important to do your research and shop around to find the best deal.

Where to Buy Mens Suits in Kenya

When it comes to buying a mens suit in Kenya, there are various options available both online and offline. Here are some of the popular places to buy mens suits in Kenya:

Tailors: Many men prefer to have their suits custom-made by a tailor. This option is ideal if you want a suit that fits perfectly and is tailored to your style and preferences. However, this option can be more expensive than buying a ready-made suit.

Retail stores: There are various retail stores in Kenya that sell mens suits from different brands, styles, and price ranges. You can find popular brands such as Zara, H&M, and Mr. Price at these stores. However, the prices may be higher compared to other options.

Online stores: You can also buy mens suits online from various e-commerce platforms such as Jumia, Kilimall, and Masoko. This option offers convenience and a range of options at different price points. However, you need to be careful when buying online to ensure that you get what you paid for.

Overall, where you buy your mens suit in Kenya depends on your preferences, budget, and style.

Factors that Affect Mens Suits Price in Kenya

As mentioned earlier, various factors can influence the price of a mens suit in Kenya. Here are some of the most significant factors:

Fabric: The type of fabric used in making a suit can affect its price. High-quality fabrics such as wool, cashmere, and silk are more expensive than synthetic fabrics.

Brand: The brand of a suit also influences its price. Popular designer brands such as Armani, Hugo Boss, and Gucci are generally more expensive than local or lesser-known brands.

Style: The style of a suit can also affect its price. For example, a slim-fit suit may be more expensive than a regular-fit suit.

Customization: If you decide to have your suit custom-made, this can also affect the price. Custom-made suits are generally more expensive than ready-made suits.

How to Choose the Right Mens Suit

Choosing the right mens suit can be a daunting task, especially if you are not familiar with the various styles, fabrics, and brands available. Here are some tips to help you choose the right suit:

Consider the occasion: The occasion will determine the type of suit you need. For example, a formal event may require a tuxedo or black suit, while a business meeting may require a navy or grey suit.

Choose the right fabric: The fabric will affect the comfort, durability, and appearance of your suit. Choose a fabric that suits the occasion and climate.

Get the right fit: The fit is crucial when it comes to choosing a suit. A poorly-fitting suit can ruin your overall appearance. Ensure that you get the right measurements, whether you choose a ready-made or custom-made suit.

How to Care for Your Mens Suit

Caring for your mens suit is essential to ensure that it lasts longer and maintains its quality. Here are some tips to help you care for your suit:

Dry clean: Dry clean your suit regularly to remove dirt, stains, and odors. Avoid washing it in a washing machine or hand washing it.

Hang it properly: Hang your suit on a hanger to prevent wrinkles and creases. Avoid folding it as this can damage the fabric and shape.

Store it properly: Store your suit in a garment bag or closet to protect it from dust, sunlight, and moisture.

Conclusion of Mens Suits Price in Kenya

In conclusion, mens suits price in Kenya can vary depending on various factors such as fabric, brand, and style. It is essential to do your research and shop around to find the best deal that suits your budget, occasion, and style. Whether you choose to buy a ready-made or custom-made suit, ensure that you get the right fit and care for it properly to maintain its quality.

Question and Answer

Q: Where can I find affordable mens suits in Kenya?

A: You can find affordable mens suits in retail stores such as Mr. Price, Woolworths, and Tuskys. You can also check online stores such as Jumia and Kilimall for deals.

Q: How much does a custom-made mens suit cost in Kenya?

A: The cost of a custom-made mens suit in Kenya can vary depending on the tailor and fabric used. Generally, a custom-made suit can cost anywhere from Ksh 20,000 to Ksh 100,000 or more.

Q: What is the difference between a slim-fit and regular-fit suit?

A: A slim-fit suit is a modern style that fits closer to the body, while a regular-fit suit is a classic style that is looser.

Q: How often should I dry clean my mens suit?

A: It is recommended to dry clean your mens suit every three to six months, depending on how often you wear it.
