Discover The Oldest Streetwear Brands That Have Stood The Test Of Time

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Streetwear has become a cultural phenomenon over the years, with its roots tracing back to the skater and hip-hop subcultures of the '90s. As the popularity of this fashion style continues to soar, many people are curious about the oldest streetwear brands that paved the way for the current trend. In this article, we will explore the history of streetwear and the iconic labels that helped shape it.

Many people who are interested in streetwear may find it challenging to identify the oldest brands. It can be overwhelming to navigate through the vast sea of clothing labels and find the ones that have been around for decades. However, knowing the oldest streetwear brands is essential for anyone who wants to understand the roots of this fashion trend and make informed purchasing decisions.

So, what are the oldest streetwear brands, and why are they significant? In this article, we will answer these questions and provide an in-depth look at some of the most iconic streetwear labels.

Supreme

Supreme is one of the most famous streetwear brands and is known for its bold designs and collaborations with high-end fashion houses. Founded in 1994 in New York City, Supreme has become a cultural icon and is often associated with skater culture. The brand's iconic box logo is instantly recognizable and has become a symbol of the streetwear movement.

My personal experience with Supreme is that I was always intrigued by the brand's unique designs, and I saved up for months to buy my first Supreme hoodie. The quality and attention to detail of the product were impressive, and I felt like I was part of a community of people who appreciated the brand's aesthetics and values.

Bape

Bape, short for "A Bathing Ape," is a Japanese streetwear brand that was founded in 1993. The label is known for its bold and colorful designs, and its signature camouflage pattern has become synonymous with the brand. Bape is often associated with hip-hop culture and has collaborated with several musicians over the years.

When I first discovered Bape, I was drawn to the brand's unique designs and eye-catching color schemes. I was impressed by the quality of the products, and I felt like I was wearing something special and unique.

Stussy

Stussy is another iconic streetwear brand that was founded in the '80s in California. The label is known for its surf-inspired designs and has been a significant influence on the streetwear movement. Stussy has collaborated with several brands and artists over the years, cementing its place in the fashion industry.

Stussy has always been a brand that I admired for its laid-back and effortless style. The brand's designs are timeless and appeal to people of all ages, making it a staple in the streetwear community.

How to Identify Authentic Oldest Streetwear Brands

As streetwear continues to gain popularity, many people are trying to cash in on the trend by selling counterfeit products. It's essential to know how to identify authentic oldest streetwear brands to avoid falling victim to scams. Here are some tips:

1. Check the label: Authentic streetwear brands will have a label that is sewn or printed onto the garment. The label should be clear and legible, and the stitching should be even.

2. Look for quality materials: Streetwear brands are known for using high-quality materials, so if the product feels cheap or flimsy, it's likely a fake.

3. Research the seller: If you're buying from a reseller, make sure to research their reputation and read reviews before making a purchase.

4. Price: If the price seems too good to be true, it probably is. Authentic streetwear brands are expensive, so if you're getting a deal that seems too good to be true, it's likely a fake.

Conclusion of Oldest Streetwear Brands

Knowing the oldest streetwear brands is essential for anyone who wants to understand the roots of this fashion trend. Supreme, Bape, and Stussy are just a few of the iconic labels that have shaped the streetwear movement, and their influence is still felt today. When shopping for streetwear, it's important to know how to identify authentic products to avoid falling victim to scams.

A: Streetwear is a fashion trend that originated in the '90s and is characterized by casual, comfortable clothing that is often associated with skate and hip-hop cultures.

Q: What are the oldest streetwear brands?

A: Some of the oldest streetwear brands include Supreme, Bape, and Stussy.

Q: What makes streetwear brands unique?

A: Streetwear brands are known for their bold designs, collaborations with high-end fashion houses, and use of high-quality materials.
